## Your Digital Identity Analyzed Locally

Discover how LocalAI Analyzer processes your browsing history entirely offline,
        produces transparent interest profiles, and offers fully editable insights All without transmitting any data to external servers.

## Interface Showcase

### LocalAI Analyzer

### Browser Extension

LocalAI Analyzer is a browser extension designed to run entirely on your local device , providing comprehensive analysis of your browsing history without relying on any external services .
By processing browsing activity directly on the device, the extension constructs a transparent and personalized interest profile while supplying the data foundation for an adaptive recommendation page.
Users can review, edit, and refine every personalized tag generated by the system, ensuring full control over their individual preference signals . No user data is ever transmitted to remote servers ;
              all computation and storage occur exclusively on the user’s own device .

### Control Panel

The central starting point for backend operations

### Main Control

This popup acts as the core interface of LocalAI Analyzer. Users can start the
              browsing-history export process, trigger backend analysis, navigate to results, or
              adjust system settings. All interactions occur locally, ensuring maximum privacy with
              no external server communication.

### Interactive Interest Label

Your personalized interest labels

### Personalized Label

This panel visualizes your automatically generated topic categories derived from
            browsing history embeddings. Edit, remove, or add new tags to gain full control over
            your personalization signals.

### Personalized Recommendation Page

Daily insights based on your local interest model

### Recommendation Page

A custom new tab experience that aggregates RSS articles matched to your interest profile.You can access
            article recommendations tailored to your interests here.
            All ranking is computed locally using semantic similarity — no cloud services involved.

### Analysis Settings

Control the data used for model analysis

### Analysis Settings

Adjust the number of browsing days included in the analysis, specify websites to exclude, and fine-tune the
            level of detail for generated labels. You can also choose whether to enable deep analysis.

### Recommendation Settings

Manage Personalized Recommendation Content

### Recommendation Settings

Users can add custom RSS sources, control the time window for recommended articles, adjust the number of
            recommendations, and schedule automatic updates.

## LocalAI Analyzer: Generate your personal profile securely

## Local Model

### Data is handled exclusively by on-device models, preserving the security and privacy of
        personal information

The open-source model sentence-transformers — all-mpnet-base-v2 is used for local analysis.
        Despite its compact size, it offers high accuracy and supports long-text processing.
        When deployed as a local backend, it runs efficiently while ensuring that all data processing stays strictly on
        the device, with no privacy leakage.

## IAB Taxonomy

### Employs a structured labeling system consistent with established industry taxonomies

Built to match industry-standard taxonomies, the system outputs multi-tier labels (Tier 1–3) and gives users
        full control to edit label definitions and weighting, enabling a personalized and adaptable classification
        structure.

## Customizable analytical workflow

### Apply a custom analysis process to the browsing history exported from your browser

The system exports a standardized JSON file of your browsing history and cleans and restructures its internal
        data. It then filters and preprocesses the content according to your personalized settings before passing it
        into the model for analysis.
        Users can customize blacklist rules, label granularity, and weighting strategies to generate interest labels
        that better align with their own preferences.
