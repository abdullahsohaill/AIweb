## Ways to Get Sports Without Cable

There are two main ways to watch live sport without cable. You can either stream sports online or use an over-the-air (OTA) antenna to access local channels.

### Streaming Sports Online

If you choose to go with the option to stream sports online, you’ll be hunting for the following types of channels:
- Local Channels
- Regional Sports Networks (RSNs)
- National Channels
The main local channels that broadcast major sports events are NBC, CBS, ABC, and FOX. Such channels have local affiliates, meaning each locality has a different version of the local channel with slightly varying programs.
When it comes to sports, local channels may show different games, based on where you tune in. Local channels like NBC have separate sports channels. For instance, NBC Sports is a set of regional networks (see below), while CBS Sports Network is an entirely separate national TV channel.
- ABC : At one time, ABC was the iconic sports channel (“The thrill of victory! The agony of defeat!”). But in 2006, ABC Sports became ESPN on ABC, providing local channel viewers access to part of the cable channel’s coverage, including NFL, NBA on ABC, NHL (including the Stanley Cup playoffs and finals ), international soccer, college football, and more.
- NBC : Sports programs are shown on NBC during weekend afternoons. The majority of sports is broadcast by NBC Sports’ regional channels, though you can watch sports like golf , NASCAR, and the summer and winter Olympic Games via antenna. Note that OTA sister channel Telemundo also airs sports.
- CBS : During football season CBS normally airs two games every Sunday. They also air basketball . March Madness is broadcast by CBS, as well as the occasional college basketball bout. CBS Sports, the network’s national sports channel covers far more live sports.
- Fox : Aside from the better-known FOX Sports channels (FS1, FS2 ), Fox’s local channels broadcast sports like soccer, volleyball, bowling, and exclusive shows like the NASCAR Race Hub.
Note that the big four local networks limit their sports coverage primarily to
What’s important is that you can stream these network affiliates and other local channels online to watch sports. Services like DIRECTV, Hulu + Live TV, and Fubo offer all four, and more.
Like local channels, RSNs also present live content to specific localities. For example, FanDuel Sports has over a dozen separate channels. One such, FanDuel Sports Florida, serves the regions of Florida, southern Alabama, and southern Georgia. The other FanDuel Sports channels work this same way and serve one or more local regions.
You can stream select RSNs with the large streaming providers online, without cable. DIRECTV and Fubo are the best streaming service for RSNs — providing access to over 35 location-specific channels with many additional subchannels. (DIRECTV is only missing NBC Sports Philadelphia and Fubo is missing the two Spectrum SportsNet channels in Los Angeles.)
These include some of your favorite sports channels like FS1 and ESPN. National sports channels are available nationwide and are the easiest to stream online.
A subset of these are league-specific channels. For example, baseball fans can watch live games and exclusive content on the MLB Network . There are many more league-specific channels like SEC Network and NBA TV.
There are also out-of-market packages like NFL Sunday Ticket and MLB.TV , which you can get as add-ons from live TV streaming services.

## Regional Sports Networks

Regional sports networks (RSNs) allow you to watch live and pre-recorded sports on TV in particular areas around the US.
There are dozens of RSNs — some of them are operated by major network providers like NBC, while others are run by telecommunications giant, AT&T. FanDuel Sports is the largest RSN provider having taken over Fox Sports’ channels.

## Other Ways to Watch Sports Without Cable

There are other ways to watch sports without cable. We’ve touched on some of these above.
- ESPN+ : Although not a replacement for the regular ESPN channels, ESPN+ provides access to a lot of games across the spectrum of sports.
- ESPN3 : Only Sling TV includes ESPN3 with its channel guide. But you can watch ESPN3 through the ESPN app using your streaming service to log in.
- League Passes: Many sports league provide access to all of their names. These include NBA League Pass , NFL+ , and MLB.TV . The problem with these for most fans is that they only allow you to watch out-market games. So if you want to watch the home team, you’re out of luck.

Amazon Prime shows some live sporting events. Most notably, it is the home of Thursday Night Football games. You can get more live sports with individual channel subscriptions like MLB.tv, NBA League Pass, Paramount+ , and PGA Tour Live.

### Can I record live sporting events?

All the major streaming services come with a cloud DVR. Sling TV offers 50 hours of cloud DVR. Fubo offers 1,000 hours. Hulu + Live TV and DIRECTV offer unlimited could DVR with up to 9 months of storage.
