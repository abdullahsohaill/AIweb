## Alpaca

## Chat With AI

Enjoy seamless interactions with advanced AI models directly on your device—no data collection, no cloud dependencies, and complete control over your privacy. Ask deep, meaningful questions on complex topics or leverage the power of AI to analyze files like PDFs or even images to get insightful answers.
Getting started is simple and hassle-free. Just install Alpaca, choose and download the AI model that suits your needs, and start exploring the possibilities—all without the need for complex setups or technical expertise.

## Image Recognition

Ask image recognition-capable models insightful questions about any image—all while keeping your data secure and offline. Your images and text stay completely private, ensuring everything remains between you and the model.
But that's not all! Expand your creativity by using various inputs like text files, PDFs, YouTube videos, and even websites to prompt the model. Whether you're analyzing content, generating ideas, or uncovering insights, Alpaca puts the possibilities of AI at your fingertips—securely and privately.

## Custom Models

Create personalized AI models by simply providing instructions and a profile picture. Perfect for crafting immersive roleplay scenarios with your favorite fictional characters or even developing unique AI personas tailored to your needs.
Unleash your creativity and make every interaction feel truly personal. Whether it's for entertainment, storytelling, or brainstorming, Alpaca lets you design models that fit your vision seamlessly.

## Powered by Local AI

Dive into a vast collection of pre-trained models from leading innovators like Meta, Microsoft, Google, Nvidia, and Alibaba, as well as unique independent models—all accessible through a user-friendly interface.
From coding assistants and multilingual communicators to advanced image recognition and specialized expertise, Alpaca gives you the power to experiment with a wide range of capabilities. Even better, you can combine multiple models in a single conversation, ensuring you always have the perfect tool to answer any question or tackle any challenge.

## Code Generation

Most models in Alpaca are designed to generate clean, efficient code across a variety of programming languages. To make your workflow seamless, Alpaca automatically formats the output into easy-to-read code blocks, simplifying the process of copying, reviewing, and implementing the code.
But that's not all—Alpaca takes it a step further by letting you run Python scripts directly within the app! Using a built-in virtual environment, you can execute scripts with full support for user input and library installation, giving you a complete coding experience without leaving Alpaca.

## Live Chat

With Alpaca you can have live conversations with your AI models as if you were in a call with them.
A mode centered in speech recognition and dictation.

## Privacy

Alpaca is built on Ollama , an open-source framework for running AI models, primarily developed by Meta (formerly Facebook). Ollama is committed to privacy and does not collect any data about your conversations or model usage.
The model list used by Alpaca is provided by Ollama, ensuring secure, offline operation. Models have no direct access to the internet, and all information is stored locally on your device.
- Local Data Storage: All conversations are securely saved in SQLite3 files on your device.
- Limited Internet Usage: Alpaca only connects to the internet for specific actions, like extracting text from websites, extracting YouTube captions, or downloading models.
- Offline Capability: Once your models are downloaded, you can use Alpaca entirely offline.
- No Cloud Dependency: Alpaca and Ollama do not rely on cloud computing or account systems for operation.
- Performance Transparency: The quality and speed of responses depend solely on your system specifications and the model in use.
