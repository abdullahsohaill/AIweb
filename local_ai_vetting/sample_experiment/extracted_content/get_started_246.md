## "Protect Our Wildlife: Cody Roberts Must Face Harsher Punishment!"

## The Issue

This petition urges Governor Mark Gordon of Wyoming to take immediate action against hunter Cody Roberts, owner of C Roberts Trucking, who cruelly chased down a wolf on a snowmobile, taped its mouth shut, brought it into a bar, tormented it and then later shot it. Despite this heinous act, the judge only fined him $250.00, which is an inadequate punishment for such egregious animal cruelty. We demand that Cody Roberts face the full extent of the law for his actions.
The brutal treatment of this wolf is a stark reminder of the cruelty that wildlife often faces at the hands of humans. This innocent creature suffered needlessly, and justice must be served to ensure that such acts of cruelty are not tolerated in our society. Let us come together to protect our wildlife and stand up for those who cannot speak for themselves.
The incident involving Cody Roberts and the wolf has gained widespread attention, with witnesses and reports confirming the horrifying treatment the animal endured. This is not an isolated incident but a reflection of a larger issue of wildlife exploitation and abuse that must be addressed with urgency and seriousness.
Governor Mark Gordon, we urge you to take immediate action to further prosecute Cody Roberts and ensure that he faces appropriate consequences for his cruel actions. Every signature on this petition is a voice for the voiceless, a stand against animal cruelty, and a demand for justice. Together, we can make a difference and protect our precious wildlife from such senseless acts of violence.
