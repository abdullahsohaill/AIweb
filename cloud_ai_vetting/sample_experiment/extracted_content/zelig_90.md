⦿  Our Team

## Sandy Sholl

### CEO & Founder, ZELIG

Sandy Sholl is the visionary force behind Zelig, driven by a deep love for fashion and a relentless pursuit of innovation. As a longtime luxury retail expert, she saw how digital shopping had lost its artistry and emotional connection. She founded Zelig to redefine how shoppers discover, engage with, and create fashion in an omnichannel world. With decades of expertise in luxury retail and consumer innovation, Sandy co-founded MadaLuxe Group in 2010, building it into a global leader in luxury distribution. She also launched MadaLuxe Ventures and SLS Journey, investment funds fueling the next generation of tech-driven consumer experiences. Beyond business, Sandy is dedicated to philanthropy, focusing on combating antisemitism and supporting global humanitarian efforts through the Sholl-Freede Foundation. Under her leadership, Zelig is bridging the gap between AI, retail, and human expression, ushering in a new era of immersive and intuitive fashion engagement.

## Adam Freede

### Co-Founder, ZELIG

Adam Freede, a seasoned investor, excels in crafting operational strategies that transform businesses into enduring high-growth companies. His passion for luxury led him to co-found MadaLuxe alongside his business partner, Sandy Sholl. With a steadfast commitment to his team, Adam has fostered a culture of excellence and community, driving growth across the MadaLuxe portfolio.      Under Adam’s leadership, MadaLuxe has emerged as a preeminent luxury company, featuring an exclusive collection of prestigious brands.
A proud alumnus of the Goizueta Business School at Emory University, Adam's influence also extends to his roles as a board member for various organizations, spanning philanthropies, consumer packaged goods, and circular fashion companies. As the Co-Chair and Co-Founder of his family's foundation, The Sholl-Freede Foundation, Adam remains dedicated to philanthropic endeavors. Additionally, he serves as both an owner and board member of the Los Angeles Football Club in Major League Soccer.
