## How To Use Our AI Lyrics Generator Like A Pro

Whether you're crafting a gospel song, drill verse, or a pop chorus, our songwriting inspiration engine is designed to inspire and supercharge your creative process. You can instantly start using our AI Lyrics Generator - there's NO sign up required!

### Step 1: Input Your Topics

Begin by choosing the topics of your song. You can enter up to 3 overarching topics (each up to 100 characters) of the lyrics you wish to create.
Topics inspire the big picture of the song. From “falling in love with your friend" to “packing your bags and moving to a new continent” . There are so many ways to find inspiration by simply observing the world around you. From books, your favorite movies, speeches that inspire you, the news, conversations with a friend, and your past relationships. Find out what inspires YOU, enter the ideas into the lyric generator and note down the ideas that stick.

### Step 2: Select Genre

Next, select the genre of your song from the drop-down which lists all genres currently available on our platform. Surprise yourself by trying a new genre you might not have experimented with yet!
We are constantly innovating, do check us out frequently to see new developments, and please contact us if you have recommendations on the genres you’d like to see!
Heading to the genre specific pages will assist you with more intelligent guidance and suggestions for themes and keywords, as well as some additional functionality, like “Make it a Duet” for some of the available genres such as pop punk and country .

### Step 3: Input Keywords

Next, head over to the Keywords tab to input core keywords or phrases of the song you wish to create (up to 30 characters each). The algorithm will try to fit the keyword inputs throughout the song lyrics.

### Step 4: Describe the Emotional Journey

You’ve reached the final step! Use the emotions tab to describe the emotional journey of the song. You can also think of this as the progression of the mood or vibe of the song. This is the chance to make the lyrics truly unique. You can add up to 3 emotions which describe how the mood of the song will progress throughout the lyrics.
Once you've made your selections, hit the 'Generate' button and watch your stories come to life!
Is your composition a reflective journey through life's complexities? Perhaps consider a journey of Contemplation, Resilience, to Nostalgia.
