## Your everyday AI-powered app

Routine assists you to focus on what matters: tasks, meetings, projects, notes & more.
Loved by 100k+ ambitious professionals and teams worldwide

## Never forget anything

### AI meeting notes

Automatically summarize meetings and automatically turn action items into tasks to ensure nothing falls through the cracks.
Works on all platforms, without any meeting bot!

### Quick capture

Capture your thoughts, URLs & contexts at the speed of light through a single desktop hotkey without switching context and losing focus.

### Universal inbox

Centralize all your tasks, incoming messages & notifications and stop wasting time switching between apps and services.

## Integrations

Connect all the apps & services you use daily and bidirectionally synchronize your data.
Consolidate your calendars, centralize all your work, projects, customers and more.

## Organize and plan

### Planner

Plan your tasks and events of the week through a single screen.

### Time blocking

Block time for your most important tasks to make sure those get done.

### Labels

Organize your tasks, pages, events, people and more through labels/tags to better find them.

### Recurrences

Schedule recurring tasks effortlessly, and never drop the ball anymore.

## Stay in the flow

### Menu bar widget

Instantly glance at your day through Routine's system tray/menu bar widget: tasks and events of the day but also timers.

### Work & personal

Connect and synchronize your professional and personal calendars to have a single view of your work and life.

### Timers

Use timers to stay focused, track where how much time you spend on something and record it in your calendar.

### Reminders

By notified whenever a meeting is about to start and quickly join through a single keyboard shortcut.
