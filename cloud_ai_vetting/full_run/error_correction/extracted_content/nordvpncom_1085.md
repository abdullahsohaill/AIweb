## What is a VPN?

VPN definition : A VPN, or virtual private network, is a tool that protects your online activity from spying eyes. It makes your data safer from cybercriminals by encrypting your internet traffic and sending it through a secure tunnel. Every time you go online, your data is routed through a remote VPN server first. This way, it changes your IP address and virtual location, giving you safer and more private access to the internet.
Frequently Asked Questions
VPNs come in various types, like personal VPNs, remote access VPNs, or VPN proxies. Each type of VPN works a little differently:
A personal VPN connects your device to a VPN server to keep your internet activity private. It uses a VPN protocol to encrypt your connection so no one can see what you're doing online — neither snoopy parents nor your internet provider. This type is perfect for everyday use, like gaming or online shopping.
A site-to-site VPN is a VPN server setup that links two or more networks, for example, multiple company offices in different countries. It uses a secure VPN protocol to create a private tunnel so teams can share files and work like they’re in the same building. It’s great for businesses, but not that useful for personal use.
A remote access VPN lets you connect to your work or home network through a VPN server from anywhere in the world. It uses VPN protocols to encrypt your traffic and keeps your information safe while letting you use the data and tools as if you were there in person. It’s super helpful for remote workers.
A mobile VPN is made for devices that move around a lot. It keeps your connection to the VPN server stable even when switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data. Thanks to a special flexible VPN protocol, it avoids connection drops or interruptions while making your online traffic safer on the go — even on public Wi-Fi.
A VPN proxy , or a browser-based VPN, is an extension you add to your browser. It connects to a VPN server, changes your IP, and encrypts just your web traffic coming from and to your browser. It’s fast and easy to use, but keep in mind that it doesn’t protect apps outside your browser.
