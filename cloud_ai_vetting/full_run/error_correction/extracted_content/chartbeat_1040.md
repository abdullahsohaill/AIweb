# Chartbeat
**URL:** https://chartbeat.com
**Page Title:** Chartbeat
--------------------

Smarter insights. Sharper content.
Intuitive website analytics and optimization tools designed for media.
Trusted by newsrooms and content teams around the world.
OPTIMIZE CONTENT
Get reliable editorial insights at the speed of news.
- Real-Time Dashboard. Track every page on all your sites to understand what’s driving engagement in every moment and where referrer traffic is coming from.
- At-a-glance insights. See concurrent page views, unique visitors, and recirculation–all in real time and instantly visualized in one beautiful dashboard.
- Heads Up Display. Spot top-performing stories, measure scroll depth, view articles’ ranked performance, and see which content is over- or under-performing by placement.
ENHANCE STRATEGIES
Fuel your content strategy with powerful analytics.
- Historical Dashboard. Measure 13 months of historical trends in engagement, traffic, topic-based data, and more. It’s simple to analyze historical performance, empowering you to repeat past successes.
- Advanced reporting tools and intuitive reports. See, customize, schedule, and export your data exactly how you want it. And, get easy-to-read performance visualizations sent right to your inbox.
- Competitive benchmarking. Chartbeat Cohorts lets you compare your site’s performance against other publishers in Chartbeat’s global network.
GROW LOYAL AUDIENCES
Know what drives audience engagement and recirculation.
- Headline and image testing. Adapt what your readers see with a suite of optimization tools and smart recommendation widgets powered by reader-preference data.
- Conversions. Understand what drives your audience to register, subscribe, donate, make a payment, and more, enabling you to optimize for reader journeys.
- Real-time alerts. Get notified of unusual spikes and dips so you can take action quickly. Plus, display your live audience data to the entire newsroom with a dashboard designed for the big screen.
