Use Case
Duration:
2-4 days
The provided sample presents an application crafted by a potential SAP partner or customer, tailored for SAP Business Technology Platform (SAP BTP) using Generative AI Hub and SAP HANA Cloud's Vector Engine. This scenario shows a comprehensive solution for enhancing customer support within a travel agency, utilizing advanced email insights and automation. The system analyzes incoming emails using Large Language Models (LLMs) to offer core insights such as categorization, sentiment analysis and urgency assessment. It goes beyond basic analysis by extracting key facts and customizable fields like location.

One innovative feature uses embeddings to identify similar historical emails, to help understand how similar requests were handled previously. This fosters consistent and efficient customer service. The code also demonstrates the capabilities of summarizing and translating both email subject and body, enabling streamlined comprehension across languages.

Furthermore, the system takes automation to the next level by generating potential responses for customer inquiries. This response generation is influenced by configurable actions and services, enhancing response accuracy and speed. The flexibility to connect with SAP systems like SAP Concur adds an enterprise dimension, allowing seamless integration of processes and data.

Though initially tailored for a travel agency, the code can be adapted to suit various industries, making it a versatile solution for augmenting customer support with data-driven insights and automation.
Current Position - What is the challenge?
Businesses often grapple with customer support issues such as manual email processing, insufficient customer insights, ineffective data analysis, language barriers, slow response times, lack of automation, and adaptability issues. These challenges lead to slower responses, decreased customer satisfaction, and inconsistent service quality. Language barriers restrict global reach while manual tasks lead to delays and errors. Limited automation and industry adaptability hamper growth.
Destination - What is the outcome?
The AI solution provides numerous advantages including faster, personalized, and consistent customer service, and increased employee efficiency. It facilitates cost savings, adaptability, and competitiveness for customers and partners. Seamless integration with systems like SAP Concur optimizes operations, and data-driven decisions aid strategic planning. Overall, it boosts customer satisfaction, streamlines workflows, and enhances enterprise performance.
How You Get There - What is the solution?
Our AI solution automates email handling for a hypothetical travel agency using Large Language Models for categorization, sentiment analysis, and urgency assessment. It leverages historical data through embeddings, ensuring consistent customer service. The system breaks language barriers by summarizing and translating emails. In addition, it transforms customer support operations by generating responses, being adaptable across industries, and allowing integration with systems like SAP Concur.
