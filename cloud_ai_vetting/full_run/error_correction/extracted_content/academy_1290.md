## Prompt Guide

Master camera movements, angles, and cinematic techniques with our comprehensive guide for AI video generation. Covers both Text to Video and Image to Video prompting.

### Dolly

Camera moves forward or backward on a track or wheels
" A dolly backward shot smoothly follows a lone figure walking down a dimly lit alleyway "

### Pan

Camera rotates left or right on a fixed point
" A horizontal pan sweeps left to right across pine trees and over a lake "

### Tracking

Camera follows alongside a moving subject
" A tracking shot follows a skilled astronaut skateboarder on a moon landscape "

### Crane/Jib

Camera moves up and down on a large mechanical arm
" A crane shot moves smoothly downwards, revealing a lone figure at a desk "
