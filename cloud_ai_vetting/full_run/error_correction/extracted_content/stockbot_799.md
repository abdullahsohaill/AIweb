## Welcome to the StockBot powered by Gaia!

Open source AI chatbot that uses function calling to render relevant TradingView stock market widgets. Built with Vercel AI SDK , TradingView Widgets , and powered by Llama3-8b on Gaia
StockBot may provide inaccurate information and does not provide investment advice.
