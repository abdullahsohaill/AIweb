### How Does Hostinger’s Pricing Work?

When you first sign up, you’ll begin with Hostinger’s low-cost, introductory pricing . However, this price rises at renewal. For example, the Premium Website Builder starts at $1.99 per month, but increases to $10.99 per month when it’s time to renew.
You also need to take Hostinger’s billing cycles into account. On most plans, you can pay for a billing period lasting one month, 12 months, 24 months, or 48 months. As a general rule of thumb: the longer the commitment, the cheaper your plan will be per month.
Last of all, different Hostinger plans come with freebies. As an example, the Premium Website Builder plan offers your first three months for free, as well as a free domain and two website mailboxes for one year.

Hostinger is pretty transparent about its pricing, with different subscription lengths, renewal fees, and add-ons all very visible. However, there are a few things to keep in mind if you want to keep costs low:
- Domain names – Most plans include a custom domain for free for the first year, but you’ll need to pay $9.99 per year after that
- Renewal rates – Once your initial term is up, you’ll be charged the regular plan rate by Hostinger (I’ve made a note of these when talking about each plan)
Yes, Hostinger is great value for money, since it consistently offers some of the most competitive prices in the hosting and website builder industry. Whether you’re looking for shared hosting, a website builder, or a high-powered VPS plan, Hostinger’s options won’t break the bank.
Of course, every website is unique, and your specific needs will vary. A high-traffic, resource-hungry site might need a higher-tier plan, or dedicated hosting from the likes of Bluehost or HostGator (Hostinger doesn’t have dedicated hosting plans). That said, for most small businesses, Hostinger’s plans and variety of hosting types will provide plenty of flexibility and scalability.
Overall, the Hostinger pricing plans are affordable and ideal for small businesses looking to get and grow online. Yes, Hostinger lacks some advanced options (such as dedicated hosting plans), but Hostinger is much cheaper than competitors, and it’s very easy to use.
Figure out the features and power your site needs to help you determine which plan is the right fit for your site. Or, start completely from scratch and build your website with Hostinger directly.
