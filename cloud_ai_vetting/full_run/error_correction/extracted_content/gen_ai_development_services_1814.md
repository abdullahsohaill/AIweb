## Our Generative AI Development Process

We'll guide you through every stage—from initial business analysis to successful deployment and ongoing optimization—making sure you get the most out of AI's amazing potential.

### Problem Definition and Business Analysis

At Upsilon, we start with defining the problem the product will solve and defining goals and objectives. We allocate a team of A and product experts early on to identify the business needs, conduct market research, and analyze existing data.

### Data Collection and Preparation

We'll help you identify and gather the right data to power your AI model. Don't have the data you need? Our team can collect it through advanced scraping techniques and make sure it is clear, properly labeled, correctly formatted, and error-free.

### AI Model Selection

We evaluate and select the optimal AI model for your project to match your specific needs. Going beyond model selection, we carefully consider various parameters like token limits, RPM, RPD, and others to ensure your AI solution runs efficiently within your budget.

### Setting Up the Environment

Our team expertly sets up the development environment, handles API key creation and management, and takes care of all necessary library installations. Whether we're working with OpenAI in a Node.js environment or integrating other AI models, we leverage the most appropriate technologies to bring your AI vision to life.

### Model Training and Fine-Tuning

Then we run the model through a sequence of steps needed to transform input data into outputs. During this step, we continuously fine-tune the model by adjusting the parameters, updating the prompt, and adding regularizations to make sure the model delivers the desired accuracy.

Once the AI model has been tested and debugged, we deploy it seamlessly. Additionally, we keep a close eye on how it's performing and how people are using it, making improvements based on new data, feedback, and evolving business needs.
