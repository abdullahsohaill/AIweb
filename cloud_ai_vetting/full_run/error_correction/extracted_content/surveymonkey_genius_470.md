## Discover our AI features

### Build with AI

Type a prompt that describes your survey needs, and let our AI generate a high-quality survey tailored to your goals, in under a minute. Available on all plans.

### AI-powered survey import

Copy & paste a survey you’ve written somewhere else, and our AI will intelligently transform it into a structured survey with the right question types and formatting. Available on all plans.

### Survey theme generation

Create polished, on-brand surveys in seconds. Our AI analyzes your image, logo, or colors, to generate a custom survey theme. Available on Advantage, Premier, Team, and Enterprise plans.

### Question type prediction

Start typing a survey question, and our AI will automatically predict the best question type to use, whether it’s multiple choice, checkboxes, or ranking. Available on all plans.

### Answer Genius recommendations

Get expert-recommended answer choices that are auto-generated for any question. Add them to your questions with a single click. Available on all plans.

### Survey tips

Send surveys with confidence. Our AI detects issues with survey structure or question formats and recommends changes in real time. Available on all plans.

### Analyze with AI

Ask our chat-based tool questions about your survey results, and receive insights, charts, and summaries instantly. Available on Advantage, Premier, Team, and Enterprise plans.

### Thematic analysis (beta)

Quickly identify key themes from your open-ended text responses in seconds. Available on Advantage, Premier, Team, and Enterprise plans.

### Response quality detection

Filter out survey results that don't meet quality standards, including rushed responses or gibberish. Available on Advantage, Premier, Team, and Enterprise plans.

### Sentiment analysis

AI categorizes open-ended answers by sentiment in seconds—positive, neutral, or negative. Available on Advantage, Premier, Team, and Enterprise plans.

### AI-powered insights

Uncover meaningful patterns and statistically significant trends with ease. Available with our market research solutions on all plans.
