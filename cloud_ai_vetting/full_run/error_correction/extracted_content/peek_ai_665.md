## Usage

Unzip the download file and move Peek to the application folder. You can launch the app from your Applications folder on MacOS. To switch between different chatbots, click on the dropdown menu at the top of the app and select the chatbot you want to use. To toggle the window, press ⌘+J. To take a screenshot of your chat, press ⌘+Shift+S . The screenshot will be saved to your local machine. To copy a screenshot to your clipboard, press ⌘+S .
