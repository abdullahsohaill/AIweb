## Securely architected and elegantly engineered

Keeper employs a zero-trust and zero-knowledge architecture to enforce robust end-to-end encryption across all users, devices and sessions, safeguarding your data at every touchpoint.

### Zero-trust architecture

Keeper isn't just committed to security, we are fanatical about it. Your data is encrypted and decrypted at the device and record levels - Keeper can never access it.

### Critical certifications

Keeper is FedRAMP High and GovRAMP Authorised; FIPS 140-3 validated; ISO 27001, 27017 and 27018 certified; PCI DSS certified and certified by TrustArc for privacy - among other certifications.

### Third-party validated

Our platform is continuously validated by third-party security experts through penetration testing, bug bounties and a public vulnerability disclosure program.
