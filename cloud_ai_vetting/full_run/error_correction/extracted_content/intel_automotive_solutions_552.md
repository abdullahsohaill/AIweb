### Intel Adaptive Control Unit

As a key component in the future of electric vehicle (EV) power train control, Intel's ACU U310 is a single-chip solution that enables X-in-1 power train domain control, consolidating multiple critical functions onto a single device for unprecedented cost savings, energy efficiency and performance.
ACU U310 leverages a unique architecture, combining multi-core CPUs with adaptable hardware and enabling concurrent real-time control of critical power train functions without compromising performance or safety. This innovative approach unlocks new levels of efficiency and simplifies development for automakers.
The same properties that enable an ACU to deliver superior energy efficiency in an EV power train can also be used to deliver a first-of-its-kind software-defined zonal controller solution. This comprehensive approach empowers automakers to build more efficient, cost-effective and feature-rich EVs.
- Intel ACU X-in-1 Fact Sheet : Transforming electric vehicle architectures with unmatched integration and efficiency.

### Intel Arc Graphics for Automotive

Our automotive discrete graphics processing unit (dGPU) addresses the growing demand for compute performance in increasingly sophisticated vehicle cockpits. By adding the Intel® Arc™ graphics for Automotive to our existing portfolio of AI-enhanced SDV system-on-chips, we offer automakers an open, flexible and scalable platform solution that brings next level, high fidelity experiences to the vehicle.
Automakers can now design a single vehicle platform that scales across trim levels. Entry and mid-level models can leverage the Intel SDV SoC, while higher-end vehicles can benefit from the added horsepower of the dGPU for even more premium features. This approach streamlines development as software is fully compatible across the integrated GPU and the discrete GPU.
- dGPU Fact Sheet : The new discrete GPU for the automotive industry is set to elevate in-vehicle AI capabilities in 2025.
