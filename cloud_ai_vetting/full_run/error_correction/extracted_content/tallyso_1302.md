### Privacy-friendly form builder

Your data privacy and security are our top priorities. We are GDPR compliant and treat your data with care and confidentiality. Tally is hosted in Europe , we don’t use cookie-tracking, and all form data is securely stored, and encrypted both in transit and at rest. Learn more about Tally & GDPR .
