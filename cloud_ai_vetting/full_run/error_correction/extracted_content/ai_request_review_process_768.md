## Home

The University Data Office works to ensure that all administrative data is organized, accessible, and useful. We help the university leverage data to make informed decisions and operate more efficiently.
Our scope includes all administrative data, such as people, space, and financial data, but excludes any clinical or research data.
We are committed to advancing Yale University’s scholarly mission and operational excellence through data-driven insights and solutions. We are developing a comprehensive data strategy that addresses high-level priorities across data solutions, architecture, governance, culture, and skills. This strategy targets leadership and outlines our multi-year goals.
Our office’s strategy encompasses four main focuses:
- Data Solutions: Our team is committed to providing innovative data solutions, leveraging prioritization and collaboration frameworks to create new data prototypes.
