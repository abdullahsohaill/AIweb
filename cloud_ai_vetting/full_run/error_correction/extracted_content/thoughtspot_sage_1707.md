## Self-service analytics the way it was meant to be

Asking a business question sounds simple.
But there’s a lot of complexity involved in getting a trusted,
accurate, and relevant answer from your data using GenAI. For
self-serve analytics that actually drive business outcomes, your AI
solution must:

### Handle real-world data that is messy, massive, and
complex

The data you want to analyze will never be perfectly
clean. Real-world datasets may cover multiple use cases,
requiring extensive joins across hundreds of tables and
thousands of columns to unlock insights.

### Interpret your natural language correctly

Translating complex business questions into data queries
is hard because of natural language nuances, including
spelling mistakes and typos, ambiguous references, and
incompleteness.

### Handle complex analytical queries

Business questions are inherently complex, often
requiring deep, multi-layered analyses beyond simple
queries. These analyses need to be performed on the fly,
ranging from filters and comparisons, to formulas and
query within other queries.

### Understand the context of your industry

Every industry and business uses unique terminology. The
same phrase can hold different meanings depending on
your context. Certain questions cannot be answered
reliably without additional business context or direct
guidance from users with industry expertise.
