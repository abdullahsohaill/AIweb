## Meet Maia Your Virtual Data Engineering Team

Data engineering teams are overwhelmed, facing a relentless stream of data requests, pipeline issues, and quality concerns. Maia changes that.
Meet Maia, your Agentic Data Team
Maia autonomously builds, maintains, and optimizes pipelines across your data environment, reducing manual work and helping your team deliver trusted data faster.
Join a live 30-minute session and see how Maia:
- Eliminates data delivery bottlenecks
- Multiplies your team’s output without hiring
- Activates a virtual data engineering team in your existing environment

### Our live product demos include:

- Connecting any data source to Snowflake or your cloud data warehouse
- Creating no-code batch and change data capture pipelines
- Orchestrating complex business logic via visual patterns in a low-code tool
- Speeding up development with a live sample of your data at design-time
- Taking advantage of Snowflake's native features with modern ELT and how that is faster than traditional ETL patterns
