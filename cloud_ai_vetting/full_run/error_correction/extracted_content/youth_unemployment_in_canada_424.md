Youth Unemployment Rate in Canada increased to 13.30 percent in December from 12.80 percent in November of 2025. Youth Unemployment Rate in Canada averaged 13.79 percent from 1976 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 30.30 percent in May of 2020 and a record low of 9.00 percent in July of 2022. source: Statistics Canada
