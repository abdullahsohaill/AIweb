What is Bible Chat?
Bible Chat is the world’s most popular faith app, helping Christians find peace and support every day through God’s Word.
Is the Bible Chat App free?
Bible Chat is free to download for everyone. In fact, over 95% of our users enjoy the app at zero cost. The Free Plan they enjoy includes multiple audio Bible translations, countless study plans, and our innovative Biblical chatbot, exclusively trained on the Word of God. All of that free usage actually generates infrastructure costs for our company, costs that we fully cover ourselves, in line with our Mission of Spreading the Word. For those who wish to support our Mission (e.g. currently 5% of our users), we offer a Premium Plan with a 7-day free trial. All proceeds from the Premium Plan go entirely towards covering our infrastructure and marketing costs, and also towards supporting our NGO - OpenSoul Foundation , which is in line with our Compassionate Capitalism framework.
[LINK: Compassionate Capitalism](https://www.thebiblechat.com/our-mission/compassionate-capitalism)
How much does Bible Chat's Premium Plan cost?
If you want to support our mission and you choose the Premium Plan, you will receive a 7-day free trial introductory offer. At the end of the 7 days, you can choose from weekly access at $4.99 per week, monthly access at $12.99 per month, or yearly access at $59.99 for the Premium Plan. All proceeds go towards OpenSoul Foundation (e.g. our NGO) and towards our operational and marketing costs, in line with our Compassionate Capitalism framework.
[LINK: Compassionate Capitalism](https://www.thebiblechat.com/our-mission/compassionate-capitalism)
Can I invite friends to join the Bible Chat app?
Sure! Go to your app’s dashboard, access “Profile” from the menu, and click on the “Share with friends” button.
How can I contact customer support?
If you have any questions or need assistance, our customer support team is here to help. Please contact us through our contact form.
Who created Bible Chat?
Bible Chat was created by a group of Christians who believe in the teachings of the Bible. We are software engineers, entrepreneurs, and marketers bound by our common belief that modern technology can enrich our shared faith. Our mission is to make Biblical learning accessible to everyone by providing a platform where people can explore the Word of God more easily. Learn more about the team behind Bible Chat from our About Us page.
What makes Bible Chat different from other Bible study tools?
You can ask questions relating to any topic and receive Biblical answers. The answers are constructed exclusively from Biblical verses, verses which are referenced within the answer. It’s the best Biblical study tool. The Bible Chat App is also special thanks to its user-friendly design and engaging features. It was designed to be intuitive, easy to use, and to incorporate interactive elements that make studying the Bible more engaging and enjoyable.
Does the Bible talk about AI?
The Bible does not directly mention AI (artificial intelligence) as it is a modern innovation. However, the Bible discusses themes like creation, wisdom, life, ethics, and responsibility, which can guide how we build and use AI when studying the Bible. We’ve applied Biblical principles when we’ve built BibleChat and made sure all the answers are theologically sound. We understand the skepticism some Christians rightfully have when considering technology and faith. We believe technology, when used correctly, can be a tool for helping people in their daily walk with God. We only use technology for the benefit of our users and as a tool to enable them to draw closer to God.
Is Bible Chat Catholic?
We welcome all Catholics to use Bible Chat. The app was created to help everyone explore the Bible, regardless of their Christian denomination. You can select your preferred denomination and Bible translation when setting up the app, immediately after download. Your experience with Bible Chat will adapt according to your Bible translation.
What is the app that answers questions about the Bible?
You are in the right place! Our app does exactly what you are looking for and much more, providing the best experience for learning and exploring the Bible.
Is there an AI app for the Bible?
Yes, it is! Our user-friendly application allows you to talk with the Bible and explore God’s lessons. Get it now and start asking your questions regarding faith, spirituality, life, or anything that comes to your mind.
How do I know the answers I get from Bible Chat come from the Bible?
Simply try BibleChat and see for yourself. Ask difficult questions. We care about the theological soundness of Biblechat. Our AI is constrained to respond in the context of the Bible exclusively. We are working with pastors, priests, and Biblical scholars to constantly refine and perfect our Biblical chatbot. We encourage you to test BibleChat, especially since it’s a free tool.
Is Bible Chat a non-profit?
BibleChat isn’t a nonprofit in the legal sense. We’re a mission-driven company operating under our Compassionate Capitalism framework. Our purpose isn’t to generate dividends for investors, employees, or founders—and we don’t expect to pay dividends. Instead, we reinvest all revenue into the mission: funding the technology that powers the app, supporting growth and marketing, and financing OpenSoul Foundation, our global NGO focused on mental health and education. While we offer equity-based compensation, we don’t distribute profits to shareholders or employees. The team isn’t motivated by annual profit payouts; everyone is aligned around reinvesting in impact. This approach carries real risk for both employees and shareholders—if the company fails, they may not reach their personal financial goals. But it enables faster, sustained mission growth and helps us attract world-class talent committed to faith-tech impact. Long term, our goal is to become a public company. If and when that happens, our team can receive fair financial upside, while we continue to follow Compassionate Capitalism principles. We believe a mission-first company with no profit target, paired with meaningful charitable support, is the most scalable way to reach Christians in need—more effectively than operating solely as an NGO. We’re committed to being transparent about how we balance mission focus with the financial incentives required to attract investment and top talent needed to scale BibleChat.
What is a Bible Verse?
A Bible verse is a short sentence or part of a sentence from the Bible. The Bible is a holy book for Christians, and it is divided into books, chapters, and verses. Each verse helps people find and read specific parts of the Bible easily. For example:  John 3:16 - “God loved the world so much that He gave His only Son, so that anyone who believes in Him will not die but have eternal life.” In this example: • John is the name of the book, • 3 is the chapter, • 16 is the verse. People read Bible verses to learn about God, feel encouraged, and grow in their faith.
