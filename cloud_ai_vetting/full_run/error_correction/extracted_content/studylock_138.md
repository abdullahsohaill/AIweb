## Struggling to stay focused while studying?

StudyLock helps you stay on track by blocking distracting apps — and only unlocking them once you’ve studied and passed a quiz.

## From setup to focus in four steps

### Step 1 — Upload Study Content

Use your camera, photo library, or upload a PDF.

### Step 2 — Choose apps to block

Pick apps to block during study time.

### Step 3 — Set required quiz accuracy

Choose the score needed to unlock apps.

### Step 4 — Select study language

Learn in your preferred language.
