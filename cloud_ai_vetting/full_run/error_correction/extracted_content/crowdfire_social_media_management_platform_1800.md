## Thank You for 15 Incredible Years! 💛

After 15 amazing years of helping creators, brands, and businesses grow online, we've made the difficult decision to shut down Crowdfire.
Our platform is now offline.
This isn't goodbye without gratitude—
It's a heartfelt thank you for trusting us, building with us, and being part of our journey.
We've seen millions of posts scheduled, stories told, and dreams launched. And we couldn't have done it without you.

## Looking for your next social media sidekick?

As a part of our community, we're excited to introduce you to SocialBee — a social media management tool we trust and recommend. It's packed with powerful features to help you plan, create, and grow your online presence.
As part of the shutdown process, all Crowdfire user data will be permanently deleted on June 30th, 2025.
Need help? If you have any questions about cancellations or refunds, we're still here to help.
With all our love and thanks, The Crowdfire Team ❤️
