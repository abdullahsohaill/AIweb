### How do AI website builders compare to traditional website builders?

AI website builders are significantly faster, easier to use, and more automated than traditional website builders. Here are the key differences:
- Speed: AI builders create complete websites in minutes based on simple descriptions. Traditional builders require you to choose templates, customize layouts, write content, and configure settings manually.
- Ease of use: While both are user-friendly, AI builders do most of the work for you by automating and simplifying the design process. Traditional builders require more hands-on decisions and time investment at every step.
- Content creation: AI builders generate original content, headlines, and copy tailored to your business. Traditional builders give you templates that you need to fill with your own content manually.
