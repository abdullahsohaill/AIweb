Hi, I’m Haonan Zhang (张浩楠 in Chinese). I am currently a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC), supervised by Prof. Lianli Gao and Prof. Jingkuan Song . 
I received my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science and Technology from Xidian University in 2020. That same year, I joined UESTC for a Master’s degree and later transferred to the Ph.D. program in 2022.
Currently, I am also a visiting Ph.D. student at the Multimedia and Human Understanding Group (MHUG), University of Trento (Italy), supervised by Prof. Nicu Sebe .
My research interests focus on Multi-modal Learning, LLMs. I am currently exploring the exciting and fast-growing field of Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models as part of my recent research.
For more information, please see my CV .
