## Create AI Art and run custom chatbots on your own hardware

With  AI Runner you are able to run Stable Diffusion, Kandinsky and other  diffusion AI models on your own computer without any technical  knowledge. Just download the app,

### Canvas with collapsible panels

The canvas allows you to outpaint images to any size and collapsible 
panels make it easy to view your work on a clean interface as you do so.
pair it with your favorite model and  run.

### Customizable Chatbot

Customizable chatbots run with Mistral-7b, allowing you to have real-time text-to-speech / speech-to-text conversations. You can set unique system instructions, bot names, and even personalities. The bots will change their mood based on the context of your conversations.

### Drawing tools

A simple but growing set of art tools paired with image-to-image allow you to convert sketches into works of art.

### Image filters

Modify your creations with image filters, such as the pixel art filter and film filter to give them a unique look and feel.

### Customizable

Choose custom paths to store your models, embedding, LoRAs, images, videos and gifs. Tweak memory settings and more.

### Easy prompt storage

Save your favorite prompts, edit and reload them with the prompt browser.

### Features

Please note that AI Runner is under active ongoing development. We work quickly to triage and fix any and all reported bugs. Certain features, such as real-time text-to-speech conversations with the Chatbot are prone to error.
You must download your own Stable Diffusion models in order to use the AI art features.
