## NEW: ChatGPT restaurant recs, powered by OpenTable

We may be the OG of the online restaurant reservation scene, but that doesn’t hold us back from keeping things fresh. We’re proud to be the first brand to bring restaurant recommendations to ChatGPT – aka the internet’s favorite chatbot – via its newly launched plugins. Even better: the recommendations come with a direct link to book on OpenTable.
We’re always looking for new ways to help diners discover their new favorite restaurant, and now it’s as easy as texting your best friend. Hosting friends this weekend and need help finding a restaurant with a killer outdoor patio? We got you covered. Dropped the ball on planning your sister’s birthday dinner? Ask for avails near you and we’ll hook you up.
Try asking questions such as…
- I have a date I want to impress. What’s a restaurant with oysters and great cocktails in the Upper West Side NY?
- Where’s a highly reviewed brunch spot I can take my mom this Mother’s Day in Charleston?
- My girlfriend loves Japanese food, what’s something similar that has reservations for this weekend in downtown LA?
- I’m throwing a surprise birthday dinner for my BFF next Friday and inviting 10 of our closest friends. What are some restaurants we should check out in Austin?
No matter where you’re searching, OpenTable makes it easier to find the perfect table, every time.
This integration will roll out gradually, starting with ChatGPT Plus subscribers. After additional testing, it will roll out to more people.
For updates, follow us on Instagram .
