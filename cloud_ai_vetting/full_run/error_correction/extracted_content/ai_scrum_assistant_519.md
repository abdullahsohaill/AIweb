## More details

Introducing AI Scrum Assistant, the AI-powered app tailored to optimize your Scrum team's workflow using ChatGPT-4. Elevate sprint planning with automated user stories, acceptance criteria, and QA test case generation in your Jira issues, improving efficiency and collaboration.
Key features include:
- AI-Generated User Stories: Generate or refine your user story description and keep your stories consistent across teams and projects using AI automation.
- AI-generated Acceptance Criteria: Generate detailed acceptance criteria directly from your Jira issues using AI to save time and increase precision. This includes both rule-based and Behavior Driven Development (BDD) acceptance criteria.
- This includes both rule-based and Behavior Driven Development (BDD) acceptance criteria.
- AI-generated QA test case scenarios: Leverage AI to create comprehensive test cases, ensuring thorough coverage and quality.
- Perfect for both seasoned Scrum masters and beginners.
Embrace the future of Agile with AI Scrum Assistant and transform your project management with cutting-edge technology.
