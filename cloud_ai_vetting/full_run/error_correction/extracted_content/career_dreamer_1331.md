## Frequently asked questions

### What is Career Dreamer?

Career Dreamer is an early-stage experiment exploring how AI can assist people in discovering career possibilities.

### Who can benefit from Career Dreamer?

Career Dreamer can help anyone who finds it challenging to start planning their career or figuring out their next steps. For example, you could be a recent college graduate, a military vet or parent reentering the workforce, or someone who has an unconventional job history. Wherever you’re starting from, Career Dreamer can help you make more informed career choices in a simple and fun way.

### What is a Career Identity?

Your career identity is the unique value you bring to the workforce, informed by your life and work experience and shaped by your strengths, motivations, and interests. Defining your career identity can make you better equipped to choose a career path that aligns with what matters most to you. Consider showcasing your career identity statement on your resume, cover letter, or online professional profile to communicate your unique value to potential employers.

### What data sources does Career Dreamer use?

Career Dreamer uses U.S. labor market data from Lightcast , as well as wage data from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics .

### How does Career Dreamer use AI?

Career Dreamer uses AI to generate insights based on labor market data and the information you provide. Examples include the Career Identity Statement and the career results in the Explore Paths page, among many others.

### Where is Career Dreamer available?

Career Dreamer is currently only available in the US.

### Does Career Dreamer collect my data?

Career Dreamer does not save your work on our servers. Your progress is saved in your web browser, so you can easily return to it. We use Google Analytics to monitor aggregated activity so we can improve the experience for users. For more information, see the Google Terms of Service .
