### Preference Cookies

DreamHost never sells your data. But like most sites, DreamHost uses Cookies for content delivery and website functionality. By choosing to "Accept all," we may also use Cookies to gather site statistics, remember your preferences, and deliver and measure the effectiveness of interest-based offers. Click the different category headings to find out more and change your default settings. Find out more in our Privacy Policy
Our website can’t operate properly without essential cookies. We only collect the minimum personal information needed to provide services on the Website.
We use Targeting Cookies to display personalized advertisements on other sites you may visit. We work with approved partners to deliver relevant content and measure the effectiveness of these advertisements. Opt-out requests are honored.
We use Performance Cookies to measure and improve your Website experience.
These Cookies let us save your choices and some of the information you provide when browsing the Website. They don’t track your browsing activity on other websites. Without preference cookies, the Website may not work reliably.
