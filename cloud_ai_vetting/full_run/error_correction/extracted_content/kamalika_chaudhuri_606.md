### Research

My research focuses on AI privacy, security, safety, and alignment (formerly known as trustworthy machine learning). This includes understanding how to measure privacy and security leaks in LLMs, as well as issues such as uncertainty estimation. I am also broadly interested in many aspects of safety and post-training, such as confidentiality, misalignment, and others. Here are the slides from a talk I recently gave at Harvard about our recent work.
My former UCSD group used to maintain a group blog with guest posts from collaborators. I also have a personal blog.
