## Professional Player Tools

Versus Finder Compare players and teams or see head to head results between teams, players, or player vs. team. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Player Season Finder Search through player regular season and playoff statistics spanning from 1946-47 to today for single seasons or combined seasons that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Player Game Finder Search through player regular season and playoff game logs spanning from 1946-47 to today for games that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Player Streak Finder Search through player game logs spanning from 1946-47 to today for streaks that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Player Span Finder Find best/worst statistical spans for any number of games. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Player Quarter Finder Search through player regular season and playoff logs by quarter. NBA only.

## Professional Team Tools

Team Season Finder Search through team season stats spanning from 1946-47 to today for seasons that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Team Game Finder Search through team game logs spanning from 1946-47 to today for games that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Team Streak Finder Search through team game logs and season stats spanning from 1946-47 to today for streaks that match your criteria. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.
Team Span Finder Find best/worst statistical spans for teams across any number of games. NBA/BAA, ABA, & WNBA.

## College Tools

Team Season Finder Search through team season stats (since 1947-48 for MCBB, 1981-82 for WCBB) for NCAA Division I.
Team Game Finder Search through scores (since 1949-50 for MCBB, 2002-03 for WCBB) and stats (since 2010-11 for MCBB, 2002-03 for WCBB) for NCAA Division I. Men's NCAA Tourney scores and stats all-time back to 1939, Women's NCAA Tourney scores and stats since 2003.
Player Season Finder Search through player season stats (since 1947-48 for MCBB, 1981-82 for WCBB) for NCAA Division I.
Player Game Finder Search through player game logs (since 2010-11 for MCBB, 2002-03 for WCBB) for NCAA Division I. Men's NCAA Tourney stats all-time back to 1939, Women's NCAA Tourney stats back to 2003.

## Other Tools

Event Finder Search through millions of events to find all field goals for a team , all steals for a player , and much, much more. NBA only.
Shot Finder Search through millions of shots to find the most prolific dunkers , the best clutch shooters , and much, much more. NBA only.
