# Litelink
**URL:** https://www.litelink.at
**Page Title:** Home - Litelink
--------------------

Litelink is a tool that solves the problem of linking with Instagram and with just one link pointing to a collection of links that you can manage with our platform, your problem is solved in a clean and good way.

### Create an account

You just need an account and we'll do everything else for you.

### Add your links

Create a collection of links from your account.

### Add to Instagram

Change your Instagram bio link to your new profile link and thats it :)
