## Play high quality music in your Discord server for free.

Rocking over 7 million discord servers.

## A Symphony of Sources

FredBoat rocks the house with tracks, playlists, and livestreams sourced from various platforms.

## Search on the go!

Instead of linking directly to a track, you can also choose to simply just type in the name of your song and let FredBoat find it for you.

## Mix and Match Your Own Beats

Don't just listen, take control – be the DJ of your own party! Shape your own ultimate playlists right inside FredBoat!

## Eat, Sleep, Shuffle and Repeat

FredBoat has full support for shuffling your playlist. You can even choose to put just one or the entire queue on repeat.
