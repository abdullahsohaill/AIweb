## Privacy-First By Design

Your data belongs to you, never to us. At Msty, privacy isn’t an afterthought, it’s the foundation.

### Zero Telemetry, Zero Tracking

We never track, log, or analyze how you use Msty Studio. There are no hidden analytics or background data collection.
True black box: We have no insight into your usage, and that’s intentional.

### Keep It Local, Go Offline

Prefer total privacy? Run local models and disconnect from the internet. Your conversations continue without touching the cloud.
Online models like Claude and Gemini are powerful, but if you need complete privacy, use local.

### Local-First Storage

Your chats, models, settings, and prompts live on your device, not on our servers.
Your device, your data: Nothing leaves your control.

### Privacy By Default

Msty Studio works out of the box with no account, no forced sign-in, and no hidden data sharing. Privacy isn’t a setting, it’s the default.
No surprises: Privacy is built in from day one.

### Ready To Take Control Of Your Data?

Experience the power of AI without compromising your privacy. Join thousands of users who trust Msty Studio with their most sensitive work.
