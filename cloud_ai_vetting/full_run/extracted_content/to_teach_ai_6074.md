How does artificial intelligence work at to teach?
To teach uses artificial intelligence to create practice exercises and personalize educational content. To ensure the quality of the teaching content, we provide a human-made framework in order to be able to exploit the potential of AI. We follow the Human-Centered AI approach on creating AI systems that amplify and augment rather than displace human abilities.
