### Less repetitive work. More creative work.

### GUI when you want it

Your first workflow in 5 minutes
Launch background agents in seconds. Start with battle-tested workflows for GitHub, Sentry, Snyk, Linear—then make them yours. Customize prompts, models, and MCP tools to fit your stack. Trigger on events or schedules. Monitor performance, track interventions, manage across your team. Real automation that adapts to how you work.

### Terminal when you need it

Built for how developers actually work
Same workflows as Mission Control, pure terminal power. Iterate fast, pipe outputs, script everything. For devs who think in commands and want their automation where they live. Test changes to your background agents in REPL-mode and debug in real-time.
[LINK: Build with CLI](https://docs.continue.dev/cli/quick-start#tui-mode%3A-large-development-tasks)

### Runs where you work

Fits into your development environment
Deploy your workflows anywhere: local bash scripts for testing, CI/CD for production, cron for schedules, etc. Your automation runs on your infrastructure—GitHub Actions, Jenkins, GitLab CI. Just drop your workflows into existing pipelines and watch them scale.
[LINK: Deploy in CI](https://docs.continue.dev/cli/quick-start#headless-mode%3A-automated-workflows)
