## How we approach AI projects

We build AI solutions in line with the principles of fairness and explainability. For instance, we train AI
          models with diverse and representative data sets to prevent bias, or assess model output against selected
          performance metrics to identify potential reasons for inaccuracies.
Our team follows the MLOps practices, including automated ML model training and deployment, version control,
          and continuous model monitoring to speed up time to market, make the training process reproducible and
          auditable, and perform timely troubleshooting.
Our experts develop AI applications in strict compliance with applicable data management standards and
          regulations, such as the GDPR and ISO/IEC 27001. This also involves equipping your solution with robust
          security mechanisms like IAM and data encryption to mitigate cyber exposure.
We follow data governance best practices throughout the AI lifecycle, such as performing continuous data
          validation and using data lineage tools, to provide end-to-end visibility into data flows and transformations,
          ensuring your data remains accurate, consistent, and fully auditable.
