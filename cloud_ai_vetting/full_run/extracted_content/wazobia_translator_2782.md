## Publisher Description

Wazobia Translator is a simple app that helps you translate words from English language to any of the three major Nigerian languages (yoruba, hausa, igbo) and vice versa.
The app also allows translation between the different languages e.g Yoruba to Igbo, Igbo to Hausa, etc.
