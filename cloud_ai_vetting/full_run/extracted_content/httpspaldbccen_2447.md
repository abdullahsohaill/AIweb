## Patch Note v0.7.1

2026/1/21

## Balance Adjustments

- In PvP, the on-screen raid log will now continue to be displayed while your base is under attack from other players.
- The Summoning Altar at your base will no longer be destroyed at the end of the raids inside the raid area.
- Reduce the recovery time between swings for melee weapons (Swords, Katana, Beam Swords).
- Increased the attack range of melee weapons (Swords, Katana, Beam Swords).

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where mods would still function when enabled in their individual settings even when "Allow Mod Use" was turned off in the Mod Management menu.
- Fixed an issue where attack notifications would not appear when another player approached your base while riding a Pal in PvP.
- Fixed an issue where Hartalis " Sacred Barrier " was not properly blocking the Oil Rig's anti-aircraft laser.
- Fixed an issue where working Pals would sometimes freeze in combat mode during raid boss battles and not engage in combat.
- Fixed an issue where Life Fruit , Power Fruit and Stout Fruit would not be rewarded when clearing the Hartalis raid boss on "Extreme" difficulty.
- Fixed an issue where players would die while on water after leaving a dungeon and before landing, causing their inventory and Pals to be dropped back into the dungeon.
- Fixed an issue where Pals consumed in a Pal Essence Condenser would disappear after a short time had passed since being restored from the Global Palbox .
- Fixed an issue where Wall Signs would be placed embedded into certain buildings when constructed on them.
- Fixed an issue where the graphics of some building materials and equipment would not be rendered correctly.
- Fixed an issue where some background music and sound effects would continue to play or would play in the wrong place.
- [Steam] Fixed an issue where the achievement "Alpha Pal Slayer" would not be unlocked correctly.
- Other minor bugs fixes.
