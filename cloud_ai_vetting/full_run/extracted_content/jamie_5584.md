## Safe, secure and private

Everything in Jamie is designed to keep your meetings safe and secure. Because your business is nobody else's business.
Full storage & processing EU data residency.
In our commitment to privacy, meeting audio is deleted after transcription.
Jamie forces HTTPS on all connections and encrypts data in-transit with TLS 1.2. All data at-rest is secured using AES 256-bit encryption.
Committed to compliance with Europe's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).
Your data is never used for the training of models, not by us or any of the model providers.
Restrict access to system-level privileges.
Sign in with Google or Microsoft SSO.
Privacy and security measures to ensure that personal information is appropriately safeguarded.
Let users with the same domain join the workspace automatically.
