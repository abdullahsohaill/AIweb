MPICH is a high performance and widely portable implementation of the Message Passing Interface (MPI) standard.
MPICH and its derivatives form the most widely used implementations of MPI. They are used on many of world’s top supercomputers, including all three of the world’s exascale machines (November 2024 ranking): El Capitan, Frontier, and Aurora.
