### AI co-pilot to accelerate your sales deals

Real-Time Technical and Sales Assistant for your AE's and SE's

## Reduce SE onboarding time from months to days.

Easily integrate the genie bot into zoom, google meet or Microsoft teams and the bot will join your calls and provide instant real-time, in-call answers to the product related questions raised by the prospect

## Save 5+ sales hours/week.

Import your product documents & FAQs through seamless integration with confluence, Jira, Slack or simply uploading via Aladdin’s web interface to aid in reducing the chances of SEs underselling your product and also effectively reducing async communication between SE and the prospect

## Close deals 1.25x times faster.

Aladdin reduces the hassle of back and forth communication amongst the sales team by storing the call transcripts and the conversed Q&A's to enrich your AI engine thereby ensuring that SE's and AE's have access to the latest product knowledge during their calls
