### FAQ

Unlike SEO, which is one-dimensional and cares about ranking on the SERP, GEO is two-dimensional, where being mentioned matters, but how you are mentioned is just as important.
Optimize for AI readability, leverage structured data, focus on quality content, and monitor and adapt. AthenaHQ helps you achieve all of the above in one unified platform.
The future of search is generative. Even Google is coming out with its own generative search called AI Mode beyond just AI Overviews. As the search architecture increasingly shifts to generative search, GEO will become increasingly a necessity for all businesses.
There is an early mover advantage to optimizing for GEO. Much like how many businesses are built on SEO, companies that adopt GEO now position themselves to be leaders as AI search grows.
