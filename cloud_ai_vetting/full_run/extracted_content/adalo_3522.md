## Everything You Need to Build Your App

Every Adalo app is made up of screens & components. Screens are the individual pages that a user might see as they interact with your app. Components are the elements on each screen that display information such as text, images, lists, buttons, and more. Screens and components are how you will visually build your mobile or web app.
Feature Templates allow you to quickly add an entire feature, such as user profile and account settings, and come with fully built screens, actions, and database collections. Feature Templates are a unique way of building quickly without sacrificing the ability to customize your app.
Unlike other app builders, Adalo has its own relational database that can be used as the foundation of your app. The database comes with extensive logic, and prebuilt property types to make app-building a breeze.
Actions create the interactivity of a mobile or web app. Adalo has several basic actions, like creating or editing data, and more advanced actions, such as triggering notifications. Inside Adalo you can connect directly to Zapier to connect your app to thousands of other pieces of software or create your very own custom actions.
Control the fonts, colors, sizes, shapes, and more inside our visual designer. With common tools like alignment, positioning, borders, weights, and the list goes on, you’ll feel right at home with this Canva or Figma-like experience while designing your app.
Publish your app to the public through an Adalo subdomain, a custom domain, or to one of the app stores (or both!). Get your app into the hands of all your customers without sacrificing the time or money it takes to develop apps for all platforms. With Adalo, you build once & publish anywhere!
Preview your app across all screen sizes directly in the builder. Once you’re ready to ‘go live,’ Share your app with your customers. It’s really that simple.
