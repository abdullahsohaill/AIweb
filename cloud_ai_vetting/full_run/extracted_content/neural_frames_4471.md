neural frames gives you access to the world's leading AI video models in one platform. Switch between them instantly to find your perfect visual style.

Your custom models become part of your creative toolkit, ready to use across all your projects.

Upscaling transforms AI-generated videos into crisp, broadcast-quality output up to 4K resolution. While most AI video platforms charge premium fees for HD output, neural frames includes intelligent upscaling on every export at no extra cost. Our specialized AI enhancement ensures your videos look professional on any screen—from phone displays to cinema projectors. Whether you're creating for YouTube, Instagram, or professional presentations, your videos automatically receive this quality boost without any additional steps or fees.
How does music visualization work?
neural frames offers the most advanced audio-reactive system available. We automatically extract 8 different stems from your track (drums, bass, vocals, melody, etc.) and give you precise control over how visuals respond to each element. With 10+ modulation parameters, you can make colors pulse with the kick drum, transitions follow the melody, or effects intensify with vocals. Unlike basic visualizers, our system truly understands your music's structure, energy, and emotion—creating videos that don't just match your beat but amplify your artistic vision. Perfect for electronic music, hip-hop, rock, or any genre where the visuals should dance with the sound.

We are all for simplicity, and there extract key features of your song completely automatically, such as lyrics, the key, and the tempo.
