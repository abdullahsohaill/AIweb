### What does a talent management system do?

A talent management system (TMS) is a comprehensive software solution that streamlines and integrates HR and workforce management processes. Its primary functions include:
- Recruitment and onboarding: Facilitating the hiring process by managing job postings, applications, and new worker integration
- Performance management: Monitoring and evaluating worker performance to ensure alignment with organizational goals
- Learning and development: Providing tools for workforce training, skill development, and career progression
- Succession planning: Identifying and preparing internal candidates for future leadership roles within the organization
By centralizing these functions, a TMS enhances efficiency, improves data accuracy, and supports strategic decision-making.
