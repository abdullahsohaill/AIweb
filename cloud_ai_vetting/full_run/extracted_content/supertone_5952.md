## Real-Time Voice Changer

Shift is a real-time voice changer that instantly transforms your voice. Pick a character and sound like them in a split second. Crack jokes in an FPS lobby, role-play in VRChat, or debut a new persona on your stream. Try 100+ voices free every month.

## De-Noise & De-Reverb Voice Separator

Supertone Clear is an easy-to-use de-noise & de-reverb plug-in. Three simple knobs - Voice, Ambience, and Reverb - let you achieve perfectly clear vocals in just a few tweaks.

## Reverb & EQ Dialogue Match

Supertone Air matches reverb and EQ so your ADR sits naturally in the mix. Sample any dialogue clip and Air recreates both the space and the EQ, giving your ADR production-ready sound in seconds.
