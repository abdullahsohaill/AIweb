## Explore your graph

Easily identify dependencies and connections between modules, and dive deep into the inner workings of your classes. Say goodbye to tedious manual analysis and hello to streamlined, efficient troubleshooting. See it in action!

## Routes navigator

Navigate through your system's entrypoints with our intuitive interface. Discover which guards, pipes, interceptors, and other enhancers are bound to a given route, giving you a complete understanding of your system's routing.

## Interactive code playground

Know whether a node is request-scoped or transient, exported from a host module or not. Interact with controllers, providers, and other elements in real-time and see the results instantly. See it in action!

## CI/CD integration

Keep track of changes with ease by using our CI/CD integration. It compares the updated graph to the previous version, creating a clear report that illustrates the differences. Learn more here!

## Audit

Devtools will automatically generate an audit report for you, highlighting potential issues and suggesting improvements. It's a great way to get started with your application's refactoring process.
