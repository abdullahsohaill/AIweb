## FAQ About FocuSee

What is FocuSee and how does it work?
FocuSee is an AI-powered screen recorder that automatically edits your recordings
                with auto zoom on clicks, mouse movement tracking, cursor highlighting, silence
                removal, AI audio enhancement, and more — producing professional-looking videos
                without any complex manual editing.
Is FocuSee available on Mac and Windows?
Yes. FocuSee supports both macOS and Windows.
Why is FocuSee different from other screen recorders?
FocuSee doesn’t just record your screen — it auto-polishes the video for you. Unlike
                traditional screen recorders that only capture raw footage and leave all editing to
                you, FocuSee instantly turns your recording into a polished and easy-to-follow video
                the moment you hit stop — without hours of manual work.
How does FocuSee’s auto-polishing work?
After you record, FocuSee analyzes your screen actions and applies:
- Auto zoom effects on mouse clicks
- 3d Motion on cursor movement
- Dynamic camera layouts
- Accurate captions & keyboard shortcuts
- Spotlight to highlight areas worth notice
- AI background removal or replacement
- AI audio enhancement and noise reduction
- AI removal of filler words and silence
Everything is generated automatically or with a click — no tedious editing needed.
What types of videos can I create with FocuSee?
You can create tutorials, product demos, training videos, software walkthroughs,
                online course content, and any screen-based videos that benefit from professional,
                clear, and high-impact presentation.
Is FocuSee a free screen recording tool?
FocuSee offers a free trial that allows you to explore standard features and export
                one high-quality video in up to 4K resolution for free. Some advanced AI-powered
                features require a subscription and credits to use.
Does FocuSee support webcam + screen recording?
Yes. You can record your screen, webcam, or both simultaneously, making it ideal for
                tutorial creators and product demo presenters.
Can I record my mobile device with FocuSee?
Yes. FocuSee now supports recording iOS and Android screens. Simply connect your
                device to your computer via USB and record it with the same auto-polishing features
                you get on a desktop. This makes it easy to create mobile tutorials, app demos, and
                walkthroughs.
Is FocuSee safe to use? Is my data private?
Yes. FocuSee is safe to use. Your recordings are saved locally by default, and cloud
                upload is completely optional and protected with SSL encryption. Features like
                cursor tracking, click detection, and shortcut recognition are processed locally on
                your device and are never uploaded, stored, or sent to FocuSee servers. Your data
                stays private, secure, and fully under your control at all times.
Does FocuSee support long recordings?
Yes. FocuSee can handle longer recordings for training sessions, onboarding videos,
                and detailed software walkthroughs, applying auto-editing to simplify the final
                output.
What is the Teleprompter feature in FocuSee?
The Teleprompter feature in FocuSee allows you to display text on your screen while
                recording, making it easier to follow a script for presentations, tutorials, and
                demos. The teleprompter can be adjusted to your preferred font size, speed, and
                display position, ensuring a smooth and professional presentation experience.
