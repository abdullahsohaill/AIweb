## Learn how to learn.

Bloom allows you to generate high-quality learning plans in just a few clicks, full of tutorials, course material and more.

## What's a learning plan?

A learning plan consists of course material from the best sources across the internet, and the most relevant YouTube videos, all divided into the perfect plan for you.

### Course material

Bloom analyzes your details, goals, etc. to find the best course material available on the Internet.

### Videos

It finds the most relevant videos and playlists for every lesson with you in mind.

### Notes and Progress Tracking

Takes notes, track your progress in the app itself. Everything is synced to the cloud.

## Use efficient learning techniques.

Bloom uses the latest study techniques like spaced repetition and others that are scientifically proven to help you learn better & faster.

## How does it work?

It's simple. All you have to do is enter what you want to learn, give Bloom some details about yourself, and grab a coffee (optional). It comes up with a learning plan that comes with all of the features mentioned above. And more.
- Step 1 Assessing the landscape Bloom creates a unique profile for you, that has the skill you want to learn, details and your goals.
- Step 2 The Plan It comes up with a learning plan, with content that's relevant to you.
- Step 3 Learn, note and track Start your learning plan, take notes and track your progress - all in Bloom itself. Happy learning!
