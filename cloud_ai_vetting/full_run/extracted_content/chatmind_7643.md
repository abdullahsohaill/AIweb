## One Click to Generate AI Mind Maps

Save tons of hours with Mapify: Instantly map out key points of documents, webpages, or YouTube videos—learning has never been this easy.

### PDF/Doc to Mind Map

### YouTube to Mind Map

Click to navigate key moments from the mind map directly in the video.
Get full, accurate transcripts to support your understanding.

### Ask Anything
