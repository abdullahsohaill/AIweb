## Why Direct-to-Consumer?

Going direct-to-consumer with interactive video gives you the opportunity to forge a direct relationship with your customer, entirely cutting out the middleman. With this approach, you can present your video content and foster your community on owned digital real estate. When you activate your direct-to-consumer strategy on Maestro, you take back control. You own the data and the relationship with your customers without sacrificing the reach. Collect valuable contact information from viewers to nurture your audience, and access detailed viewership and revenue analytics.
