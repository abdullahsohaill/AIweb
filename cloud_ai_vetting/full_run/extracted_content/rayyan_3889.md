## How to Save Your Time with Rayyan

Rayyan is a powerful tool designed to help researchers save time during systematic reviews and literature reviews. Here are some key features that can help you save time

### AI-Powered Screening & Mobile App

Rayyan's AI streamlines your review process, reducing screening time by up to 90%. Plus, the Rayyan mobile app enables you to work anywhere anytime, turning downtime into productive time.

### Deduplication & Systematic Resolver

Rayyan’s advanced deduplication feature automatically identifies and removes duplicate references from your dataset, ensuring you don’t waste time reviewing the same article twice. This tool is especially beneficial for systematic and literature reviews, enhancing the efficiency of your review process, improving accuracy, and helping maintain a clean and organized Review data, allowing you to focus on the most relevant and unique studies.

### Filters, Bulk Action & PICO

Rayyan Systems offers customizable filters to quickly find relevant studies and bulk actions to manage multiple items efficiently. Enhance your research with the PICO framework, structuring questions by Population, Intervention, Comparison, and Outcome.
