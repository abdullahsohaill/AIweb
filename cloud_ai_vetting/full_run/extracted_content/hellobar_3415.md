Using Hello Bar to launch a popup campaign is super easy. You can get your popup campaign launched in a few minutes and you don’t have to just stick to one pop up. To use Hello Bar’s popup builder to launch a pop up, simply:

Hello Bar’s popup builder has professionally designed popup designs so you can create stunning popups and high converting popups without the need for a designer. These popup designs are easy to edit using Hello Bar’s no code builder and you can match each popup to your brand. In fact, Hello Bar’s popup builder includes a “design assistant” feature that enables you to automatically match your popup to your website look and feel.
Hello Bar got its start as a top bar but now offers a use range of different eye catching popups. From full page takeovers to overlay popups, to bottom bar popups, slider popups, inline, on click, popups and so many more. Hello Bar’s popup builder includes a huge range of different triggers that set when a popup will be served. Many popup campaigns will include exit intent triggers that display a website pop when somebody goes to exit a website or webpage. But exit intent is just one of many triggers. Hello Bar includes scroll triggers, upsell popups when somebody gets to a completed sale, a time trigger, and so many more. The most popular triggers include: – Scroll trigger : Will serve a popup when somebody scrolls down a certain percentage of a webpage. – Exit trigger : A popup appears when a visitor goes to exit a webpage or website. – Time trigger : Popups are served based on how long a visitor has been on a website. – Visit trigger : Popups are served based on how many times somebody has visitor a website. – Visitor click: A popup can be set up to serve when a visitor clicks on a button or a word in an article. Given that these popups serve on click, these triggers can be very targeted to specific content. – And many more : There are a ton of additional ways that smart popups can be triggered through Hello Bar’s popup builder.

### Target your popups

Once you have designed your site popups, it’s now key to target your popups to ensure visitors feel that the popups are relevant to why they came to your site. You can target your popups using Hello Bar’s advanced targeting features, all of which are built right into Hello Bar’s popup builder. You can target a popup based on: Visitor source : You can serve different popups to different people based on where the visitor is visiting from. For example, you can serve a different popup to somebody who came from Facebook vs a Google search. Or, you can serve a different popup to somebody who came from one search term on Google vs another search term. The ability to serve popups based on source and can fire up your lead generation efforts and conversion rates. Visitor behavior : You can also serve different popups depending on a user’s behavior. For example, you can serve popups to only visitors who come to your site for a first time and serve different popups to those who are returning. Content : You can match the content of your popups based on the content of the page that your visitor is engaging with. Device type : Serve different popups to people who access your site on mobile devices vs desktop devices. And more… Serve different popups based on geography and other demographic information and so many more. You can even launch custom targeting rules using a custom targeting engine built directly into Hello Bar’s popup builder.
