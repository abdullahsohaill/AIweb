Frequently Asked Questions
What is an Ai Website Builder
Think of an AI website builder as your automated design partner. Instead of starting from scratch, you simply describe your business in a chat, and the AI generates a professional, fully functional website in seconds. It handles the heavy lifting by creating the structure, copy, and visuals, giving you a solid foundation that you can easily customize to match your brand.
How user-friendly is the CodeDesign AI for beginners?
The CodeDesign AI builder experience is designed with beginners in mind. You can either chat with AI or use the drag-and-drop interface. Our AI-guided assistant helps make the process of building a website as straightforward and intuitive as possible. No prior coding or design experience is required to create a professional-looking website.
What is the main difference between the two editor types?
The main difference is how AI is used.
The Vibe Coding Builder Editor relies on AI prompts to create and modify your site.
The Visual Drag & Drop Builder Editor is manual-first, with optional AI features for text and image generation.
Both editors are powerful, but they suit different building styles.
Can I publish or host my website using CodeDesign?
Yes! You can publish your website on CodeDesign. The published website will be hosted in CodeDesign and you can also connect your custom domain.
Does your AI website generator support code export?
Absolutely! Our AI website generator allows you to export the HTML code with a additional support.
Do I need to know coding to build with CodeDesign?
Knowing how to code isn't necessary to build with CodeDesign. However, having a basic understanding of web development will give you an advantage.
Is CodeDesign Free?

Who can use CodeDesign?

Can i share my project and collaborate with my team?
