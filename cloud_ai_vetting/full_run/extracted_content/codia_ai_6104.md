## Powerful Features for Modern Design

From idea to polished visuals in minutes—our AI-powered design tools amplify creativity and speed.

### Screenshot → Editable Design

Transform screenshots into editable designs effortlessly. Trusted by over 300,000 professional designers from 181 countries, generating over 1 million designs worldwide.

### Prompt → Design Magic

Supercharge your design workflow with AI-powered tool that generates fully editable UI components from plain prompts. Go from idea to visual in just one click.

### Image → Vector/PNG to SVG

Transform bitmap images into stunning, scalable vector graphics with advanced AI. Perfect for designers looking to elevate their digital graphics.

### Photoshop → Editable Figma

Easily import your PSD files into Figma or export your Figma designs back to PSD. This two-way compatibility ensures smooth collaboration between teams using different tools and eliminates the need to manually recreate assets.

### PDF → Figma

Transform static PDF files into stunning, fully editable Figma designs with unprecedented ease and precision using advanced AI technology.

### Web → Editable Figma

Effortlessly transform any website into an editable Figma design, removing the necessity to rebuild website elements from scratch.

### Illustrator/EPS → Figma

Import Adobe Illustrator (.ai and .eps) files into Figma with ease, preserving all vector layers, editable text, and design elements.

### Office Files → Figma

Convert Office files into fully editable Figma layers with enhanced efficiency, precision, and security for seamless workflow transitions.

### Canva → Editable Figma

Convert Canva designs into fully editable Figma layers, enhancing efficiency and precision when transitioning from Canva to Figma.

### Notion → Figma

Easily turn your Notion documents into fully editable Figma files. Perfect for product teams, designers, and anyone who works between documentation and design.

### Codia AI Remixr

Remixr is a powerful text-to-image AI that transforms a single prompt into infinite creative possibilities. With thousands of ready-made templates, you don’t need prompt skills to get started.
