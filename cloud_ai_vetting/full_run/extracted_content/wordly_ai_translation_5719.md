### Flexible Attendee Access Options

Select from 5 options - phone, tablet, computer, room displays, and on stage.
Attendees can access Wordly AI translation from their personal device - phone, tablet, or computer. And listen or read in their preferred language. Meeting organizers can also allow attendees to read a live transcript on monitors in the room.

### Extensive AI Translation Languages

Access dozens of translation languages - over 3,000 language pairs.
Attendees and Speakers can each select their preferred languages - including the top international business languages. Presenters can speak in multiple languages (two-way translation), enabling you to support "one to many" and "many to many" sessions. Attendees can read a live transcript or listen to audio output.
