## FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### What is Prefixbox and how does it improve on-site search?

Prefixbox is a leading AI-powered product search and discovery solution for E-commerce retailers that increases conversion rate and online revenue.
Prefixbox's powerful suite of AI Search, AI Agent, and Product Recommendations help retailers increase revenue by 45% without manual optimization.
Our robust product discovery solution is used by leading retailers like Carrefour, Leroy Merlin, and Bauhaus.
Prefixbox AI Search is now natively available in the Shopify app store and is the first of its kind to earn the 'Built for Shopify' badge.

### Why does site search matter for my online store?

Shoppers that engage with search are the highest-intent visitors; they are looking for a specific product to buy. Shoppers who search generate over 6 times more revenue than those who don’t. Prefixbox helps increase the number of search users by enhancing the search experience.
Discover more key insights in our Industry Benchmark Report.

### What is Prefixbox’s AI Agent?

Prefixbox turns your browsers into buyers with a ChatGPT-like AI Agent that supports both product discovery and support scenarios.
By simulating natural human conversation, Prefixbox’s AI Agent reduces friction in the shopping journey, provides the ultimate personalized shopping experience, and boosts conversions.
It can recommend products for all types of conversational queries like ‘Gift for a 4-year-old girl’s birthday’ or ‘Do you have products that are both vegan and gluten-free?’

### What does ‘Built for Shopify’ badge mean?

Prefixbox AI Search & Filter app officially earned 'Built for Shopify' status in 2025, the first among enterprise search providers. This recognition confirms that our app meets Shopify’s highest quality standards: it’s easy to use, natively integrated, secure, high-performing, and designed to help merchants succeed.

### How does Prefixbox help increase E-commerce sales?

By delivering more relevant search results and reducing search abandonment, Prefixbox boosts engagement and conversion rates, directly contributing to increased revenue for online retailers.
Prefixbox’s AI Search and AI Agent understand a wide range of conversational questions to return relevant results no matter how shoppers search.

### Is Prefixbox compatible with my existing E-commerce platform?

Yes, Prefixbox integrates seamlessly with major E-commerce platforms like Shopify, Salesforce, Magento, WooCommerce, and custom-built stores through APIs.

### How long does it take to implement Prefixbox?

You can install Prefixbox for your Shopify store with one click. Implementation on other platforms typically takes just a few weeks. The Prefixbox team provides full support during integration to ensure a smooth and successful launch!

### Can I A/B test search configurations with Prefixbox?

Absolutely. Prefixbox includes built-in A/B testing tools (both online and offline) that allow you to experiment with different search configurations and measure their impact on KPIs like click-through rates and conversions.
