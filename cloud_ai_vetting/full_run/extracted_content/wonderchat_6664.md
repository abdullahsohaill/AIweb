## FAQ

### What is Wonderchat?

Wonderchat is an AI Chatbot builder. It allows you to create your own custom GPT chatbot by uploading your sitelink. The chatbot will answer any question related to the sitelink.

### Does it support all languages?

Yes. The chatbot you create will answer questions in the language which the question is asked.

This is the case even if the language used in your sitelink or PDF is different from the language asked by the user.

### Do I need to know code to use Wonderchat?

No. Wonderchat is a no-code AI chatbot builder. You can build your own chatbot in under 2 mintues and add the chatbot to your site afterwards.

### Will I be able to embed the chatbot into my website?

Yes. You can embed the chatbot into any website by insert a simple script into the  html code of any website.

Our chat widgets have been easily deployed across sites such as Notion, Wordpress, Webflow, Bubble, Confluence, Squarespace and more.

### Can multiple team members in my organization manage my chatbots?

Yes, you can. All our paid plans allow for unlimited team members to be added into your team to help you manage your chatbots.

### What websites can I embed my chat widget on?

You can embed Wonderchat widgets on Wordpress, Framer, Wix, Shopify, Squarespace, Webflow, Notion and Bubble.

If you are interested to embed Wonderchat on a website not listed here, you can email us at support@wonderchat.io to ask us if it's do-able. Most of the time, it is as long as your site allows yout to make edits to your HTML code.

### Can you build an integration for me to use this tool for my business?

We offer custom solutions in our enterprise plan.

Kindly write to us at support@wonderchat.io for enquiries and custom projects.

### What is a message?

One message is defined as a message asked and replied to by our chatbot.

So one question from you and a reply from your Wonderchat bot makes up 1 message.

### Can your chatbot read multiple website links?

Yes, once you upgrade to the LITE plan and above, your chatbot will be able to read multiple website links from any number of websites you desire.

### What file formats can your chatbot read?

You can upload file formats such as pdf, txt, csv, docx, pptx and json files as a data source for Wonderchat. Soon, we will also be allowing you to upload videos as a source of knowledge.

### Which GPT model is Wonderchat based on?

Wonderchat supports a wide range of models. We allow you to pick between the latest LLM models

### Do you offer a free trial?

Yes. Every user who signs up will get 20 free messages. They can create a chatbot with any website link or PDF of their choice and start using their 20 messages to chat with their chatbot. 

The trial is free forever, until you run out of messages.
