### Vector layers for linework

- Adjust the thickness and position of lines after they’re drawn
- Erase intersecting lines to easily fix mistakes

### Effortless layer management

- Work with up to 10,000 layers
- Color code your layers and split them into two panes for better organization
- Mask , clip , and select multiple layers to edit them all at once
- Add various effects without modifying the layer itself

### Your ideal workspace

- Adjust any screen or setting to your liking, from shortcuts, units, interface color, palettes, to tool layouts
- Switch between two user interfaces: Simple Mode and Studio Mode
- Simple Mode is easy and simple with just the basic features. Best for beginners!
- Use Studio Mode to access all features

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is Clip Studio Paint chosen by so many creators?

In addition to its natural and easy-to-use pens and brushes, it offers convenient features not found in other drawing apps/paint apps. Its flexibility, which allows both beginners and professionals to create in their own style, is highly valued and makes it a popular choice among creators.

### What kind of works is Clip Studio Paint best suited for as a digital art app?

Clip Studio Paint comes with specialized features not only for illustrations but also for comics and animation. It also supports design work, making it a comprehensive digital art app that helps with a wide variety of creative projects and is widely used in professional environments.

### On which devices can I use Clip Studio Paint?

It is available on Windows, macOS, iPad, iPhone, and Android. As a multi-device drawing app/painting app, it provides a seamless environment where you can sketch on mobile while on the go and continue working on the same project on your PC at home or in the studio.

### Is Clip Studio Paint free to use?

You can try it free for up to 6 months. After that, you can choose from affordable monthly or annual plans, or purchase a one-time perpetual license for Windows and macOS. This flexibility lets you select the option that best fits your needs and budget.

### Is a digital drawing app like Clip Studio Paint easy for beginners?

With its natural pens and brushes and rich support features, beginners can create with confidence. The mobile version uses a simplified UI, making it an intuitive digital drawing app to start with.

### How can I learn to use Clip Studio Paint and its features?

Plenty of official tutorial articles and instructional videos are available. In addition, the community and social media share user tips, helping you efficiently learn to use various drawing tools/paint tools.
