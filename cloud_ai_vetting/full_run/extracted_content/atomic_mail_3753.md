## Blockchain class privacy

Our super secure mail features guarantees only you have access to your emails and your data

### End-to-end encryption

When E2EE is used, a message only appears in decrypted form for the person sending the message and the person receiving the message

### Decrypt emails only on your device locally

We don't keep your private keys on our servers. Once Atomic Blockchain Secured Mail is active, decryption occurs only on your authorized device

### Zero access encryption

Ensures that the content of emails and files is always stored in such a way that the solution provider can never access the information

### Recover account access with seed phrase

No personal data required. Your seed phrase is your key to regain access. Keep it secure and private

## Questions & Answers

### What is Atomic Mail?

Atomic Mail is an email provider with a focus on security and privacy of personal data with encryption function

### Is Atomic Mail free?

Yes. All essential email features, including end-to-end encryption, are free and will stay free. We also offer a paid Plus plan with advanced privacy tools and extra functionality

### What AI email features does Atomic Mail offer?

Our Plus Plan includes multiple AI tools : AI writing assistant (Help me Write & Help me Answer), Summarizer, Security Helper, Translator & Grammar Checker, and a Text-to-Voice converter. For your privacy, these AI tools can’t access encrypted content

### Does Atomic Mail have features for businesses?

Not yet, but they’re in active work. We’re planning to roll out custom domain support for individuals and teams, along with an admin panel for businesses (team management, communication, meetings, advanced tools). Future updates will also include business packages and productivity features like calendar and calling integration. Stay tuned for updates

### How fast are encrypted emails delivered?

Just as fast as a regular emails

### How many aliases can I create from one account?

Free plan: up to 10 aliases. Plus plan: up to 15 aliases per account

### Is it possible to send encrypted emails to other email providers?

Yes, sure! We provide multiple options to encrypt emails for external recipients. For example, Password protected emails are available to be read from any platform and email client

### Does Atomic Mail have a roadmap for new features?

We are constantly building new, powerful tools for Atomic Mail. Upcoming updates include custom domains, dark mode, and IMAP encryption bridge. Check our blog announcements and follow our social media to stay updated
