## What Does This Course Contain?

Week 1: The Foundational Blueprint 🏗️ This week builds your core understanding of what a startup is, how to find its market fit, analyze economic impacts, spot trends, identify ideal customers, validate demand, and build initial prototypes. Week 2: Growth Engines & User Dynamics 🚀 This week focuses on perfecting your product through user feedback, mastering strategies to acquire and retain customers, understanding key growth metrics, and designing sustainable revenue models for exponential growth. Week 3: Competitive Edge & Strategic Adaptation ⚔️ This week teaches you to analyze competitors, build strong competitive advantages, strategically adapt through pivots and iterations, track essential startup metrics, manage basic finances, and build a high-performing founding team. Week 4: Funding, Ownership & The Future 💸 This week guides you through evaluating startups, understanding different funding stages, learning how venture capital works, mastering the art of pitching, decoding ownership structures, and planning for your startup's financial sustainability and ultimate exit. Week 5: Workshops Various workshops ranging from learning to write cold emails and resumes, to public speaking, time management, and networking skills.
