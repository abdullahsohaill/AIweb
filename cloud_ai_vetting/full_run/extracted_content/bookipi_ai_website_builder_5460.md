### How do I see the performance metrics of my free AI website?

You can easily add Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to your website with your own GA4 Measurement ID snippet.
To set it up, simply paste your GA4 Measurement ID snippet onto your “Custom” tab -> “Custom code” section and we’ll do the rest.
Just follow the steps below:
- Sign in to your Google Analytics account or create a new one if you don’t have an existing account.
- Once you’re in, create a new property specifically for GA4.
- You’ll receive a Measurement ID, which you’ll need to copy.
- Navigate to your website’s “Custom” tab.
- Next, paste the GA4 Measurement ID snippet on the “Custom code” section.
- Save and publish the changes to your website.
- Finally, return to your Google Analytics account, navigate to the Admin section, and select your GA4 property.
- Verify that data is being received by checking the Realtime or Reporting sections.
- And done! GA4 is now successfully added to your website.  You can track and analyze valuable insights about your website’s performance and visitor behavior.
