## People often ask

### What does Promova mean anyway?

It’s a Ukrainian word that means “speech” or “pronunciation”.
Promova also breaks into two words, which turns it into “about language.” So, we’re all about languages! To learn more about us and our many learning tools, visit this page or contact us to say hi!

### How is Promova different from other language learning websites?

Promova offers much more than just a learning language website! You can:
- Learn new language skills with our app
- Find hundreds of helpful articles on our blog
- Improve your speaking with our AI role-play practice
We introduce handy new tools for learners all the time! With our help, you don’t have to go anywhere else to practice and fully immerse yourself in language study.

### Is the Promova language learning platform free?

Some of our online language courses are free and many core features are (and always will be!) free. However, if you’re serious about learning, consider Promova Premium to speed up your progress greatly.
Do what works for you, and remember that having fun is the most important part of success!

### How quickly can I become fluent?

It depends on your native language, background, and abilities. On average, it takes learners 500 hours of dedicated practice to master a language that shares the same roots and 1000+ to learn languages that don’t share the same culture and general rules.
It might not be a feel-good answer, but it’s true! Your success depends on the time and effort you put in, as well as the kind of help you’re getting. Some people can shave this time in half with dedication, language classes, and regular practice.
We suggest you focus on small daily tasks and bite-sized lessons with Promova to make steady progress. You’ll be surprised how far you make it in just a few months!

### How is the Promova approach better than traditional classes?

With Promova, you don’t squeeze your life around a class — your learning fits you. Here’s what you get:
- Your schedule, your pace. Learn anywhere on the web or app in short, focused sessions — no commuting, no fixed timetables.
- A path built around your goals. Take a quick level check, choose general or special courses, and follow a personalized plan with smart reviews.
- Native-like input. Modern vocabulary, accents, and everyday situations — not just textbook dialogs.
- Clear progress tracking. Daily goals, streaks, and a dashboard that shows exactly how you’re improving.
- All-in-one learning. Grammar, vocabulary, listening, reading, writing, and speaking — in one place. Traditional courses are one-size-fits-all. With a quick Promova login, you get access to your personalized, outcome-focused way to learn the same grammar and skills — but through topics you care about, with tools that keep you practicing and progressing.
