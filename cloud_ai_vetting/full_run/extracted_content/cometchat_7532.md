CometChat unifies user-to-user chat, voice and video calls, and user-facing AI agents in one platform — secure, compliant, and built to scale. With guardrails, insights, and notifications baked in, your teams ship faster and your users get the seamless experiences they expect.

### Moderation & Guardrails

AI-native, context-aware safety for both user content and agent responses - built-in, not bolted on. Includes visual rule engine, fallback handling, and multilingual/media support.

### Analytics & Insights

Track everything from conversation health to agent success. Out-of-the-box dashboards help you improve UX, tune logic, and measure ROI - without extra tooling.

### Notification Engine

Push, Email, and SMS alerts that respect user preferences and timing logic. Includes retries, quiet hours, frequency controls, and custom templates.
