## Why Choose Cherry Studio

Your all-in-one AI assistant platform, making AI accessible to everyone

### Multi-Model Freedom

Support 50+ AI providers including OpenAI, Claude, Gemini cloud models, plus local models via Ollama and LM Studio. One client for all your AI needs .

### 300+ Smart Assistants

300+ pre-configured assistants for writing, coding, learning, translation, marketing and more. No prompt engineering needed , ready to use instantly.

### Smart Document Handling

Work with text, images, Office docs, PDFs. WebDAV file management , Mermaid chart rendering, and syntax highlighting built-in.

### Productivity Tools

Global search , topic management, AI translation, drag-and-drop organization. Streamline your workflow effortlessly.

### Customizable Experience

Light/dark themes, transparent windows , full Markdown rendering. Beautiful interface, ready to use out of the box .

### Powerful Extensibility

Support for mini-programs and MCP servers . Extend functionality infinitely, build your personalized AI workspace.
