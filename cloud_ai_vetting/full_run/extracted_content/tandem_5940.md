## How Tandem works

Our AI medical scribe captures the conversation so you can give your full attention to your patient.
1. Start Tandem
Simply start Tandem on your computer or mobile, and you're all set. It works seamlessly for both in-person and virtual visits.
2. Your note is created
While you care for the patient, Tandem works in the background to draft your note.
3. Review and send
Review the note and send to your medical record system in one click. Then get back to your day.
