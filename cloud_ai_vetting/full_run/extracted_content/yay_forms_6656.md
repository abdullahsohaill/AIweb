## For your research team

## AI-Powered Insights

Research teams benefit from AI-generated insights that automatically analyze all survey responses, identifying trends and patterns across large datasets. This feature condenses complex information into clear, actionable summaries, allowing researchers to quickly understand broader findings. It saves time, reduces manual effort, and enhances decision-making by providing a comprehensive overview of collected data.

## Emotion and sentiment analysis

Emotion and sentiment analysis allows research teams to uncover deeper feelings behind responses. For example, if a survey on a new product feature receives negative feedback, this tool can reveal that the feedback is driven by frustration, indicating usability issues rather than dislike of the concept itself. This nuanced understanding helps teams prioritize improvements that address the real pain points, leading to more effective product adjustments and improved user satisfaction.

## Keyword extraction and text classification

Keyword extraction enables research teams to categorize responses based on common terms and phrases, revealing topics that matter most to respondents. For example, if many survey responses mention “usability,” “pricing,” or “support,” you can quickly identify which areas require focus. Text classification allows you to normalize open-ended answers into categories you define.

## Questions & answers

### Can I try Yay! Forms before paying?

Yes! You have 7 days to try it for free and keep your account if you like it. Otherwise, you can cancel it at any time.

### How to cancel if I don't like it?

You can cancel your account without contacting us. Simply log in, click your name at the top of the page, then select Security . From there, you’ll find the option to cancel your account.

### What happens when my plan reaches the responses limit for the month?

You will receive an email notification in advance when your account is approaching the response limit. To ensure your form continues receiving responses, you have two options:
1. Upgrade to a plan with a higher response limit.
2. Enable the “additional responses” add-on , allowing your form to keep receiving responses even after reaching the plan limit. In this case, an additional fee will be charged based on the number of extra responses and your selected plan.
For more details, check our pricing table .

### Can I use more than the 20k response limit per month on the "Professional" plan?

Yes! You can enable the “additional responses” add-on to continue receiving responses beyond your plan’s limit. You’ll be charged based on the number of extra responses received.

### Can I share access with other people like teammates or clients?

Yes! You can add as many teammates as you want and distribute them among their respective workspaces so they can only see and edit the forms of their department. For example, you can define that the marketing team has access to the "Marketing workspace," while the HR team can only see and edit the "HR workspace", well you got it ;).

### I already have several forms created in Typeform. Is there a way to migrate them to Yay! Forms?

Yes! Just contact our support and we will be happy to migrate the forms for you.
