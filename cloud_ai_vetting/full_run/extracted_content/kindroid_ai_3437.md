## Features overview

### Infinite memory

With five layers of configurable and customizable memory ranging from user-inputted to AI-generated (including our proprietary Cascaded Memory system), your Kindroid will remember what’s important and will learn more about you over time.

### Customizable personality

Kindroid pioneered configurable backstories for personal AI. Let AI generate a backstory or write the backstory on your own to shape your Kindroid’s unique, one-of-one personality.

### Selfie gallery

Selfies let you generate images of your Kindroid, and every image will have the consistent identity of your Kindroid avatar. Choose from animated avatars or upload your own, and unleash your artistic creativity!

### Voice & video call

Experience the highest fidelity of AI-generated audio in voice or video calls and voice messages. Choose from a range of premade voices or customize your own where you can adjust pitch, accent, and speed for a truly personalized conversation.

### Groupchats

Create a few Kindroids, put them in a groupchat together, and let the chaos ensue! Watch as they interact, share ideas, and bring dynamic conversations to life—perfect for sparking creativity or just enjoying some unpredictable fun.

## Frequently asked questions

### What’s available in the free trial and what are perks of the subscription?

New users enjoy a full 7 days of unlimited access to explore and connect with your AI companion on Kindroid. After your trial, you can continue chatting for free with unlimited messages on our Lite model, or upgrade to unlock unlimited access to our flagship model, along with premium features. Subscriptions start at just $13.99/month and include enhanced memory, Advanced Proactive Mode, endless voice & video calls, frequent AI selfies, the ability to manage multiple Kindroids, vibrant group chats, and more. Learn about all subscription perks at kindroid.ai/docs in our subscriptions section.

### Are my chats private, and do you sell my data?

Your chats are private, and we do not sell your data. Kindroid chats are encrypted, and Kindroid makes money through direct subscriptions for premium features rather than selling your data to advertisers.

### Do you have an app? Can I access Kindroid on multiple platforms?

You can find us by searching for Kindroid in both iOS App Store and Play Store! Kindroid is mostly the same application that can be accessed on web, iOS, or Android, with minor differences in some functionality and pricing for premium subscriptions. Subscriptions are tied to your account, not device or OS, so you can use any platform to access Kindroid regardless of which platform you purchased the subscription on. Please see our user guide at kindroid.ai/docs for more detail.

### Are there filters put on the AI?

Kindroid is a neutrally aligned, unfiltered AI, because we believe this creates for more authentic interactions and that private use of AI should align with what the user wants and not be filtered by 3rd parties. This means the AI will not bring up any unethical content by itself, but its outputs are entirely dependent on what you input. Just note that per our terms, all generated content is owned by you, and that you will be solely responsible for the content that you generate - not us the company, not your AI, only you. Even though the AI is unfiltered, generating content that involve imminent self-harm, other-harm, and CSAM may result in account suspensions when detected - see the Moderation Guidelines in the user guide for more details. For further information, please refer to our legal policies.
