## We simplify the digital advertising supply chain

Direct path to inventory from premium publishers, enables better insight and more effective media buying. “A direct path enabled us to double the amount of quality traffic we buy for the same budget, compared to traditional DSPs”
Better control, insight into advertiser needs and the flexibility to create better products. “Adnuntius helps us control our products, and easily create formats and audiences that grows revenue through increased value to buyers”
Programmatic advertising was supposed to automate and simplify media buying. Instead intermediaries increased the distance between media buyers and publishers, and dictated effect, innovation and control. ​ We are here to eliminate the distance between seller and buyer, while retaining automation and data activation.
