Verbal intelligence goes beyond capturing your words. Verbit deepens your understanding by providing actionable insights. We then apply those insights exactly where they’re needed to unlock the value of speech.

Our Generative AI technology, Gen.V™ , offers real-time insights on captured content within seconds. It makes your transcripts actionable and helps its users work, learn and share information more efficiently by generating quick summaries, keywords and more.
