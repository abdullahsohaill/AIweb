## The RoleCatcher Way In Action

Here’s how RoleCatcher takes you through a focused, high-quality application — from start to submit.

### Know If It’s a Fit — In Seconds

Without RoleCatcher, evaluating job relevance means reading every line of the job description. With instant keyword analysis , RoleCatcher's free Chrome plugin helps you highlight your strongest matches — so you can prioritize the right opportunities in seconds .
Use highlighted keywords to refine your search filters — or tailor your LinkedIn headline to match what employers are actually asking for.

### See What You’re Missing — Instantly

Without RoleCatcher, knowing which CV/Resume to use — and spotting what’s missing — means scanning every job spec manually . With keyword gap analysis, RoleCatcher pinpoints your best-matching CV/Resume and highlights missing terms instantly .
The more often a skill or term appears in a job description, the more likely it’s key to the role — and important for ATS ranking. Prioritize these when tailoring your CV/Resume.

### From Generic to Targeted — Fast

Without RoleCatcher, tailoring means rewriting your CV/Resume by hand , slotting in keywords, and risk introducing formatting errors or typos. With intelligent AI editing, RoleCatcher adjusts entire sections — or your full CV/Resume — to align to the job spec with precision and speed .
After tailoring your CV/Resume, always ask someone else to proofread it. Fresh eyes catch the formatting slips and typos that are easy to miss when you've been staring at the same text for hours.

### Get ATS-Ready — Before You Send

Without RoleCatcher, there's no way to know how your tailored CV/Resume will score in an Applicant Tracking System (ATS) — or if it’ll even be seen . With a detailed ATS assessment, RoleCatcher simulates recruiter scoring — giving you the same insight before you hit send, so you can make final adjustments with confidence .
Recruiters may only see CVs/Resumes that hit a minimum score — missing key terms can mean automatic rejection. Effort wasted.
