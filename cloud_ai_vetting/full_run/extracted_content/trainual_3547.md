## A 0.1% payroll investment that helps teams do more without adding people

## Here's how Trainual saves you money

The calculator estimates how much time and money your managers spend training employees and fixing avoidable mistakes. It combines national hiring and training benchmarks with your inputs — like how many people you’re hiring or promoting, how many managers help with training, and their average pay. From there, it calculates the hours typically spent onboarding, ramping, retraining, and correcting errors, and shows how much time, money, and team capacity you can reclaim by using Trainual’s AI-powered training and documentation to reduce manual work and speed up learning.
