## Copy, Right

A CLA Licence provides blanket permission, protecting you from the risk of legal action for copyright infringement, where your organisation copies from books, journals, magazines or websites. Our licences and technology give you the coverage and flexibility you need to share content within your organisation.
As a not-for-profit company, we distribute our revenues back into the creative industries helping to ensure that rightsholders are fairly remunerated when their works are used.

## Government regulated

The Copyright Licensing Agency is regulated by the UK government as the collective licensing body for the reuse of text and images from books, journals, and magazines.
