## FAQ

### What is Startup Fame?

Startup Fame is a platform for discovering new startups and promoting your own. Showcase your startup and gain high-authority do-follow links to boost SEO, increase visibility, and drive more traffic. Plus, you can quickly create your unique startupfa.me/profile to share all your startups with a single link.

### How do I get featured?

To get featured on Startup Fame, create a free account and add your startup in seconds. Just enter your website URL, and our AI will fetch your website to automatically generate your startup's details. Once your startup is added, simply select the promotion type that fits you best.

### Is it free?

Yes, you can be featured at not cost by verifying your startup. Just add a verification badge to your website’s homepage to start the review. For more visibility, you can upgrade to Highlight ($ 19 /month) or Spotlight ($ 149 /month). Compare all plans here .

### What types of startups are accepted?

We accept modern companies, micro-startups, and side projects. Each verified startup goes through multiple quality checks to maintain high standards for the platform and backlink value. It can take several business days to review and approve your startup.

### What is Domain Rating (DR)?

Domain Rating (DR), or Domain Authority (DA), is a metric that measures the authority of a website based on its backlink profile. It is calculated by analyzing the number and quality of backlinks from other websites. DR is a crucial factor in SEO, as it helps search engines understand the relevance and credibility of a website.

### What is a do-follow link?

A do-follow link is a link that search engines can follow and crawl, which helps improve the website's Domain Rating and search rankings. On the other hand, a no-follow link is a link that search engines ignore and do not follow.
