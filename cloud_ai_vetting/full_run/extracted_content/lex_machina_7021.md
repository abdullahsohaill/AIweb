## What is Lex Machina?

Lex Machina elevates legal decision-making with an unparalleled Legal Analytics ® platform to win cases and secure new business — now empowered by Protégé ™ . This proprietary technology and AI-assisted attorney review converts raw legal documents into comprehensive data sets, filling gaps in court records and delivering unique case insights you won’t find anywhere else.

## Generative Analytics

Experience intuitive, AI-powered assistance built on high-quality, structured Legal Analytics. With Protégé in Lex Machina, simply enter a prompt to quickly access complex data for actionable intelligence, smarter decisions and winning outcomes.

## Legal Analytics

Gain insights into the past behavior of parties, counsel, experts, judges, and courts to improve litigation and business outcomes. Lex Machina goes beyond the docket, revealing judge’s specific findings, awarded damages, case resolutions, involved parties, timelines, and much more.

## Comprehensive Court Database

Access insights from 45M customer-facing documents, across over ten million cases, involving 8K+ judges, 6K+ expert witnesses, with 146M+ counsel and 149M+ party mentions. Coverage includes all 94 federal district courts, 13 courts of appeal, PTAB, specialty venues, and various enhanced state courts. Party analytics for business intelligence benefits from an additional 18M state cases. *Based on data as of April 2025. Largest firms indicated by 2025 Law360 Pulse Leaderboard Report and 2025 AmLaw100 Report.

## Legal Entity Analytics

Experience enhanced legal insights with enriched case data, detailing law firms, attorneys, parties, and judges in each dispute. This proprietary AI analyzes a wide array of documents, including reading signature blocks, updating case data, and grouping related entities, offering a comprehensive view of timelines and relationships to better understand the connections and see the complete legal picture.

## Additional Features

### State Court Motion Metrics

Leverage document driven state court motion metrics, focused on orders to access motion practice and timing analytics for over 40+ motion types by judge and court to capture key outcomes and enhance judge analytics.

### Legal Findings

### Appeals Analytics

Explore detailed analytics, information, and documents for all civil cases filed in federal appellate courts since 2012.  Discover reversal rates revealing outcomes from re-hearings and U.S. Supreme Court decisions.

### Class Action Analytics

Use class action analytics to view settlement damages, attorney fees, and class representative awards. Summarize timelines to forecast litigation budgets and manage client expectations effectively.
