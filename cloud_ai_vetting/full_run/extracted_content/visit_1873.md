## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I use SPEECHMA audio for commercial purposes?

Yes! Every audio generated on SPEECHMA is completely free for commercial use. You can use it on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, podcasts, audiobooks, business presentations, or any other platform without worrying about copyright issues from us. When you create audio on SPEECHMA, you retain all rights to it. The only restrictions are that the audio should not be used to harm anyone or contain inappropriate content. For more details, check our Terms of Service.

### Is SPEECHMA really free? Are there any hidden costs?

SPEECHMA is completely free to use with no hidden charges, subscription fees, or paid plans. We don't require any registration, credit card information, or personal details to get started. You can access all 580+ voices and features immediately without any cost.

### Do I need to create an account or sign up?

No registration required! You can start using SPEECHMA immediately without creating any accounts, providing personal information, or going through signup processes. Simply visit the website and begin converting text to speech right away.

### What is the CAPTCHA and how do I use it?

The CAPTCHA is a security verification where you simply enter the 5-digit numbers from the image into the input box. Write your text, select a voice, enter the CAPTCHA code, and click Generate. Please note that the CAPTCHA expires every minute, so refresh it if you've spent time preparing your text. Click the refresh button next to the CAPTCHA image to get a new code if needed.

### How many voices and languages are available?

SPEECHMA offers 580+ premium AI voices across 75+ languages and dialects. Our collection includes diverse accents, gender options, and speaking styles to match any project requirement. You can preview each voice before making your selection.

### How can I add pauses to my generated speech?

You can add natural pauses using punctuation in your text: Comma (,) for short pauses (~0.5 seconds), Semicolon (;) for medium pauses (~1 second), and Exclamation mark (!) for longer pauses (~1.5 seconds). For custom pause durations longer than these options, you can download the MP3 files and use audio editing software like Audacity to manually insert pauses of your desired length.

### How do I customize voice settings?

Click on any voice to select it, then use the "Voice Effects" button next to the "Generate Audio" button to access customization options. You can adjust pitch, speed, and volume to fine-tune the voice to your preferences. The platform also includes preset pause buttons for easy punctuation insertion.

### What is the character limit for text input?

Each conversion supports up to 2000 characters. For longer content, simply split your text into multiple sections and generate them separately - you can do this almost unlimited times at no cost. This limit helps ensure optimal performance and prevents system abuse while still accommodating most use cases.

### How do I download my generated audio?

Simply click the download button after generating your audio. Files are saved as high-quality MP3 format to your browser's default download location (usually the "Downloads" folder). You can access downloaded files through your browser's download section (Ctrl+J on most browsers) or your device's file manager.

### How are audio files stored on the platform?

Audio files are securely stored in your browser's local storage for privacy and quick access. They remain available until you clear your browser data or manually delete them. For permanent storage, we recommend downloading your audio files to your device.

### Is voice cloning available?

Voice cloning is not currently available but is on our development roadmap. We're working to make SPEECHMA even more versatile and will announce this feature as soon as it becomes available. Stay tuned for updates!

### Does SPEECHMA provide an API?

The API feature is currently in development and may take several months for the final launch. We're focused on perfecting the core platform experience first, then will expand to API access for developers and businesses.
