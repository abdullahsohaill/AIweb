## The AI Assistant for financial advisors

Trusted by thousands of advisors and firms of every size

## You need to spend time with clients, but you’re stuck doing admin work

## Zocks saves financial advisors like you 10+ hours a week by automating your admin work

### Focus on your client while Zocks takes notes

Zocks automatically preps you for every meeting, takes detailed notes, updates your CRM & financial planning tool, and writes a follow up email.

### Instantly fill out forms like client intake and account openings

Zocks automatically fills out intake, fact finder, account forms and more, so you can close more prospects and provide a better client experience.

### Reply to client emails in seconds

Zocks instantly writes detailed replies to client emails so you can give clients fast, personal service.

### Get detailed reporting on your client conversations

Zocks shows you which topics have the most impact on your firm, helps you spend time with the right clients, improves your meetings & more.
