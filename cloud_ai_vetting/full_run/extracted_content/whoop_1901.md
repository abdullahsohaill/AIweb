Advanced Labs testing is not available in Arizona, Hawaii, Wyoming, North Dakota, or South Dakota; however, tests may be completed out of state. If you are within 20 weeks postpartum, weigh under 100 lbs (45 kg), have severe anemia (Hb < 8 g/dL), or have recently undergone surgery, we recommend waiting until you have been cleared by your clinician before testing.
