## Sunsetting the product

To our community,
We’ve loved building Hatch Canvas with you this past year. On January 23, 2026 we announced that we’re sunsetting Hatch Canvas. All Hatch Canvas projects will be officially shut down on Friday, February 27, 2026 .
Here’s what you need to know:
- Credits you currently have or purchase must be used before February 27.
- Projects will remain fully functional until February 27 and then appear as a 404 page.
- You have until February 27 to export your project assets through the canvas download options.
- Monthly subscriptions will not renew.
If you have questions, please reach out to us through support@hatchcanvas.com .
