## AI Agents for Everyone

Drive digital transformation with Laiye's digital worker solutions.
From rule-based Robotic Process Automation (RPA), to the combination of RPA and AI, and now to AI Agents, automation has entered a new era. The fusion of automation and AI inspires us to build a pattern of software - Digital Workers . They can operate complex systems, process diverse types of data, and collaborate smoothly with human workers.
Laiye's Work Execution Platform (WEP) is designed to enable governments and enterprises to build digital workforces powered by LLMs. Bridging the work execution gap at individual and organization levels, Laiye helps accelerate the digital transformation from robotic automation to intelligent automation with AI Agents.
