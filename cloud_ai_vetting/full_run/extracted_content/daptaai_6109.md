## You have questions? We have answers

### 1. How do credits work in Dapta?

Credits in Dapta power features like AI Calls, text agents, and chatbots. Each interaction, whether it’s a call, a chat message, or content generation, uses a small amount of credits. You can monitor your credit usage in real-time and easily add more to keep your workflows running smoothly.

### 2. Can Dapta integrate with my tools?

Absolutely! Dapta integrates with over 300 tools natively and can also connect to your favorite APIs. Whether it’s your CRM, email marketing platform, or project management tool, Dapta helps you build seamless workflows effortlessly.

### 3. How can I reach customer support?

We’re here for you 24/7! Contact us anytime via support@daptatech.com or use the live chat on our website. Our team is ready to help with anything you need.

### 4. What are AI Calls, and how do they work?

AI Calls are automated, AI-driven phone calls that handle tasks like lead qualification, customer follow-ups, and prospecting. These calls sound natural, engage your audience effectively, and help save your team hours of manual outreach.

### 5. What can the Dapta Chatbot do for my business?

The Dapta Chatbot is an AI-powered assistant that engages customers 24/7. It can answer frequently asked questions, collect leads, and guide users through your sales funnel. Its advanced conversational abilities ensure human-like interactions that leave a great impression.
