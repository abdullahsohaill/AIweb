## 5. How does publisher ad management help increase revenue?

Publisher ad management consolidates inventory control, optimises ad placements,and connects you with premium ad networks and exchanges, which maximizes yield on every impression.

## 6. What’s the difference between ad networks and ad exchanges?

Ad networks aggregate and channel inventory to sell to advertisers, while ad exchanges allow for real-time bidding between a buyer and seller, and are generally more transparent and offer greater control.

## 7. How can digital advertising management improve performance?

Digital advertising management allows publishers to analyze performance metrics, optimize targeting, and automate delivery for higher ROI and a better user experience.
