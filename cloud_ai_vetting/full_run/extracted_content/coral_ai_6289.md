## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can I use this?

You can use Ivory Mind to search and understand all of your PDFs. Ask questions, get citations, find information, and more in seconds.

### What questions should I ask?

You can ask whatever you want! We also have a library of questions and prompts to give you inspiration and get you started on our Tutorials page.

### Does Ivory Mind work in other languages?

Yes, Ivory Mind works in 90+ languages for documents including Spanish, Chinese, Hindi, French, Arabic, German, Portuguese, and more. You can upload documents written in different languages, ask questions in different languages, and translate between different languages.

For audio transcription, Ivory Mind works in English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Afrikaans, Albanian, Amharic, Arabic, Armenian, Assamese, Azerbaijani, Bashkir, Basque, Belarusian, Bengali, Bosnian, Breton, Bulgarian, Burmese, Catalan, Chinese, Croatian, Czech, Danish, Estonian, Faroese, Finnish, Galician, Georgian, Greek, Gujarati, Haitian, Hausa, Hawaiian, Hebrew, Hindi, Hungarian, Icelandic, Indonesian, Japanese, Javanese, Kannada, Kazakh, Khmer, Korean, Lao, Latin, Latvian, Lingala, Lithuanian, Luxembourgish, Macedonian, Malagasy, Malay, Malayalam, Maltese, Maori, Marathi, Mongolian, Nepali, Norwegian, Norwegian Nynorsk, Occitan, Panjabi, Pashto, Persian, Polish, Romanian, Russian, Sanskrit, Serbian, Shona, Sindhi, Sinhala, Slovak, Slovenian, Somali, Sundanese, Swahili, Swedish, Tagalog, Tajik, Tamil, Tatar, Telugu, Thai, Tibetan, Turkish, Turkmen, Ukrainian, Urdu, Uzbek, Vietnamese, Welsh, Yiddish, Yoruba.

### Will it give correct answers?

Ivory Mind works by giving you answers based on the document that you upload. For example, if you upload a legal document, our AI uses information from that legal document to answer your questions.

The responses will always include the page number of the document that the AI referenced when creating the response. You can double check the information in the responses with the page number.

### Can I upload different file types?

Yes! Ivory Mind supports many file types, including:
- Word documents (.doc, .docx)
- PDFs (.pdf)
- PowerPoint presentations (.ppt, .pptx)
- Ebooks (.epub, .mobi, Kindle files)
- Text files (.txt, .rtf)
- Markdown files (.md)
- Websites (.html)
- Audio and video files (.mp3, .mp4, .mp2, .aac, .wav, .flac, .pcm, .m4a, .ogg, .opus, .webm, and more)

And many more file types like .tex, .pages, .key, .abw, .odt, .odp, .djvu, etc.

If you have a scanned PDF, we also have OCR (it even works on handwriting!)

If you’d like to upload another format, please let us know.

### Can I chat with multiple documents at the same time?

Yes. Just upload all of the documents you want to chat with to your Ivory Mind Dashboard then click "Chat All." Type in your question or prompt, and Ivory Mind will search all of your documents to give you an answer with page citations.

### Can I create content with Ivory Mind?

You can use Ivory Mind to write blog posts, Instagram captions, tweets, LinkedIn posts, website copy, and everything in-between.
