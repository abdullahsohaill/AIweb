## Put production-grade analytics into your app without drama

Embed dashboards, visualizations, or self-serve reporting in your SaaS app. Choose iframes for speed or the React SDK for customization and control.
- Way less engineering overhead than rolling your own in-app reporting – Empower data teams (or any non-dev teammates) to manage permissions, iterate on dashboards, and refine reports—without bugging devs.
- Scales with you – Start with simple charts, embed full dashboards, or use the SDK for advanced setups as your needs grow.
- Customizable to fit your product – White-labeling, dynamic styling, and interactive controls from view-only to full data discovery.

### Keeps up as you grow

Advanced features for managing lots of users and compliance. From pre-seed to post-IPO.
Multi-tenant data segregation
Granular permissions to make sure people see what they need to, and nothing else.
Secure and simple access
Integrate with SSO IdPs (SAML, LDAP, JWT, Google) and map to Metabase groups.
Drop into SQL
The visual query builder gets you pretty far. When you need an escape hatch into SQL to run raw queries, tweak logic, or join tables, you're covered.
Track data and dashboard usage
No more unused dashboards . Inspect user behavior across content, data access and downloads.
Keep dashboards snappy
Go faster with result and model caching . No schedulers or pipelines needed.
[LINK: result and model caching](https://www.metabase.com/docs/latest/configuring-metabase/caching)
Test and deploy safely
Spin up staging environments without touching prod. Export configs, models, and dashboards to test or scale across multiple instances.
[LINK: staging environments](https://www.metabase.com/docs/latest/installation-and-operation/development-instance)
