## How long does it take to generate the animation?

Generation speed depends on the input video length, for Video-To-Video generation it takes 1 min for every 1s of content, so a 10s clip will take 10 mins to generate

## How do i get good results?

To generate good results you need to describe the most important aspects of the subject of your video, for example, their cloths and facial features, you also need to add a brief description of the background to help keep it consistent, and then generate a preview image to make sure you get the results you want.
