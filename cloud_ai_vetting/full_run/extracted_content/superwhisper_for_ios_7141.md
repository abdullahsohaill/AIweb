### Works offline

Superwhisper works offline, so you can transcribe anytime. No Wi-Fi, no problem.

### Use your own words

Enter names, abbreviations, and specialized terms once. Superwhisper remembers forever.

### Predefined modes

Predefined modes optimize tone, structure, and formatting. So your text is always right, right away.

### Multilanguage support

Superwhisper supports 100+ languages & dialects. It can also translate them all to English.

### Clipboard integration

No need to copy and paste. Your edited transcript is automatically pasted in the app you're using.
