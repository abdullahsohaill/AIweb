### Is Podwise free?

Yes, Podwise offers a free plan. Registered users can browse 4 episodes per month for free. You can upgrade to a paid plan if you need more features, such as unlimited access to AI-enhanced episodes, running AI on 20 episodes each month, and saving to note-taking software.

### What languages does Podwise support?

Podwise currently supports the following languages: English, French, Spanish, Portuguese, German, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Japanese and Korean. We are constantly adding support for other languages.

### Does Podwise support mobile devices?

Yes, Podwise supports mobile devices through responsive design and mobile apps. You can access Podwise on your mobile browser and enjoy a near-native experience through PWA (Progressive Web App). If you prefer using an app, our iOS app is available on the App Store, and the Android app can be downloaded from our official website.

### What if I need more AI processing requests?

If you need more AI processing requests on your Podwise Pro plan, you can purchase additional credit through the Podwise website. Any purchased AI credit will not expire, but it must be used within the subscription period.
