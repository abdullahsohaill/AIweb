## What is VRtuos?

Just calibrate your real piano and press play! Notes will fall directly onto your keys, so it's really easy to learn how to play any song pretty fast. There are many songs included, but you can also import your own .mid files.
You can also connect your midi piano using the Companion app from https://vrtuos.eu . If you do that, you will unlock two more modes and you will see keys press down.
