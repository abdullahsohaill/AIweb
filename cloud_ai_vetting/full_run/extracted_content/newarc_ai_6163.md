## Everything you need, all in one place

A complete suite of AI-powered fashion design tools to explore, refine, and present your designs without switching platforms.

### Sketch-to-Image

Whether it's a hand-drawn sketch, a technical drawing from Adobe Illustrator, a fashion illustration, or a collage, NewArc transforms it into a photo in seconds. Use it to explore ideas or to create polished visuals for presentations and marketing goals.

### Virtual Try-On

Let a model wear your design with a simple prompt. NewArc shows your product worn on a person in any setting you describe. The model can be either AI-generated, or a real model that you work with. No photoshoot is required.

### Image-to-Video

Bring your visuals to life. Upload an image, choose the video type (e.g. spin) and NewArc would generate a smooth, realistic animation in seconds.

### Image-to-Sketch

Convert product photos into clean technical sketches ready for your tech pack. Whether it's a product in isolation or worn on body, NewArc extracts the design and translates it into a clear outline ideal for production and documentation.

### Materials

Apply real-world fabrics and textures directly to your designs. Upload a sketch along with a photo of your desired material and explore fabrics, refine design details, and present visuals in a style that is inherent to your brand.

### Patterns

Overlay repeating patterns onto your clothing designs with pixel-level precision. Adjust scale, brightness, and rotation to get the exact look you want on any part of your design using NewArc's masking tool.

### Colorize

Quickly adjust colors of your entire product or specific parts. Fine-tune generated images or build colorways with precision using a color picker, eyedropper, or HEX code. Explore variations or get the exact shade you need.
