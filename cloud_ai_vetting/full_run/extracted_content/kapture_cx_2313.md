## Everything You Need to Fully Automate Customer Support in One Platform

Don’t just take our word for it. We’ll let the product do the talking.

### AI Agents Trained on 1B+ Minutes of Real Conversations

Put AI Agents in front of every channel – customers get instant, accurate help; humans only step in when it matters.
Low-latency voice agents that resolve routine calls, verify identity, and complete requests for both inbound and outbound.
Automate fast, accurate, empathetic support on Email, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and more—with smooth human handoff.
Trace every agent interaction end-to-end, watch live outcomes, and step in when needed—all in one view.

### Give Agents Superpowers, Not More Tabs

A unified console with AI Agents and automation – shrinks handle time, lifts FCR, and kills busywork.
Auto-classify, dedupe, and route with intents and SLAs; Customer 360 keeps context so queues move fast.
Real-time guidance, next-best actions, one-click knowledge, and workflow automation to resolve accurately—faster.
Real-time dashboards and drill-downs that tie every interaction to outcomes and reveal where to improve next.

### See What’s Working. Fix What Isn’t.

Quality, intelligence, and feedback that turn conversations into product and process improvements
Score 100% of interactions for accuracy, compliance, and empathy—then auto-coach with evidence.
Surface intents, broken journeys, and product issues to understand customers and prioritize what to automate next.
Trigger contextual surveys, summarize verbatims, and auto-open tasks to close the loop.

### Support Your Team Like You Support Your Customers

AI Agents and a modern service desk that resolve HR and IT requests fast – without Slack-swivel.
Answers policies, resets access, kicks off workflows, and drafts docs—so teams move faster and focus deeper.
Unify internal support across HR/IT with smart intake, routing, self-service, and rapid resolutions to keep employees productive.
One hub for chats, files, and tasks; collaborate naturally, automate the busywork, and stay locked on high-impact work.

### Agentic AI

Trained on real human conversations, our AI Agents understand nuance and empathy – so they connect, decide, and act with accuracy.
