## SuperSplat

SuperSplat provides a powerful and lightweight visual editing environment that runs in the browser. There is nothing to download and install!
SuperSplat is completely free! It is open sourced on GitHub under the MIT license.
[LINK: Fork on GitHub](https://github.com/playcanvas/supersplat)
SuperSplat operates on the industry standard PLY file format. Use it with any engine you like!
Powered by the PlayCanvas Engine, SuperSplat can handle even the heaviest 3D Gaussian Splat scenes with ease.

## Splat Editing Made Easy

3D Gaussian Splatting is an exciting new technique to create photorealistic 3D scenes from photogrammetry. However, a captured 3D Gaussian Splat may sometimes require some editing. This is where SuperSplat comes in!
- Easily select specific splats for deletion
- Translate and rotate your scene
- Save to PLY, compressed PLY or SPLAT formats
