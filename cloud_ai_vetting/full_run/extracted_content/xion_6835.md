## MARKETING

## GAMING

Bots, AI and exploits disrupt fair play across 3.3 billion gamers worldwide. XION launched XION Arena, the first un-bottable game, with a 1-billion-Won (~$750K) prize to prove verifiable play. It enables fair economies, human-only participation and provenance for in-game assets across titles.

## LOYALTY

Global airlines, hotels and ride-share platforms are piloting proof-based loyalty on XION to enable status matching, portable points, power-user rewards and verifiable redemption. This reduces fraud, prevents unused value, and increases retention across brands without relying on third-party data or locking customers inside closed loyalty ecosystems.

## SOCIAL MEDIA

15% of accounts (roughly 775 million profiles) are bots or fake, and 60% of viral content is AI-generated. XION enables platforms to ensure real people, authentic media, and verifiable provenance, restoring trust and safety for the world’s 5.17 billion social media users.

## RECRUITMENT

30% of resumes are AI-generated and 65% contain false information. XION enables employers to verify real applicants and past credentials instantly without exposing private data, lowering verification costs, reducing fraud, and shortening time-to-hire while filtering AI spam before it ever enters the hiring funnel.
