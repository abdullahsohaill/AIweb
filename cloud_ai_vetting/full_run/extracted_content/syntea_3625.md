## Innovate how you learn

Syntea is here to make your studies easier. Our custom AI study assistant is like having a personal tutor ready to help you ace your courses.

### What is Syntea?

Syntea is an AI-powered chatbot, tailored to fit your unique learning needs. It was developed by IU International University of Applied Sciences specifically for our students, and it’s available in all of our courses for the on-campus programmes.
Unlike other AI tools (like ChatGPT, Gemini or Claude), Syntea is specifically trained in our course materials, so it offers information that is guaranteed to help you in your studies.

## Why you should study with AI

### Personalised tutoring

Syntea adapts to your individual learning style, providing custom feedback and guidance.

### 24/7 availability

No matter the time, Syntea is always ready to help with your coursework and questions.

## What you get

### The Q&A feature

Ready when you are Ask Syntea any course-related question, any time of day – and get an immediate answer.
Answers you can trust The answers given are verified and monitored regularly by our teaching staff.
As detailed as you like Syntea will refer you to the relevant coursebook section that covers your question.

### The Pre-Assessment feature

Save time Take a pre-assessment of your knowledge before you start a course, and get a personalised learning plan.
Focus on what’s important Plan your studies based on Syntea’s plan, and complete the course faster – by covering only what’s new for you.
Track your progress Use this feature throughout your studies to get feedback on how well you’re advancing.

### The Deep Dialogue Learning feature

Build mastery Dive into a conversation with Syntea about any topic from the course you want to cover in depth.
Tackle challenging concepts Sometimes, the best way to understand something complex is to talk about it with a friend—or your AI study assistant.
Develop your problem-solving skills Syntea will guide you to figure out things independently, by offering questions of its own.

### The Exam Trainer feature

Methodical preparation This feature tests your exam preparation by quizzing you on each unit in the coursebook.
Personalised training You can choose to go by order or pick one unit at a time, depending on what you want to focus on.
Boost your confidence With the Exam Trainer, you can test your knowledge again and again, until you’re 100% ready to take the final exam.
