### What is Kommunity?

Kommunity is a platform that helps you organize events, grow your audience, and activate your community with powerful tools designed for modern builders.

### Who is Kommunity for?

It’s built for anyone creating a community—whether you’re running a tech meetup, a local club, a university group, or an online network.

### Do I need to be a developer to use it?

Not at all. Kommunity is designed to be simple and intuitive, with no technical background required.

### What can I do with Kommunity?

You can create events, collect feedback with forms, grow your email list, match members, share real-time content with your community, and offer exclusive content to supporters based on different subscription tiers.

### How does Kommunity help me grow my community?

With tools like smart forms, personalized invites, and community analytics, you can reach the right people and keep them engaged.

### Is Kommunity free to use?

Yes, it’s free to start. You can run events and build your community at no cost. Advanced features are available with paid plans.

### Can I customize my community page?

Yes! You can design your community space, add your branding, and create a unique experience for your members.

### How do events work on Kommunity?

You can publish online or offline events, manage RSVPs, send reminders, and even track engagement after the event.

### How is Kommunity different from other platforms?

Unlike generic tools, Kommunity is purpose-built for community growth—everything is focused on helping you build real relationships.

### What kind of communities use Kommunity today?

From developer groups to universities, non-profits to startups—hundreds of communities across different fields use Kommunity every day.
