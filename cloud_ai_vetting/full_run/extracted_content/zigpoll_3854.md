### Frequently Asked Questions

- What is Zigpoll Zigpoll is an contextual micro-survey tool. The idea is that you create small interactive surveys that show up where and when you want them on your website. The data you gather from surveys polls can then be used for lots of different things, like making your business better, increasing your conversion rate, and generating new leads.
Zigpoll is an contextual micro-survey tool. The idea is that you create small interactive surveys that show up where and when you want them on your website. The data you gather from surveys polls can then be used for lots of different things, like making your business better, increasing your conversion rate, and generating new leads.
- Can I have multiple surveys on my website? Yes. You can have as many surveys as you want on your website. You can only show one survey per page though.
