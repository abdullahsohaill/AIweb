## Watercolour Leaves

Our Watercolour Leaves fabric is one of our bestsellers, loved for its fresh, painterly design and timeless appeal. Available in two versatile weights - Curtain and Upholstery – this fabric can be tailored to suit every room in your home. The softer curtain weight drapes beautifully for curtains and blinds, while the durable upholstery weight brings style and resilience to sofas, chairs, and cushions. With a wide range of colourways to choose from, Watercolour Leaves adapts seamlessly to different interiors – whether you’re styling a calm, nature-inspired space or adding a touch of contemporary pattern to a modern home. Its hand-painted leaf design offers the perfect balance of elegance and character, making it a true statement fabric for everyday living.

From reimagined heirlooms to made-to-order sofas, custom upholstery sits at the sweet spot between craftsmanship, creativity, and individuality.
