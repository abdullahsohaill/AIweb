## Frequently asked questions

### What kind of writing is Type best for?

Type excels at long-form professional writing: books, novels, reports, technical documents, essays, long-form marketing copy, or any type of writing that requires careful crafting, editing, and review.

### Does Type train on my data?

No, Type does not train any AI models on your data. All of your uploads and documents in Type remain private to you. You can read Type's full privacy policy here .

### What exactly is Type?

Type.ai (aka "Type") is the most popular alternative to ChatGPT and Claude for long-form writing tasks that require a more involved editing process. It has a feature-rich document editor with deeply-embedded AI features for generating, revising, and reviewing documents. You'll also find tools for easily organizing your work.

### Can I edit an entire book in Type?

Yes, you can edit an entire book or novel in Type. Type's editing and writing features are designed to work on documents as long as 130,000 words. For manuscripts longer than 130k words, we recommend breaking it up into multiple documents.

### Can I edit and generate word documents in Type?

Yes, you can import and edit word documents and PDFs inside of Type's editor. You can also use Type's AI to generate entire documents and export them as Word docs, PDFs, and AI narrated audio files.
