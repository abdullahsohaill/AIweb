## Questions

### "How is this different from Duolingo?"

Duolingo trains recognition (picking the right answer). We train production (saying French out loud with AI feedback). You'll know exactly how you sound before talking to a real French person.

### "Is copying really how languages are learned?"

Yes. Babies learn language exclusively through imitation for 2-3 years before they create original sentences. Research confirms this is the most natural path to fluency.

### "What if I'm embarrassed to speak?"

You're talking to an app, not a judgmental person. Make mistakes with Copy, not with a French waiter.
