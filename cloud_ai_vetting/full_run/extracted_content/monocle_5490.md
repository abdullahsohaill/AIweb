## Reasoning That Drives Performance

Monocle personalizes the strategy for each of your subscribers. Our AI agents make real-time decisions for each customer based on behavior, intent and predicted outcomes.

### Engage each subscriber when it matters.

Monocle leverages real-time behavioral signals, going beyond RFM or static scores, to time each email or journey perfectly - increasing conversions while keeping audiences engaged.

### Not everyone needs the same number of touches.

Some subscribers need one message. Others need five. Monocle decides how many touches to send based on each person’s intent and likelihood to engage.

### Send the message each subscriber is most likely to respond to.

Monocle selects from your existing email/SMS templates - matching messaging and product content to each subscriber’s interests, affinities, and past behavior.

### Only offer discounts where they’re needed.

Monocle identifies when a product-led message will convert - and when an incentive is justified - and selects the minimum effective offer to both convert and protect margin.
