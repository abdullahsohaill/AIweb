What is a residential proxy?
A residential proxy provides the feature to select a desired location (e.g., country, state, city) and enables its users to gather web data as if they were physically located in that region. Residential proxies act as a buffer to safeguard your online activities while simultaneously hiding your real IP address. In essence, residential proxies provide our users with a stand-in IP address that we lease out, resulting in a higher level of anonymity and flexibility when it comes to web data collection.
What are the benefits of using residential proxies?
Residential IPs are associated with a physical location, therefore, they appear more genuine and legitimate, helping you minimize the number of IP bans and CAPTCHAs. Additionally, residential proxies cover more geographic locations, so you can easily access geo-specific content at a country or city level.
Do static residential proxies support subscription renewal for an order?
Yes, you can renew your static residential proxy before its expiration, and thus keep the same IP address. A new duration from your plan level is added to the existing expiration date. For example, if you have a 30-day IP in your account, and on the 30th day you renew it with a 30-day plan, your IP address will remain for another 30 days.
What's the difference between the Enterprise Plan and the Regular Plan for Residential Proxies?
The enterprise plan allows you to purchase residential proxies at a more favorable price, with access to an independent management dashboard and support for CDKey distribution. Unallocated proxies remain permanently valid in your enterprise plan account. For details, please visit the Enterprise Plan page.
Are there any limitations when using Unlimited Proxies?
Our Unlimited Proxies have absolutely no limitations on bandwidth and IPs. However, you won't be able to choose your proxy locations though, and we will randomly allocate available IP addresses to your requests. This randomness provides for a broader coverage and can be better for anonymity in certain use cases.
Can I purchase proxies for a specific city?
When it comes to Residential Proxies, you get access to our full proxy pool and can choose to generate a list of one or multiple proxies from a desired location and can even narrow it down to a state within the country.
Should you wish to use IPs from a particular ISP, please contact us via the inquiry button on our Static Residential Proxy Pricing page and we will put your request into consideration and should the city be available, we will process your order manually.
What type of authentication do your proxies support?
Our Residential Proxies, Unlimited Proxies and SOCKS5 Proxies support both username/password and IP Whitelisting authentication methods. Static Residential Proxies support the username/password authentication method only.
