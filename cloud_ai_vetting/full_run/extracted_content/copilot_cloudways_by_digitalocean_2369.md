## How AI-Powered Insights Work

Cloudways Copilot  helps you troubleshoot, identify and resolve issues
impacting server workflows, that let you focus on what matters the
most – managing your core business
Our AI monitors your web stack, disk, inodes, and host health
24/7, quickly detecting  problems that can affect the performance
of your applications and servers. The moment an issue is detected,
you receive an alert notifying you of the problem.
Within seconds of detection, our advanced AI performs a rapid
analysis to determine the root cause of the problem. This near
instant diagnosis can save you hours in troubleshooting.
Based on the diagnosis, you receive clear, actionable insights
through email and via platform notifications. Each notification
includes specific steps to help resolve the issue, helping you
recover optimal server performance, with less effort.
