## FLock Co-Creation Platforms: Unlock DeAI Potential

FLock.io is a decentralised AI training platform that lets enterprises and communities build privacy-preserving AI models without moving or exposing their data. Combining federated learning with blockchain-based verification, we provides a unified, trust-minimised infrastructure for secure training, evaluation, and deployment.
The core modules include AI Arena for decentralized model training, FL Alliance for collaborative federated learning, the API Platform for deploying FLock-trained models into real applications, and Moonbase-FOMO for enabling real model assets and driving ecosystem growth.

### Trained and Validated

Upon a task creation, the model is first trained and validated in AI Arena, a blockchain-based decentralised training platform.

### Further Finetuning

Upon a task creation, the model is optionally further fine-tuned in FL Alliance using participants' local data.

### Use and Feedback

Finally, the model is deployed by applications in the AI Moonbase, where feedback will be used to further improve the model.

## Why Privacy Powers Everything FLock Builds?

### Making Decentralised AI Truly Private and Usable

Enable organisations/communities keep their data local, maintain fair value distribution, and launch community-driven AI models that can be deployed and monetised globally.

### Built for Privacy, Security, and Scale from Day 1

Secure high-value training with advanced Federated Learning and LoRA-based optimisation, reinforced by a proven on-chain incentive system that ensures resilience, accuracy, and fair rewards across the network.

### The Foundation Chosen by World-Class Institutions and Partners

Collaborate with the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), Alibaba Cloud’s Qwen, and the Ethereum Foundation, providing a privacy-preserving infrastructure that meets real-world security and compliance needs.
