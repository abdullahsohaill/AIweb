### Is it okay to use AI to write my resume?

Yes, using AI to write your resume is completely acceptable. resume-example.com helps organize your experience, enhance wording, and optimize your resume while ensuring your information remains accurate and professional.

### Is it okay to put an AI-generated photo on my resume?

Using an AI-generated photo is acceptable, particularly in regions where resume photos are commonly expected. The photo should appear professional, natural, and appropriate for your industry and location.

### Should my resume have one column or two columns?

Both one-column and two-column resume formats are widely accepted. One-column resumes provide a clear and simple structure, while two-column layouts help present skills and key information more effectively. The best option depends on your experience and the role you are applying for.
