### What is Maths-Whizz?

- Maths-Whizz is the world's leading AI enabled virtual maths tutor
Maths-Whizz has helped teachers and parents worldwide to achieve measurable learning gains at scale by guiding students along a tailored learning journey, continually adapting to their needs and pace of learning.
The tutor begins by diagnosing each student’s knowledge gaps across several topics, and then automatically develops a unique learning journey to address those gaps.
Through engaging lessons and continuous assessment, the tutor tracks students’ progress, maintaining an up-to-date understanding of their needs and providing the appropriate support at all times.

### Identify learning gaps

Initial assessment reveals your child's learning profile. Virtual tutor then delivers and continuously adapts learning to your child's individual needs.
