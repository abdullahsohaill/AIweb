## AI prototyping for teams, not just solo heroes

Prototyping with Supernova means you can think out loud, explore without breaking things, and turn good ideas into great ones. All in one collaborative space.

### Work in parallel with threads

Branch and experiment freely with threads. Each path stays isolated, so your team can explore bold ideas side-by-side without conflicts.

### Scale with shared AI configuration

Project contexts keep every prototype aligned with your design system, themes, and documentation. Everyone can then build with the product foundations connected.

### Consistent prototyping at scale

Import your Figma variables and themes seamlessly. AI prototypes automatically inherit your design tokens, ensuring every experience feels like part of your actual product.
Ensure every prototype looks like your product by defining themes once and applying them across all projects.
Save time with pre-built libraries of buttons, inputs, and layouts. Focus on your ideas, not the setup.
Choose from a variety of icon sets to match your brand style and keep prototypes visually consistent.

## Connected to your design system

When AI understands your components, patterns, and guidelines, it doesn’t just build prototypes — it makes them truly on brand.

### Prototypes that know your brand

AI prototyping powered by your design tokens and themes, automatically maintaining your brand standards.

### Document design guidelines

Build a single source of truth for design standards that your team and AI both understand and follow.

### Ask AI anything about your design system

Ask AI about your design system and get immediate answers, so you can build with confidence instead of hunting through documentation.

## Product documents and explorations, always in sync

Convert prototypes into actionable PRDs and specifications, giving PMs, designers, and engineers a clear path forward.

### Product specs that update themselves

Close the gap between what you create and what you write. AI keeps your prototypes and product documents synchronized, saving hours each day.

### Push code directly to development

Bridge the design-to-dev gap. Push code from prototypes directly into Cursor, VS Code, and other development tools.

### Get instant feedback from your colleagues

Smart permissions for every stakeholder. Give the right access level—from viewing to building—with one-click invites.
