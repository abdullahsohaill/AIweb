The Problem
Growing agencies struggle with this every day
You find out too late when rankings drop or critical issues hit your clients’ sites
Search Console and GA4 native interfaces have bad UX and limited features
Looker Studio isn’t designed to work with dozens of websites and team members
SEMrush and Ahrefs rely on modeled clickstream data, not your clients’ actual traffic
Adding users and websites in SEMrush or Ahrefs quickly drives up your costs
The Solution
One platform that covers what others miss
Proactive alerts across Search Console and GA4 metrics, site health and content changes
Spot issues and ranking drops before your clients do with instant alerts that combine Search Console, Google Analytics, site health and content changes data.
One unified dashboard for all client sites
Monitor performance across dozens of websites without logging into multiple tools or managing messy Looker Studio dashboards.
Data analysis that goes beyond Google Search Console limitations
Export more than 1,000 keywords and pages. Use additional metrics like Ranked Pages and Ranked keywords. Apply multi-condition filters by dimensions and metrics.
Agency-ready SEO reporting in minutes
Share clean, customizable reports your clients will actually understand. White label the entire app if needed.
Simple, scalable pricing. No extra fees for users
Add unlimited users to every project. Request a custom plan based on the amount of websites and keywords. Sitechecker grows with your agency, not against it.
