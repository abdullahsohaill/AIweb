### Features

Our mass email service can break Gmail's sending limits using our distribution technology.
GMass will read data live from any Google Sheet and even monitor for new rows and then send emails automatically.
Campaign level reports that show how many people opened, clicked, replied, and bounced. See reports inside Gmail on your desktop or the Gmail app on your phone.
