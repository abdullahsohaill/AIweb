## BridgeWise’s Platform: A Complete Solution for Investment Intelligence

With a full suite of solutions targeting broad categories of investors and use cases, BridgeWise helps financial institutions serve their clients across the globe.

### StockWise

Provide actionable insights for any stock across multiple categories including fundamental, technical, ESG analysis, and more, helping investors make informed decisions.

### FundWise

Game changing mutual fund and ETF analysis. Help investors choose the right fund for them with transparent analysis of each and every asset in funds and clear, interactive comparisons to benchmarks.

### SignalWise

Near real-time, personalized market alerts with contextual reasoning and statistical outcome analysis

### Bridget™

An all-in-one, compliant LLM chat that combines BridgeWise’s investment intelligence, including macro events and news report analysis, with your proprietary data to boost investor activity.

### ThemeWise

Set your investor journey apart with a friction-free asset discovery experience designed to engage and drive intent across a wide range of interests, kept current by the Bridgewise research team based on emerging market trends.

### AltWise

Customizable widgets delivering 360-degree intelligence—spanning technical analysis, news sentiment, and peer comparison in any language—across alternative asset classes like crypto, forex, and commodities to power sharper, faster decisions.

### FixedWise

Revolutionary AI-powered bond analysis that empowers retail investors with clear, actionable insights while providing institutions with lightning-fast, regulation-compliant credit reviews to seize market opportunities.
