## The Future of Live Video Is Here

Livepeer built the foundation for open video—powering everything from transcoding to livestreaming. Now with Daydream, we’re showcasing the fusion of breakthrough video infrastructure and generative AI: real-time creativity, unleashed.

## Introducing Daydream

Daydream is a generative AI video platform that turns your live camera input into animated visuals from text prompts — in real time. Instant video creation for storytelling, marketing, and creative expression.

## Experience the Power of Real-Time AI Video

Livepeer enables cutting-edge capabilities including real-time content creation, instant video understanding, and advanced communication AI - all powered by our cost-effective decentralized infrastructure. Developers can build and scale innovative applications on an open, accessible platform that's driving the future of video technology.
