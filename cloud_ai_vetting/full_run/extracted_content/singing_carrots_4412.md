+ Who is this voice training for?
Singing Carrots is ideal for helping with:
- Intonation If you want to learn to sing in tune, improve your musical ear, master general intonation, or tackle it in some particular musical piece, song, or melody.
- Vocal Range If you're looking to expand your voice range or become more comfortable singing in a specific part of it.
- Rhythm If you aim to improve your ability to hit the notes on tempo.

+ Is Singing Carrots better than taking lessons with a vocal coach?
That's not exactly the right comparison—and here's why:
Imagine you decided to get fit. Would you ask if it's better to get a personal trainer or to go to the gym? That's absurd, right? A trainer can definitely boost your results, but going to the gym regularly—showing up and actually doing your exercises—is the essential part!
Singing is exactly like working out. You're learning to control and strengthen your muscles through regular practice. Singing Carrots is your vocal gym—your space to exercise consistently and see real progress.
Can you practice without a personal trainer at the gym? Absolutely. Can you practice singing without a vocal coach on Singing Carrots? Definitely. Will a great teacher supercharge your vocal progress? Yes, they can.
But here's the key: Can you just go to a singing lesson once every one or two weeks without practicing regularly and still improve? Definitely not.
That's where Singing Carrots comes in. We provide the space, guidance, real-time feedback, and motivation you need to keep practicing consistently—so you keep seeing results, week after week.
