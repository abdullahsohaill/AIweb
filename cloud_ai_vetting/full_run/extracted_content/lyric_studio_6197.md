How does it help me be creative and support self-expression?
It is well-known that professional songwriters work in teams. LyricStudio is much like a co-writer - giving you ideas for getting started and helping you whenever you're stuck. This will really get your creative juices flowing!
Just as important, you can always ignore the suggestions and write your own lyrics, using LyricStudio's suggestions, rhyming, and thesaurus as needed. It's like a co-writer, but you can't hurt its feelings! You are always in the driver's seat.
How does it help me get out of writer's block?
When it comes to curing writer's block, there is nothing as powerful as LyricStudio. By giving you suggestions tailored to your unique writing style, topics, and genre, your mind will be spinning with fresh ideas in no time. Many musicians who were unable to finish songs for months got unstuck using LyricStudio in a matter of minutes.
If you are stuck on a song, simply type in your lyrics, and see what LyricStudio suggests! If you don't know how to get started, LyricStudio will come up with limitless ideas to inspire you based on the topics of your choosing or the genre you select.
