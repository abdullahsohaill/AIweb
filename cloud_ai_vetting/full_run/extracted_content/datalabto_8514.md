## A flexible and powerful API toolkit to power your workflows

### Parse

Accurately parse complex PDFs, Spreadsheets, Powerpoint slides, and Word Documents (with redlines). Our models can detect complex layouts and multi-page section hierarchies, and produces precise layout and bounding boxes.

### Steer

Control outputs with natural language prompts, segment documents into useful units, and fine-tune models with your own data

### Extract

Transform documents into clean, structured data by defining extraction schemas specifying key information of interest

### Audit

Track data lineage through citations and bounding boxes for parsed outputs
