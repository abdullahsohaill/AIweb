# Text Difficulty Converter
**URL:** https://ai-tools2.netlify.app
**Page Title:** Text Difficulty Converter
--------------------

Your API key is required for text conversion, audio generation, and audio transcription.

## Text Difficulty Converter

This tool allows you to adjust the difficulty level of your text. 
            Select your target proficiency level (from A1 to C2), and the converter 
            will rewrite your text to match that level, making it easier or more 
            challenging to read.

## Text to Audio

This tool converts your text into spoken audio. Choose your preferred 
            text-to-speech model and voice, then enter your text to generate an 
            audio version. You can listen to individual chunks or play the entire 
            text as audio. Once generated, you can also download the audio files 
            for offline use.

## Audio to Text

This tool transcribes your audio files into text. Upload an audio file, 
          and the converter will use advanced speech recognition to generate a 
          text transcript. You can then copy or download the transcribed text.
