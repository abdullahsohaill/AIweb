### Venturo - Stock Option Grant Tracker + Tax Optimization Tool

Optimize Your Equity Compensation Strategy - Track & manage employee stock options. Make tax-optimized decisions when exercising and selling your equity.
Venturo is an easy-to-use tool for tracking your employee stock options. Leverage powerful tax-optimization tools to make smarter decisions about exercising and selling your equity, saving on costly advisory services.
- Monitor Your Equity at a Glance: Track all equity grants, exercised units, and vesting schedules in one intuitive dashboard with clear visualizations of your current value and progress.
Monitor Your Equity at a Glance: Track all equity grants, exercised units, and vesting schedules in one intuitive dashboard with clear visualizations of your current value and progress.
- Customize Holdings: Model even the most intricate ISO/NSO equity grants with flexible, month-by-month editing to support custom and nuanced vesting scenarios.
Customize Holdings: Model even the most intricate ISO/NSO equity grants with flexible, month-by-month editing to support custom and nuanced vesting scenarios.
- Minimize Tax Impact: Optimize your exercise strategy with advanced tax simulation tools that calculate AMT tax implications and maximize your savings.
Minimize Tax Impact: Optimize your exercise strategy with advanced tax simulation tools that calculate AMT tax implications and maximize your savings.
- Plan Your Exit with Confidence: Model various exit scenarios to maximize after-tax proceeds when selling your stock options.
Plan Your Exit with Confidence: Model various exit scenarios to maximize after-tax proceeds when selling your stock options.
If you're a private company employee, equity is such a large part of your compensation package. Whether you're planning an exercise, preparing for a liquidity event, or simply tracking your portfolio, Venturo provides the clarity and control you need to maximize your equity's value while minimizing tax burdens.
