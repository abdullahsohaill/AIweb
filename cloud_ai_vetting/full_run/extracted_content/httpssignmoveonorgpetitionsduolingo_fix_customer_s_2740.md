## To: Luis Von Ahn, CEO of Duolingo

## Duolingo: Fix Customer Support NOW!

- Hire more full-time customer support agents— ensuring paying users can actually get help.
- Guarantee response times for Super and Max subscribers —if people are paying, they deserve real service.

### Why is this important?

- A Max subscriber (a $30 a month subscription) had their entire course locked for days —even after filing multiple support tickets. They only got help after messaging a Duolingo employee they know directly.
- A Super subscriber complained about Max ads in their "ad-free" Super subscription —and then Duolingo had their account canceled. They still haven’t gotten a response.
- A paying subscriber who was hospitalized after an assault lost their streak and reached out for support —but never got a reply.
- Duolingo is worth $16 BILLION. Yet, it refuses to invest in real customer support, the CEO thinks AI will fix everything.
- Paying subscribers are waiting weeks—or forever—for help.
- Duolingo is leaving real users stranded.
- Companies don’t fix problems out of goodwill—they fix them when customers demand it.
- If you’re a Duolingo user, this affects you. If you’re a paying subscriber, you deserve better.
- If you believe billion-dollar companies should actually support their customers, now is the time to act.

### How it will be delivered

To Duolingo.
