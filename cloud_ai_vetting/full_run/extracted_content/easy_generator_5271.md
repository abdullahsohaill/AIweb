### Easy for everyone

Easygenerator is designed for simplicity and ease of use, which means anyone in your organization can share knowledge without adding to L&D’s workload.

### Instant localization

Translate courses in one click with AI, then have local teams review the content. Easygenerator supports 75 languages so everyone can learn in their native language.

### Reliable Al that helps you create

Creating courses is easier and faster with EasyAI. Turn old files into  e-learning or have AI help create training from scratch. Included in all plans at no extra cost.

### AI-powered video creation

Create professional training videos without crews, cameras, or complex tools. EasyVideo makes it simple for anyone to create engaging videos at scale.

### Distribution without roadblocks

Publish to any LMS or LXP with native integrations, sync updates instantly with dynamic SCORM, or own every detail with manual SCORM. Your content, your rules.
