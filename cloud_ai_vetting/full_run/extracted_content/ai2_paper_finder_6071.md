## Welcome to Ai2 Paper Finder's new home

Introducing Asta, Ai2's unified research assistant. Paper Finder is now Find papers , one of Asta's research capabilities.
A scholarly research assistant that combines literature understanding and data-driven discovery. Asta uses 108M+ abstracts and 12M+ full-text papers to find, summarize, and analyze scientific evidence. A project from Ai2 .
Not sure where to start? Try these examples:
- Research on generative document retrieval models that allow for quick addition of new documents as they become available
- Papers about long term memory in LLM agents
- Papers about shallow marine ecosystem classification using hyperspectral imagery
- Papers introducing a dataset of an unscripted dialogue between 2 speakers (written or transcription) in English where there is an annotation of some property (emotion, age, gender, etc.) of one of the speakers.
- Papers by Dan Weld about planning
- The Brown clustering paper
