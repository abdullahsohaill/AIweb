## For Text Prompts:

- Topic : Be specific about what you want the AI to focus on
- Purpose : Define what you want the AI to do (explain, analyze, create, etc.)
- Tone : Select the appropriate writing style for your audience
- Format : Choose how you want the information structured
- Length : Adjust between brief and detailed responses

## For Image Prompts:

- Subject : Clearly describe the main element of your image
- Style : Select an artistic approach (realistic, digital art, etc.)
- Setting : Describe the environment or background
- Lighting/Mood : Define the atmosphere of the image
- Perspective : Choose the viewing angle

## For Video Prompts:

- Scene : Describe what you want to see in the video
- Camera Movement : Select how the camera should move
- Style : Choose the visual approach
- Action : Describe any movement within the scene
