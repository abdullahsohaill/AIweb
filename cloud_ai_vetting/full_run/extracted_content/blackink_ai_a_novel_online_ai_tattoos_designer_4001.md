Yes, 100% unique! No tattoo will be the same design, even with the same prompt and style choice. What you get will be 100% unique and just for you. :)

## How much does BlackInk.AI cost?

BlackInk.AI is free to try. You can generate 3 times for free. After that, you can subscribe to the Pro Unlimited Plan.

Currently, no. But we plan to in the future.

## Can I upload my own image?

You can upload your own image through our "Image-to-Stencil" feature.

## What size are the image downloads?

4x High Res Image: 2048x2048px Low Res Image: 512x512px Printout Image: 512x512px

## What languages do you support?

The AI tools support over 100 languages for user input. We translate the idea only if we detect non-English text, so we recommend English if possible. Content on the site is in English, so we recommend Google Translate while browsing.

## Are my custom design kept private?

When you create a design, you can specify if you want it private. Afterwards, you can toggle it being public in 'My Collection' >> click the 'Eye Ball' icon.

## Why do some tattoos come out weird?

Tattoos should be weird! It's a new and experimental product so there's always a chance of creating something strange.

## Should I get these tattoos as-is?

Sometimes there are weird shapes or bad symmetry. Always have your tattoo artist review and cleanup the design.

## What is the license for the generated tattoos? Am I allowed to get them tattooed?
