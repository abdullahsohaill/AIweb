## Automate and control every piece of your cloud infrastructure

### Platform Engineering

Automate self-service provisioning and manage IaC frameworks
Convert unmanaged resources to IaC with AI-assisted generation to achieve 100% IaC coverage
Identify cloud misconfigurations in real time and remediate issues automatically—before they impact production
Trace and map all cloud assets, dependencies, and owners across your organization for full cloud visibility

### FinOps

Identify idle resources and implement automated cleanup through IaC workflows
Block budget-breaking deployments in CI/CD and enforce cost policies early in development
Apply and maintain consistent resource tags to improve cost allocation, optimization, and reporting
Complement FinOps reporting with actual remediations in IaC, and move from findings to real savings

### SecOps

Instantly fix cloud security issues directly in IaC
Transform security requirements into reusable, versioned Policy-as-Code
Alert and rollback when resources deviate from approved configurations
Integrate 600+ compliance checks directly into your CI/CD workflows

### CXO

Back up all cloud infrastructure configurations with rapid restore capabilities to meet RTO and BCDR goals
Comprehensive Cloud Asset Management provides asset tracking with ownership history and complete IBoM
Implement change management that traces every modification from pull request to production deployment
Keep your engineers on-task for maximum effectiveness: spending their time building features that drive revenue, not on cloud configurations
