### Frequently asked questions

### What is Colossyan?

Colossyan is an AI video platform where anyone can easily turn text, documents, presentations and screen recordings into avatar‑narrated training videos. Colossyan’s technology eliminates the need for expensive video production, while enabling teams to create, update and translate video content.

### How do I create a video in Colossyan?

1. Start a new draft, or upload your document/PPT/PDF. 2. Paste and edit your script in the editor. 3. Pick a relevant AI voice and/or AI avatar. 4. Design your scenes, and add animations and media where needed. 5. Preview and generate your video, and export or share it once it’s ready.

### Is it possible to upload an existing document into Colossyan?

Yes! You can either import your PPT/PDF into Colossyan and keep the original slides, or upload  a PPT/PDF/DOC into the AI video creator, which will generate a video draft and scripts based on  your file’s content, and use Colossyan’s pre-made video templates.

### Can I create videos in different languages?

Absolutely. Colossyan supports automatic translation into 80+ languages, with a selection of over  600 different AI voices and accents. Additionally, if you’re on the Enterprise plan, you can create variants in multiple languages, and combine them into one multi‑lingual video, where the viewers  can pick their preferred language.
