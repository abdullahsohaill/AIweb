### Fully-managed team

### Fully-managed team

A fully-managed team provides companies with a comprehensive, end-to-end product development solution. Your agency will cover all aspects of building a product, from conceptualization to deployment, leveraging an experienced team of designers, engineers, and product owners to deliver high-quality, market-ready solutions.
Use cases:
- Develop a new product or feature — from idea to launch.
- Overhaul existing products with modern technology stacks
- Keep your core team focused on core product while building a new feature
- Enter new markets with products tailored to local demands
- Execute large-scale digital transformation projects.
- Launch MVPs to test markets with minimal time and investment.

### Team augmentation

### Team augmentation

Team Augmentation allows companies to expand their existing product and engineering workforce by integrating skilled talent from top-tier agencies. Your agency will provide the right talent to fill critical skill gaps and accelerate product development — ensuring projects meet deadlines and quality standards without the need for long-term hiring commitments.
Use cases:
- Fill in skill gaps for specialized technologies like AI or Web3.
- Accelerate development during critical project phases.
- Embed niche industry expertise like Fintech or Healthcare.
- Support rapid scaling for startups or fast-growing companies.
- Enable 24/7 development cycles by leveraging global time zones.
- Add engineering capacity and capabilities for product upgrades.

### Build a team

### Build a team

Build your own team with our Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT) model—a strategic approach that helps companies establish a dedicated development center without overhead and risks. Your agency partner builds and operates the team while managing all operational complexities until you are partially or fully ready to transfer a team of full-time employees to your payroll.
Use cases:
- Establish a development center risk-free with a local partner.
- Access top global talent in key hubs while optimising costs.
- Ensure quality, cultural fit, and IP protection from day one.
- Maintain full ownership with minimal operational burden.
- “Try before you buy” to mitigate hiring and attrition risk.
- Easily transition the team to your payroll when ready.

### Fractional CTO/CPO

### Fractional CTO/CPO

Connect with top-tier fractional CTOs and CPOs to guide your development. Our curated network features seasoned technology executives from leading unicorns and tech enterprises, each with 10+ years of expertise in product and engineering. Spanning the US, Latin America, Europe, and the Middle East, we bring you the best in strategic fractional leadership.
Use cases:
- Maintain leadership while hiring permanently or upskilling your team.
- Engage on demand, scale up or down to fit your needs and budget.
- Impartial second opinion—by your side—as you build.
- Avoid costly mistakes and accelerate development.
- Validate ideas and safeguard key decisions.
- Executive expertise at a fraction of the cost.
