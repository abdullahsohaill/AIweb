iClone 8 - Try 30-Days Free
iClone delivers the fastest professional results for real-time 3D animation. Orchestrate facial performance, motion capture, scene design, and cinematic storytelling—all within one intuitive platform backed by a rich 3D ecosystem.

## START CHARACTER ANIMATION EASILY

### BODY ANIMATION

Create high-quality character animations in the quickest way possible with realtime previews, smooth motion blending, mocap correction, and layered motion controls.

### FACIAL ANIMATION

Take complete command of expressive facial performances with advanced facial animation tools, including muscle-level editing, real-time puppeteering, and precise lip sync.

### 2,000+ FREE ASSETS

Ready-made characters, motions, props, scenes, effects, and lighting presets—all at your fingertips.

## OPTIMAL ANIMATION WORKFLOW

### MOTION CAPTURE

Motion LIVE is iClone’s mocap hub, capturing full-body motion from leading devices—perfectly synced for animation.

### CROWD SIMULATION

Real-time, customizable crowd animations with smart 3D characters that self-navigate and perform diverse actions.

### GAME-LIKE CONTROL

Motion Director lets you control 3D characters with game controllers, waypoints, or AI—just like playing a video game.

### CUSTOM DEVICE CONTROL

iClone supports gamepads, MIDI boards, Stream Decks, and SpaceMouse for hands-on experience.
