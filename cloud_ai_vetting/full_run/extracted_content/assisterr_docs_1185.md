## Overview

Assisterr AI offers the infrastructure and no-code tools to solve real-world problems with AI Agents organized with a Mixture of Agent structures and powered by Small Language Models (SLMs). 

The platform allows individuals and organizations to easily build , monetize, and tokenize custom SLM-powered Agents while incentivizing community contributions.
SLMs, while similar in concept to Large Language Models (LLMs) like Chat-GPT or BERT, are designed to be more accurate, specialized, and efficient. By focusing on specific tasks and data sets, SLMs provide superior performance for niche applications, making them ideal for business and domain-specific use cases. Through AssisterrAI users gain the ability to create tailored solutions that address their unique challenges with precision and efficiency.
