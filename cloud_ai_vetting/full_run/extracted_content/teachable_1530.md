## Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my account at any time?
We hope you don’t, but of course you can! If you decide Teachable is not right for you, simply cancel your account.
Can I change my plan once I sign up?
Yes. You can change your plan at any time from your school’s admin. You’ll be charged the new plan amount at your next billing date.
Are there any transaction fees?
There are 0% transaction fees on the Builder, Growth, Advanced and Unlimited plans for those using using teachable:pay or Monthly Payment Gateway. There is a 7.5% transaction fee on the Starter plan. Standard processing fees may still apply.
Who uses Teachable?
Teachable is for creators, experts, entrepreneurs, and businesses who are serious about education. From language teachers helping people connect across cultures, to finance professionals teaching investment strategy, to health and fitness leaders building programs that transform lives—our platform is built to scale real-world expertise into thriving education businesses.
