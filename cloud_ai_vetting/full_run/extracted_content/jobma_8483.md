### Jobma AI

Jobma uses ethical AI for standardized hiring. Ensure fair evaluation with AI scoring and proctoring and get accurate multi-lingual transcriptions for video and audio responses.

### One-Way Video Interviewing

Accelerate hiring with asynchronous one-way video interviews. Assess a large pool of candidates from anywhere in the world with standardized interview kits and assessments.

### Live Video Interviewing

Interview top candidates face-to-face with live video interviews and panel interviews. You can record the live video interviews and share them with your team.

### Written & Audio Assessments

Gain deeper insights into candidates with AI-powered written, multi-choice, and audio assessment tools. Choose from pre-existing skill assessments or create your own.

### Coding Assessments

Assess technical skills efficiently through standardized coding assessments and automated evaluations. Save time and money and scale your technical teams.

Use automated interview scheduling to let candidates schedule interviews per your availability. Sync with your calendar and get interview notifications by email and phone.
