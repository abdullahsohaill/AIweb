## Current Limitations For FLUX Kontext

We believe in transparency. FLUX Kontext technology continues to evolve with ongoing improvements. Here are the current limitations we're actively working to improve.
- Multi-round editing may introduce quality degradation after extensive modifications, though performance degrades gracefully.
- Text editing currently supports English only. Chinese and other languages preserve original text but cannot be modified.
- Large-scale changes or dramatic style shifts may trigger safety filters and require prompt refinement.
- Complex multi-image references work best when images are manually combined before processing.
- Character facial features may drift slightly with extreme pose or expression changes.
- Best results with specific, detailed prompts. Vague instructions may produce unexpected variations.
