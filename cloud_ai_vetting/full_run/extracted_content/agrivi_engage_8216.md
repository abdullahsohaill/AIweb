## AGRIVI Product Family

Comprehensive digital agriculture solutions empower growers and agrifood companies across the value chain with AI and data-driven tools to produce healthy, safe, and nutritious food in an efficient and sustainable way.
AGRIVI AI
Engage
Innovative AI-advisory platform, tailor-made to support agrifood companies to engage and nurture strong relationships with farmers by bringing agriculture knowledge and advice via white-labeled WhatsApp or Viber AI agronomic advisor.
AGRIVI 360 Farm Management Software
Farm Enterprise
Market leading farm management software supporting farmers with data-driven tools and real-time insights from the field to make precise agronomic and economic decisions and maximize productivity and profitability.
AGRIVI Food
Traceability
Digital solution for complete food production traceability, by giving food and retail companies the ability to provide transparent insight into the lifecycle of fresh or processed food products to the end customers, from seed to shelves.
AGRIVI Connect

## Monitor All Your Data from One Place in Real-Time

AGRIVI offers complete interoperability for all your machinery, IoT and ERP. Seamlessly connect and synchronize your weather station, soil sensors or machines and collect and manage all data from the fields in one place. Simple plug & play integration to AGRIVI farm management platform for real-time and precise agricultural data monitoring .
