- Following Alan Turing 's work in the 1950s on intelligent machines (future computers), the first chatbots were created in the 1960s . ELIZA, created in 1966 by MIT, was one of the first chatbots. Far more rudimentary than today's chatbots, ELIZA was already able to detect questions and keywords in user requests. These new technologies have greatly improved machines' understanding of contexts and human language, making chatbots much more versatile.
