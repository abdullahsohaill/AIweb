Connected Worker Platform

## The Only Agentic AI Platform for Connected Work

Onboard workers faster, reduce time to productivity, deliver targeted training, and support workers with AI Agents and AI-powered immersive guidance.
Augmentir is the unified "Single Pane of Glass" for frontline operations - digitally connecting skills, training, and work to help you maximize the potential of your workforce.
Trusted by the world’s most innovative companies
Why Augmentir?

## The smartest way to run your frontline operations

In a world of constant workforce disruption, outdated paper or static processes can’t keep pace. Frontline digitization is now essential – and Augmentir delivers smart digitization across the entire worker lifecycle, from hire to retire.
Augmentir’s AI-native connected worker platform combines Generative AI (via Augie, the first industrial GenAI suite) with Agentic AI for autonomous, context-aware support. This creates a step change in how manufacturers develop, train, and support their workforce.
With integrated digital workflows, remote collaboration, skills management, and AI-powered insights, Augmentir continuously adapts to your dynamic workforce – helping every worker perform at their best.
How We Help

## All-in-One AI-Powered Connected Worker Platform

Augmentir is a connected worker solution that allows industrial companies to digitize and optimize processes that support frontline workers from “hire to retire”.
The suite of connected worker capabilities are built on top of Augmentir’s patented Smart AI foundation, which closes the loop between training and work execution, delivering the data and in-line insights necessary to continuously improve operational excellence day-over-day, year-over-year.

### AI Agents

## Build and manage custom AI Agents for your factory

Augmentir’s Industrial AI Agent Studio empowers manufacturers to build, customize, and deploy their own Industrial AI Agents quickly and easily, with no-code tools, supporting use cases across workforce training and development, skills management, safety, quality, operations, and maintenance.

### Gen AI Assistant

## Augie™ – Industrial Generative AI Suite

Quickly generate standard work procedures from Excel, Word, PDFs, images, or videos. Augie takes your existing content and generates digital smart forms, checklists, and immersive digital work instructions.
- Provide digital assistance to workers
- Create procedures from videos and existing content
- Deliver contextualized training in the flow of work
- Ensure work accuracy and safety with image verification
- Utilize Augie as an autonomous AI Agent

### Augmented Reality

## Modernize training and standard work with Industrial AR

Augmentir AR modernizes training and standard work by putting clear, spatial guidance directly on equipment and work areas.
For plant managers, trainers, and frontline operators, industrial augmented reality translates to faster onboarding, fewer errors, and higher first-time-right performance.
Where Augmentir Adds Value

## Augmentir is a Single Pane of Glass Across Your Entire Frontline Operations

Safety
Digitize safety incident and near-miss reporting. Digital checklists and instructions help ensure compliance and minimize impacts. Executive scorecards highlight key safety KPIs and at-risk procedures.
Quality
Replace paper and digitize quality data collection and inspection procedures to reduce errors and waste, and improve timeliness with live integrations to your QMS to ensure that you are always “audit ready”.
Operations
Ensure equipment uptime and yield and improve the safety, quality, and efficiency of assembly and operator care routines performed by plant management, operations, and maintenance.
Autonomous Maintenance
Give operators more control over equipment cleaning, lubricating, and inspection to identify issues before they become failures. Use Smart Digital Work Instructions to empower a wider range of workers to perform maintenance jobs safely and correctly.
Preventative Maintenance
Improve the quality, transparency, and efficiency of maintenance and repair procedures. Access to remote expert assistance in the flow of work helps solve problems quickly. Integrate to popular CMMS such as SAP PM & Maximo to automatically capture repair times and material usage to improve the accuracy of maintenance planning and scheduling.
Workforce Development
Digitize the entire skills management lifecycle and instantly visualize the skills matrix for your team, see what skill gaps exist, and take action to fill them. Smart AI insights enable targeted reskilling and upskilling, as well as individualized support to workers.
