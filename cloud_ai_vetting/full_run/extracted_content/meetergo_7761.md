## Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about meetergo

### Is there a free plan?

Yes! meetergo offers a generous free plan that includes unlimited bookings, calendar integrations, and basic features. You can use meetergo at no cost for as long as you like.

### Does meetergo sync with Outlook and Google Calendar?

Absolutely. meetergo integrates seamlessly with Google Calendar, Microsoft Outlook, Apple Calendar, and any CalDAV-compatible calendar. Your availability syncs in real-time across all connected calendars.

### Can I accept payments through my booking page?

Yes, meetergo supports integrated payment processing through Stripe and PayPal. You can collect payments, deposits, or consultation fees directly when clients book appointments.

### Is meetergo GDPR compliant?

Yes. meetergo is fully GDPR compliant and hosted on EU servers. We take data privacy seriously with enterprise-grade security, data encryption, and full compliance with European data protection regulations.
