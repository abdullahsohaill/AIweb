## Legora streamlines everything from research to drafting and review — helping lawyers spend less time managing process, and more time delivering value.

### Review faster

Being an exceptional lawyer means reviewing and reading mountains of material. Legora can analyze tens of thousands of documents simultaneously, and suggest well-crafted markup based on your preferences.

### Draft smarter

Legora draws on precedent to draft, rewrite, and refine your content in Word. It identifies the substance of each document and suggests relevant, ready-to-use language—with you in control of what’s approved and applied.

### Research deeper

Access up-to-date information, legal databases, and your DMS content, all within Legora. With partnerships across jurisdictions and integrations with iManage and SharePoint, everything lawyers need is now in one place.
The collaborative AI workspace built to fit seamlessly into a lawyer’s workflow. Legora adapts to your ways of working, unlocking team and machine collaboration at scale.
