## Research starts here

### The new standard for academic research

Search and analyze peer-reviewed literature. Consensus draws on 250M+ research papers, including licensed full text content from leading publishers.

### Used daily at top research institutions

Over 170 university libraries partner with Consensus to give students and faculty access. 10 million researchers, students, and clinicians use Consensus to supercharge literature reviews — without sacrificing academic rigor.

### Automate Literature Review with Deep Search

Turn days of literature review into a few minutes. Consensus Deep Search builds a comprehensive search strategy, expanding key terms, identifying conflicting arguments, and exploring the citation graph.

### Try Medical mode

Narrow your results to the highest-quality medical sources — about 50,000 clinical guidelines and 8M articles from the top 1,000 medical journals for quick, trusted clinical answers.

### Use filters with natural language

Specify timeframes, populations, designs, and more directly in your prompt — and Consensus automatically applies the right filters for your search.

### See where the research agrees

Ask a clear yes-or-no research question and the Consensus Meter instantly shows how much the evidence agrees or disagrees — giving you a fast, visual read on the state of the research.
