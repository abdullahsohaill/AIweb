## FAQs of Image Sharpening

- What is image sharpness? In simple words, image sharpness refers to the clarity and detail of an image. It measures how well-defined the edges and fine details are in an image. A sharp image has well-defined edges and crisp details, whereas a blurry image lacks clarity and appears out of focus. Sharpness is determined by various factors such as camera settings, focus, aperture, lens quality, and image processing. It is also possible to enhance image sharpness through photo editing software after capturing the image.
