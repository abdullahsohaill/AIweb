## FAQ

What is Gistly?
Gistly is a tool that uses YouTube AI technology to summarize videos in seconds, making it your go-to for video summaries.
How does Gistly summarize videos?
Gistly employs its proprietary AI technology in along with OpenAI GPT to extract, analyze, and condense the main points of a video. We prioritize the quality of our AI YouTube summaries.
Can I summarize videos in other languages?
Yes, you can. Gistly supports any input language and offers translations in over 40 output languages. Our YouTube AI breaks language barriers.
Can I summarize my own videos?
Yes, you can. Simply upload an unlisted video on YouTube and use Gistly to summarize it. Our AI YouTube tool makes it easy.
Can I summarize lengthy videos?
Sure. You can summarize even 4-6 hour videos with our YouTube summary AI. There are technical limits, but they are very hard to reach.
Can Gistly summarize podcasts and news as well?
At the moment, Gistly only supports YouTube videos. However, many podcasts and news segments can be found on YouTube and can be summarized using our tool.
