## Image Sequence Animation WordPress Plugin

Blow your website visitors away as they scroll through your homepage or posts. Create image sequence animations that will set your website apart, without compromising performance.

### Designed to grab attention, optimized for speed

Silky smooth animation powered by GSAP, even on mobile devices with sluggish processors
Animate elements on scroll, in sync with your media. Fade in or out, move, and scale, all within the plugin UI.
Scripts are loaded only when a scroll sequence is present on the page. Zero weight on other pages.
Only 129 kB script size.
Only a few images are fetched on page load. The remaining ones load in the background.
Tested across all major browsers, devices and operating systems. Choose where to disable animations if needed.
Compatible with Gutenberg, Elementor , Oxygen, Divi , Beaver Builder and other WordPress page builders.
