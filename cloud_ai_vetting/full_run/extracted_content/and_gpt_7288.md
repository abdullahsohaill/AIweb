## Copilot

## For your Android

And-GPT is an AI agent that uses the latest GPT-4 technology to understand your goals and automatically break them down into smaller tasks.
Based on the tasks, And-GPT will decide what app should be opened and how the app should be operated for the next step: searching, clicking, typing, all left to And-GPT. As one of the first examples of allowing GPT to manipulate Android devices, And-GPT pushes the boundaries of what is possible with AI.
