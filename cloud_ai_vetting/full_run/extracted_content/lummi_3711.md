## What's new

### Introducing Video Generations

Generate videos up to 8 seconds long with a prompt or animate an image in seconds. Now with audio too, giving you a solid start right from the jump!

### Your generations just got better!

Nano Banana and Seedream now power your generations and edits. That means character references stay consistent, objects don’t get weirdly warped, and your prompts land way closer to what you imagined.

### Download all your selects in one go

You can now bulk download images straight from the grid. If you're a Pro user, just select your favorites and get them all at once.

### Reframe 2.0 just dropped

Now you can move any image’s focal point wherever you need. Plus, you can zoom in on details or out for the full scene, giving you total control over every Reframe.

### 3D models are here

What used to be static 3D illustrations are now full 3D models. Rotate them, pick your angle, change the background color, and grab the shot you need. Or download them as .gltf for design projects, games, and interactive experiences.
