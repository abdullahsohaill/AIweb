## What can Bricks do?

Drag, drop, and customize manually or use natural language to edit with AI.
Update your data and instantly regenerate all charts while preserving your layout.
Click any data point to filter your entire dashboard and uncover hidden patterns.
Save your perfect dashboard layout and reuse it with fresh data every time.
Apply beautiful, polished visual themes to your dashboards - no design skills needed.
Import your data from Google Sheets or databases to create dashboards and reports.
