### Receive

### Split & Classify

Sort, split, and route documents by type, and proactively identify missing files. No more manual sorting headaches.

### Extract

Capture structured data from unstructured tables, forms, handwriting, and other complex layouts with 95%+ accuracy, every time.

### Enrich & Categorize

Combine extracted data with business context - categorize transactions, tag metadata, and link related records for smarter insights.
