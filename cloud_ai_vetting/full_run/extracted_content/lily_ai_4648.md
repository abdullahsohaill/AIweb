### Data Layer

### AI Models

AI models expertly trained to unlock rich product details from your catalog and transform them into conversion-driving language that resonates with shoppers and machines.

Your catalog is expertly translated into the language your customers speak and optimized for machine understanding, fueling  connections, visibility, and results.

### Trends

### Occasions

### Product Copy

### Site Search

Turbocharge your site search with Lily’s enriched product attributes, synonyms and trends, empowering shoppers to discover the perfect item every time.

### Paid Ads

Maximize Ad performance with data populated into Google Merchant Center that’s structured for machines, informed by consumer searches, and dynamically updated to reflect shopper intent.

### SEO & GEO

### Marketplaces

### Application Layer
