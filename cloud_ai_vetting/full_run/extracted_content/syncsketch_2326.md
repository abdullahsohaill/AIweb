## Features All the bells and whistles

We’ve got all the features you need to support your workflow

### Rich Annotations

Use our powerful brush engine to add detailed and frame-accurate feedback to your media.

### Synchronous Presentation

Review your media offline or in real-time with your team. No compression artifacts or skipped frames. With SyncSketch, it feels like you’re sitting in the same room.

### 3D Model Reviews

Review static or animated 3D assets directly within SyncSketch including shading, textures & UV maps.

### Share with Anyone

Powerful and easy link sharing makes collaboration with your clients a breeze. Get feedback instantly and iterate less.

### Presentation Mode

Allow specific presenters to drive the review, while including as many observers as you like in the session.

### Smartphone and Tablet Support

Use your mobile devices to give feedback from wherever you happen to be.

### PDF Summary

Quickly generate a PDF summary of your reviews including screenshots with all annotations and comments.

### Compare Files

Easily compare any item to a previous version or reference footage.

### Whiteboard

Brainstorm on the fly with our collaborative whiteboard. Use custom brushes to sketch, animate and share ideas on a timeline.
