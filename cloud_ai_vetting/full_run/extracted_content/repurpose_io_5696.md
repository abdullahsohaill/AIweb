## FAQ

What platforms can I repurpose content to and from?
Repurpose.io connects with all major social media and cloud platforms — including YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, Pinterest, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), Amazon, BlueSky, Twitch, Google Drive, Dropbox, and more. You can automatically post videos, Stories, or audio across multiple destinations.
Can I repurpose Stories?
Yes! 🎉
Repurpose.io now supports Instagram, Facebook, and Snapchat
Stories (TikTok coming soon) — letting you automatically share your Stories from one platform to another. Reach new audiences without uploading the same Story multiple times.
Do I need to download or manually upload my videos?
No. Once your workflows are set up, Repurpose.io handles everything automatically. Your content is pulled directly from your source (like TikTok or Instagram) and published to your selected destinations — resized, watermark-free, and ready to go.
Do I need to share my login info with Repurpose.io?
Nope — your accounts stay secure. You connect through official APIs (like “Connect with TikTok” or “Connect with YouTube”), so you never share your passwords. Repurpose.io is an approved app and official partner of Meta, YouTube, Snapchat, TikTok, Amazon, and more , ensuring your content and data are always protected.
Is there a free plan or trial available?
Yes! You can publish 10 videos for free — no credit card required. Then you can upgrade to a paid plan to unlock more accounts, higher limits, and premium automation features.
