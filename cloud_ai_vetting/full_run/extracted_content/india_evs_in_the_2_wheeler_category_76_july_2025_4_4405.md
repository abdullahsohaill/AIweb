Total Registered Electric Vehicle Sales – Jul’25 – 1,88,804 I Jun’25 – 1,81,573
Source: Vahan Dashboard as of Aug 2, 2025. Telangana Data not included. Low speed e-2W sales data not included.
Electric vehicle sales in India showed a mixed trend in July 2025. E2W sales declined to 1,02,924 units from 1,05,931 in June. E-rickshaw sales increased to 39,792 units from 35,364, while E3W L5 Passenger sales rose to 20,448 from 16,329. E-cart sales remained nearly unchanged at 6,813 units compared to 6,849 in June. E3W L5 Cargo/Goods also saw a slight increase to 2,092 units from 2,016. E4W sales grew to 15,437 units from 13,921. E-bus sales declined to 361 units from 531, while E-Goods Carrier sales rose to 875 from 617.
21,11,554 EVs sold in last 12 months from Aug 2024 to Jul 2025
Source: Vahan Dashboard as of Aug 2, 2025. Telangana Data not included. Low speed e-2W sales data not included.
India recorded 21,11,554 EV sales from Aug 2024 to July 2025, per Vahan Dashboard data (excluding Telangana and low-speed e-2Ws). Monthly sales peaked in October 2024 (2,19,724) and were lowest in Feb 2025 (1,40,182).
