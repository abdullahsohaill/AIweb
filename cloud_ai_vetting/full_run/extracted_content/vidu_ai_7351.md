- Vidu's Image-to-Video feature allows users to create AI-generated videos from a static image, bringing them to life with motion and animation. - Reference to Video lets users upload three or more images of an object, character, or scene as references, and Vidu can combine elements into a seamless video based on a prompt. Vidu's Reference to Video also supports uploading different angles of a character or object to ensure consistency. Vidu also offers the My References feature, allowing users to save characters, props, and scenes for effortless reuse, enhancing both creative efficiency and video quality. This feature is ideal for maintaining consistency and seamlessly generating videos that blend multiple visual references.
