# Noctie
**URL:** https://noctie.ai
**Page Title:** Noctie.ai (chess AI)

## A chess bot that's built for your training

## The most realistic and fun chess bot

Noctie is a chess AI that mimics human play from beginner to
                    grandmaster level.
That means, humanlike opening choice, mistakes and move
                    timings.
Trained on billions of games between humans and carefully
                    fine-tuned over several years.
Try it out for yourself!

## Pick your opening and practice

Choose among all common openings, and Noctie will play the
                    most common human responses.
Import your own repertoires / opening books and drill them
                    together with chess AI.
Truly learn openings by playing realistic full games in
                    them.

## Puzzle decks from your mistakes

Your mistakes are learning opportunities.
Our chess AI creates puzzles after each game, from your most
                    instructive mistakes.
Practice decks with these and other puzzles using spaced
                    repetition learning.

## Learn from instant feedback

Get real-time feedback on your chess moves with Noctie's
                    color-coded system.
When you make a brilliant move, our AI chess helper lets you
                    know instantly — like a high-five for your brain!
Made a mistake? Take it back and ask for a hint.

## Set up positions and endgames

Noctie is perfect for playing out interesting positions
                    against the chess AI.
Set up endgame positions or choose from our library of
                    positions.
Explore instructive positions with chess AI, without the
                    pressure of a clock.

## Lessons and daily content

Explore our in-built lessons covering theoretical endgames
                    and positional themes.
Learn a new opening via our pre-made, commented repertoires.
Challenge yourself by playing our weekly scenarios – a new
                    position to beat every weak.
Keep your streak by solving the daily puzzle every day.

## Our philosophy

We're creating a chess learning platform centered entirely
                    around the joy of learning.
While online chess is exciting and competitive, it can limit
                    real learning opportunities.
We're creating an relaxed, fun space where you're in control
                    – where you can learn at your own pace and grasp new
                    knowledge without pressure.

## A new kind of chess AI

Noctie is nothing like regular chess engines – you know,
                        the ones alternating between crushing your spirit with
                        flawless chess moves and making silly one-move blunders.
To create the ultimate learning experience, we had to
                        rethink AI for chess from the ground up.
Normal chess bots get their strength from calculating to
                        inhuman depths. Noctie instead relies on intuition
                        obtained from billions of human games.
That means Noctie's strengths, weaknesses and even move
                        timings, are all similar to a human at your own skill
                        level - whether you're a beginner or an aspiring master.
