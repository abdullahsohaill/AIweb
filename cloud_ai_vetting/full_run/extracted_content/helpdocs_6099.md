## Deflect tickets before they're created.

Turn documentation into your most productive support channel, delivering instant answers through smart search, AI-powered summaries, and contextual help that appears exactly when customers need it.
Our built-in contact form suggests replies when your customers is typing their ticket.
Natural language questions get instant answers synthesized from your entire knowledge base, no hallucinations.
Surface answers in your app with Lighthouse, chatbot with our source page, or with one of our integrations.
Our search has been in the making for years. It's typo-tolerant and understands intent, not just keywords.

## Make it unmistakably yours.

Go live with professional templates in hours, then customize at your own pace, from simple no-code branding to full CSS/JS/HTML control. All without vendor lock-in.
Engineered for SEO, accessibility, and speed. Our hand-crafted templates are designed to convert visitors into self-serve users.
Simple styling or complete custom builds with our industry-standard HTML templating with Handlebars.
Our staging environment feature lets your developers work on your template in the background. And when you're ready, promote it live.
