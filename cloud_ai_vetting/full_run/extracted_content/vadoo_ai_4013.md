## What is Vadoo AI?

Vadoo AI is an all-in-one AI platform that lets you generate videos and images using text, scripts, or prompts. It combines multiple advanced AI models to help creators, marketers, and businesses produce content faster and more creatively — with no editing experience required.

### How can I get started with Vadoo AI?

Simply sign up at ai.vadoo.tv , choose the type of content you want to create (video, image, short, etc.), enter a prompt or upload your source, and let Vadoo AI generate your content in seconds.

### What types of videos can I create with Vadoo AI?

You can create explainer videos, YouTube Shorts, TikToks, Instagram Reels, product demos, talking-head videos, podcasts, and more. Vadoo AI also supports long-form to short-form video repurposing.

### Can I upload my own videos or images for editing or enhancement?

Yes, you can upload videos and images and use Vadoo AI to generate captions, voiceovers, translations, stylized effects, B-rolls, and more.
