## The AI Platform For Workforce Transformation

Connect talent strategy to business outcomes with real-time intelligence on people, skills, and tasks.

## Explore Our Workforce Intelligence Platform

### Build on connected, trusted data

Bring together fragmented data on skills, roles, people, and the market to create a dynamic, reliable source of truth. Beamery’s platform connects your ecosystem, powering every decision with a consistent view of your workforce.

### Turn insight into strategic advantage

Model future scenarios, uncover talent risks, and understand how work gets done. With Beamery’s Workforce Intelligence Suite, you can plan with precision: balancing automation, reskilling, and hiring to meet your goals.

### Meet Ray, your AI Talent Advisor

Ray is your embedded, agentic AI consultant: built to help you understand and shape the future workforce. Built on robust ethical AI models, Ray accelerates workflows and delivers transparent, context-aware recommendations for faster, smarter decisions.

### Activate talent strategies faster

Move from planning to action with AI-powered sourcing, matching, and engagement – fully integrated into your existing HR systems. Beamery connects across your tech stack to unify workflows and accelerate the execution of your talent strategy.
Beamery is a strategic tool and a strategic investment that we made that is going to ultimately help us find the right talent at Salesforce that will allow us to innovate, deliver, and expand as a company.

## Unlock Data-Driven Workforce Decisions At Every Level

### Executives

Align your workforce strategy with your business vision. Ensure you have the right skills in place to meet your goals, navigate change, and lead with confidence.

### Human Resources

Proactively manage risk, better advise the business, and support talent development, by integrating unified skills data and ethical AI into your hiring, upskilling, and retention strategies.

### Recruiters

Find and hire the best people, faster. Use AI and actionable insights around candidate skills and potential to spot top talent, match them to roles, and build diverse, high-performing teams.

### Candidate Experiences

Discover relevant roles, and map out a clear path to career success. Our AI platform connects skills and ambitions with the right opportunities, to help people make their ideal next move.
