- Contract drafting and approval routing within AXDRAFT

## Create contract templates easily

## Build dynamic templates with AXDRAFT

Template creation and logic live inside AXDRAFT. Upload agreements, define clauses, and build guided templates that automatically adjust based on user input. AXDRAFT ensures consistency and compliance across every document.

## Generate contracts with automation

AXDRAFT handles drafting. Users answer guided questions to produce accurate, ready-to-review contracts. Once generated, every document syncs into ContractWorks for storage, tagging, and visibility.

## Collaborate seamlessly across projects

AXDRAFT enables fast, controlled collaboration with built-in redlining. Internal and external stakeholders can comment, suggest edits, and approve changes in real time. Every version is tracked within the workflow, maintaining accuracy and accountability through each negotiation. Once finalized, approved contracts automatically sync to ContractWorks for storage and visibility.
Projects operate seamlessly across both systems. AXDRAFT manages the workflow — from drafting to review and approval — while ContractWorks organizes project data, access controls, and historical records. Together, they keep every contract transparent, traceable, and on schedule.

## Approvals made easy

AXDRAFT manages review and approval routing. Define approvers, set conditions, and manage role-based access within each workflow. Approvals are completed in AXDRAFT, and final contracts are automatically moved to ContractWorks.
While AXDRAFT manages the workflow logic, access to final contract files and folders in the repository is controlled through ContractWorks. Admins can configure repository access—who can view, edit, or store each contract—ensuring compliance and data security. AXDRAFT access levels are set separately and are not automatically linked to ContractWorks roles.

## Power contracts through to signature

## Execute contracts with ContractWorks

AXDRAFT automates the pre-signing workflow. Once approved, contracts are executed through ContractWorks’ built-in ReadySign eSignature tools or integrations. AXDRAFT does not handle signing directly; all signature management occurs within ContractWorks.

## Store and manage contracts confidently

All contracts created and approved through AXDRAFT are securely stored in ContractWorks, which serves as the centralized contract repository. AXDRAFT maintains the document history, version control, and related audit trails, while ContractWorks manages long-term storage and repository-level access permissions.
ContractWorks manages all metadata, reminders, and tag-driven reporting. Dates, clauses, and obligations become searchable, reportable fields across your entire contract portfolio.

## Analyze and track important updates

All tracking and reporting are managed in ContractWorks. Dashboards provide a clear view of active contracts, renewal schedules, and team activity. AXDRAFT feeds completed contracts and related data into ContractWorks for ongoing analysis.
ContractWorks handles notifications and reminders. Automated alerts ensure your team never misses a renewal or expiration date.

## Smarter contracts. Faster business.

With AXDRAFT, powered by ContractWorks, you get automated contract creation, streamlined approvals, and full visibility through one connected platform. Simplify your process, eliminate manual steps, and bring your entire contract lifecycle into focus.
