## Build new creations from your old bricks

## Focus on creativity while Brickit guides you through the hard parts

Our instructions are just the beginning. There's plenty of room to experiment. Сhoose
        different colors, swap out bricks, and make up your own rules!

## Got a building idea? Let it go viral!

Submit your idea to Brickit and watch how others build on it, adding their own creative
          touches!

## Catalog every part you have. Build bigger

Find exact parts you need in seconds and explore advanced builds tailored for your bricks collection. All that, and more — with Pile[o]meter, an app for organizing your whole storage, powered by the best parts recognition technology there are.

## Brickit for Classes

A version of Brickit tailored for use in schools and camps. Social features are out,
          one-time purchase is in. An easy way to set up fun hands-on building classes for any K-8
          group.
