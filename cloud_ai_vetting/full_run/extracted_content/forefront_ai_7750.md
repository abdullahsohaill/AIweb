```markdown
  __3_

   -126
```

So, 129 divided by 42 is equal to 3 with a remainder of 3.

If you want to express it as a mixed number, it would be 3 and 3/42. To simplify the fraction, you can divide both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor, which is 3. So, the simplified fraction would be 1/14.

Thus, the final answer is 3 and 1/14, or 3.07142857 when expressed as a decimal (rounded to eight decimal places).
