## There’s an answer for every question about Wave

What is Wave?
Wave is a powerful AI note taker for iOS and Android. It records meetings, phone calls, and podcasts—then transcribes and summarizes everything for you so you never miss a detail.
How does Wave work?
Just press record. Wave captures your audio, transcribes it using AI, and generates a clean, searchable summary you can edit or export.
Can I access Wave on multiple devices?
Yes. Wave works on iPhone and Android devices, and you can also access your recordings and summaries from any computer using our web app. Everything stays synced across platforms.
Can I use Wave while using other apps or with my screen off?
Yes, Wave can record in the background, allowing you to use other apps or even when your screen is off.
Does Wave work offline?
You can record offline. Transcription and summarization will process once you’re back online.
What languages does Wave support?
Wave transcribes and summarizes in most major languages.
Is there a limit on recording time?
Yes, depending on your plan. The Free plan includes 30 minutes per month. Upgrade to Standard for 5 hours, or Pro for unlimited recording.
Can I share my transcripts and summaries?
Yes. You can copy, export, or share your notes directly from the app.
How accurate are the transcriptions?
Wave uses an industry-leading transcription engine with a very low word error rate. It delivers high-quality, speaker-labeled transcripts in most major languages.
Does Wave train its AI on my recordings or data?
No. Wave never uses your recordings, transcripts, or summaries to train AI models. Your data is private, encrypted, and fully owned by you.
How can I customize my summaries?
Once Wave has transcribed and summarized your audio, you can easily adjust the summary's length or format within the app.
What about my privacy?
Wave prioritizes your privacy. Read more about our commitment to your privacy in our Privacy Policy.
Can I use Wave for my team or purchase outside the app stores?
Yes. You can buy Wave subscriptions directly on the web, including options for team accounts with centralized billing. It's perfect for businesses that want to give multiple people access under one plan.
