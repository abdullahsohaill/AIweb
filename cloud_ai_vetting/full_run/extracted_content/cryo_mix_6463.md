## How Cryo Mix Works

From rough mix to release-ready in three clear steps - no plugins, no extra routing, fully in your browser.

### Upload your mix or stems

Drag & drop a stereo mix or individual stems (vocals, drums, instruments, FX, etc.).
Works in your browser on Mac, PC, tablet and mobile.
Fits into your existing workflow with Ableton, FL Studio, Logic Pro, Pro Tools, Reaper and more.

### Let the AI mix & master

Cryo Mix analyzes your audio, balances levels and shapes EQ and dynamics.
It applies an intelligent mastering chain so your track translates across headphones, speakers, cars and streaming platforms.
Cryo Mix studies your song, balances the elements, controls dynamics automatically and sonically adjusts mixing parameters with a cutting edge intelligent copilot.
It then curates the perfect mastering chain to give you the clearest, loudest and most consistent sound across speakers and streaming platforms.

### Review, refine and export

Listen to your original and the processed version side by side.
Adjust tone, loudness and details with presets, advanced controls, or by chatting with your mix in plain language.
Export a high-quality WAV that's ready for Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, TikTok and more.
