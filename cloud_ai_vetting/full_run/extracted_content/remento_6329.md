## How It Works

## Choose your questions

Pick from our expertly-curated prompts, or write your own. Or upload photos to spark specific memories. You (and your family) are in control of what gets asked.

## Record responses

Each week, your storyteller receives a new prompt via email or text. Simply record the response answer with video or audio only, your choice. No apps to download, no passwords to remember.

## Your book comes to life

Our Speech-to-Story™ technology transforms recordings into beautifully written stories - either cleaned transcripts or re-written prose.

## Scan to play

Every chapter includes a QR code. Scan and listen to their original voice recordings anytime you want to feel close again. Access the recordings forever.

Our Story
In 2017, my mom was diagnosed with cancer, and I suddenly felt an urgent need to preserve her voice. I bought her a memoir-writing book, but she never used it. “Writing feels like homework,” she said. So I pulled out my phone, hit record, and we just talked. I learned more than I ever could have imagined. That’s why we built Remento, the simplest way to preserve a parent's memories for the future. Because one day, having those stories and their voice will be more precious than you could ever know. From our family to yours, happy storytelling.
