## A CLM built for everyone

Our embedded contract lifecycle management software connects teams and tools together for maximum efficiency. From the initial request within your collaboration tools, AI-powered contract review in Word and smart repository storage, it’s the only way for your whole business to manage contracts.

### Self-serve contracting for the entire business

Pre-approved templates and workflows let business users create compliant, low-risk contracts directly in the tools they already use, saving time and freeing up legal.

### Speed up contract reviews with AI

Powered by SIA (Summize Intelligent Agents) and guided by your legal standards, our review functionality reviews, analyzes, redlines and redrafts your contract, providing the accuracy and speed you need for expert contract reviews. And it all happens in Microsoft Word.

### All your contracts, all in one place

Our smart repository centralizes storage, simplifies search and provides a single source of truth for all your contracts.

### Make smarter decisions with AI-powered contract analytics

Leverage AI to analyze contract data, spot risks and identify opportunities.

## Let AI do the heavy lifting

Think this is ChatGPT, think again! Our Summize Intelligent Agents (SIA) handle everything from drafting and rephrasing to redlining and summarizing. It’s secure, business-grade AI built for legal.
