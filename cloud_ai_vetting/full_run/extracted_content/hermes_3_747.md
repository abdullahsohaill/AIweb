## Hermes 3 Technical Report

## Abstract

Hermes 3, a neutrally-aligned instruct and tool use model with strong reasoning and creative capabilities, achieves top performance on public benchmarks.
Instruct (or "chat") tuned models have become the primary way in which most
people interact with large language models . As opposed to "base" or
"foundation" models, instruct-tuned models are optimized to respond to
imperative statements. We present Hermes 3, a neutrally-aligned generalist instruct and tool use model with strong reasoning and creative abilities . Its
largest version, Hermes 3 405B, achieves state of the art performance among
open weight models on several public benchmarks.
