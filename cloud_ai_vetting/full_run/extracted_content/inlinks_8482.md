## Audit and Optimize Internal Linking at Scale

Inlinks finds gaps in your internal linking across your site with a powerful entity-based audit. It shows the link opportunities you are missing , and can “magically” insert internal links within the main body text on your site. Links can be managed directly from the InLinks dashboard if you use our JavaScript integration option.
Alternatively, you can export the links for manual insertion.
Because we structure this internal linking based on entities, anchor text is varied and contextually relevant, not just exact match links.
