## Full Explanation

Mind Network is revolutionising Web3 and AI security by developing the first FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption) infrastructure, enabling true end-to-end encryption. Unlike conventional encryption methods, FHE allows computations on encrypted data without decryption, eliminating security vulnerabilities at every stage. This technology integrates seamlessly with Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZK), Multi-Party Computation (MPC), and Trusted Execution Environments (TEE) to extend HTTPS into HTTPZ, a next-generation, fully encrypted transfer and processing framework.
[LINK: FHE](/minddocs/research/fhe-research)
Mind Network’s mission is to create a zero-trust digital ecosystem where data remains encrypted throughout transmission and computation, ensuring the highest level of security for Web3 and AI applications.
- The Web2 Security Gap: HTTPS encrypts data in transit but exposes it upon reaching servers. As of December 2022, over half of the top websites had adopted HTTPS, yet user data remains accessible to banks, tech giants, and cloud providers, leaving it vulnerable to breaches and surveillance.

Mind Network addresses these challenges with HTTPZ , a quantum-resistant, fully encrypted internet protocol that keeps data encrypted during storage, transfer, and computation—eliminating reliance on centralised entities. This innovation enhances security across AI, DeFi, DePin, RWA, gaming, and beyond.

Decentralised AI presents significant security challenges, as computations occur across distributed and potentially untrusted nodes, increasing risks of data leaks and manipulation. Mind Network enables secure AI computations on encrypted data using FHE, ensuring sensitive information remains protected even in decentralised environments.
By embedding HTTPZ into crypto-based AI, modular chains, secure voting, and DePin, Mind Network delivers the first productised FHE infrastructure for Web3, bringing encryption-first AI and blockchain solutions to life. Mind Network leads advancements in cross-chain security, fair consensus, and privacy-preserving AI, shaping the future of Trusted AI and encrypted computing.
Mind Network has raised $12.5 million in funding from Binance Labs, Cogitent, Hashkey, Animoca Brands, Chainlink, and other top investors. It has also received two Ethereum Foundation research grants for its breakthroughs in FHE cryptography.
Partnering with industry leaders such as Zama , Mind Network is accelerating the adoption of FHE technology. It is the first project to implement TFHE-rs v1.0.0 (Zama's first production read FHE library) in real-world applications and has open-sourced multiple FHE-Rust codebases , including, FCN (FHE Consensus Network), MindChain, Swarms-rust and so on.
