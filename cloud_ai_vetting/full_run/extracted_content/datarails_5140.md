Datarails FP&A is a robust financial planning and analysis solution that enables finance team to benefit from consolidation, reporting, budgeting, and planning. Datarails Cash Management gives finance teams clear, real-time visibility into their cash position. It simplifies the complexities of managing multiple bank accounts by automatically categorizing transactions and providing up-to-date balances across entities. With built-in cash forecasting, Excel-based drill-down capabilities, and support for entity-level management, teams can make smarter decisions and stay ahead of their liquidity needs.
Datarails Month-End Close streamlines and simplifies the close process for finance teams. It brings all tasks, reconciliations, and documentation into one centralized workspace, making it easier to stay organized and on track. With built-in visibility across the entire close timeline and support for multi-entity and intercompany operations
