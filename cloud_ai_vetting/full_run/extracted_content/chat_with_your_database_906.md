## Chat with your database

Unlock the power of your data: Connect any Postgres database and let our AI dive deep. Chat to query, analyze, and even run operations - all from one intuitive interface. Experience the future of database interaction today!

## How It Works

User asks a question about their data in natural language
AI interprets the question and generates appropriate database queries
The system executes the generated queries on your database
AI analyzes the query results and generates insights
