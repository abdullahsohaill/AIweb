## Track Local SEO Performance Over Time

If you've ever searched for anything local, you're familiar with the Map Pack — three featured business profiles, neatly packaged together atop search results.
The Google Map Pack attracts a whopping 70 percent of clicks — meaning if your location is not included, it may as well be invisible.
Local Falcon's Share of Local Voice ( SoLV "solve" ) metric is based on how frequently your listing show up in the Map Pack for a search term and radius. The greater the SoLV, the greater your local search visibility. Automating scans using Campaigns will make it easy to see your SoLV improvements over time.
