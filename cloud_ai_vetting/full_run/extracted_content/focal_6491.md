## Features

### Generate from a Script

Bring your own script, and Focal will adapt it faithfully. Just an idea? We can help you turn it into a script first.

### Edit with Chat

Edit your script with commands like "Make this conversation shorter" or "replace this with a series of over-the-shoulder shots aimed at the person who is speaking."

### Precise Control

We support the traditional timeline editing tools you need to polish your work.

### Latest models

We provide all the features of the latest models, like video extension, frame interpolation, and more.

## FAQ

### Which models does Focal support?

Focal integrates best-in-class models for videos, images, and voices.
We support Veo , Seedance , Kling , Minimax , Luma , and Runway for video, GPT Image , Nano Banana (Pro) , Z-Image , and Seedream for images, ElevenLabs and OpenAI for voices, and ElevenLabs for sound effects.

### Are there any discounts available?

If you would like to do a project with Focal but cannot afford these prices, we occasionally sponsor projects. Please reach out to us at founders@focalml.com.

### Do you support consistent characters and locations?

Yes. You can generate and re-use characters and locations in your projects.

### Can I use my outputs commercially?

Anything you make on a paid plan is yours to use commercially. The free plan is for personal use only.

### I have another question. How do I get in touch?

You can talk directly to us through the Support button (in the top right corner) or reach out to us at founders@focalml.com.
