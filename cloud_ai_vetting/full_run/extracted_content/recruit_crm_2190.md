## Features built to give you the edge

### AI Sourcing

Use natural prompts to instantly find ideal candidates from LinkedIn and the web. Access enriched, ready-to-use profiles in seconds—no Boolean required. Source faster and smarter, with AI doing the heavy lifting.

### Calling

Make, record, and transcribe calls directly within Recruit CRM. Every call is logged, searchable, and tied to the candidate or client record. Stay organized, compliant, and connected—without leaving your CRM.

### AI Sequencing

Deliver personalized outreach via Email, LinkedIn, and SMS—completely on autopilot. Boost candidate response rates, reduce no-shows, and free up recruiter time by automating your follow-ups.
Let your outreach run in the background while you focus on building relationships.

### Submit Candidates to Clients

Submit shortlisted candidates via reports, client portals, or email in just seconds. Get real-time feedback and keep hiring managers perfectly aligned throughout the hiring process.
Accelerate decisions and deliver a seamless client experience.

### Chrome Extension

Source candidates directly from LinkedIn, Xing, ZoomInfo, and other platforms with one click. Automatically import profiles into Recruit CRM—no manual entry, no switching screens.
Sourcing is faster, smoother, and fully integrated into your workflow.

### Kanban Board

Track every candidate in your pipeline with a clean, visual hiring flow. Move candidates across stages, assign tasks, and manage your process from one central view.
It’s recruitment visibility and control—made simple.
