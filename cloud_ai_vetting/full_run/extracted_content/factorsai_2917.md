## Why is Demand Gen this messy?

Today, signals live across multiple tools – website visitor identification, ad platforms, sales intelligence, analytics, and more. Each holds a piece of the puzzle, but none shows the full picture.
But pipeline woes can’t be fixed by signals living in silos.
Factors unifies rich intent signals and powers them with advanced AI to make sense of it all. Agents then help you act—through better ads, sharper outreach, and clear visibility into what’s truly driving pipeline.
The result? Pipeline that consistently converts.
