- Support Automation and Troubleshooting Deliver 24/7 support and free up your team from having to answer the same questions and resolve repetitive requests. The AI voice bot can easily handle FAQs, provide information, and troubleshoot tech issues autonomously or route calls to your live reps without losing any context.
- Appointment Scheduling and Reminders Automate your entire appointment scheduling process. The voice bot can connect to your calendar software, check availability in real time, suggest suitable time slots, book appointments or demo calls, send confirmations, and deliver reminders – all without human intervention.
- Payment Reminders and Debt Collection Handle payment reminders automatically and boost your collection rates. The AI voice bot can remind customers about upcoming or overdue payments, present debt details, and even process payments securely over the phone while freeing your agents from making collection calls.

Instead of frustrating customers with outdated IVR menus like "Press 1 for support, 2 for sales", the bot can interact with users through a natural dialogue, creating a much better experience and improving customer satisfaction.

The bot can call your customers after a service interaction or a purchase to conduct a short satisfaction survey and capture more genuine open-ended feedback that you can use to improve your products or services.

The AI voice bot can check your ERP/inventory system and return an answer instantly. It can also find the nearest store where the product is available, suggest alternatives, and provide delivery timelines.

The voice bot can initiate outbound calls, handle pre-sales, qualify your leads through a series of pre-qualification questions, route quality leads to your sales agents, and log everything in your CRM.

It can make thousands of outreach calls to engage supporters, share your mission, securely collect donations over the phone, and sync all data with your donation management system.

- What is a contact center solution? A contact center solution is a software platform that helps businesses manage customer interactions across different channels from a single interface. That may include phone, email, SMS, live chat, social media, instant messaging apps, chatbots, and voice bots. It's basically a centralized hub where businesses can handle all customer communication, with the aim to deliver efficient and consistent service no matter what channels customers use.
- What is the difference between a call center and a contact center? The primary difference between a call center and a contact center is the communication channels they handle. Call centers handle voice-based interactions (inbound and outbound) through phone calls. And today's call centers are also increasingly integrating AI-powered voice bots that can manage inbound and outbound calls independently, without involving human reps. In contrast, contact centers typically support multiple communication channels, including voice and digital channels like email, chat, and social media.
- How is omnichannel different from multichannel in a contact center? Multichannel means offering customer support across multiple communication channels – but those channels often operate in silos and are managed separately. That ultimately means customer information, conversation history,  and context are not shared between channels. An omnichannel approach, on the other side, integrates all communication channels into a single, unified system. That gives agents a full picture of each customer's journey and enables them to deliver a more seamless experience. For customers – that means a more consistent support without having to repeat themselves even when they move between channels.
- How does an omnichannel contact center improve customer experience and agent productivity? A truly omnichannel contact center improves customer experience by allowing customers to interact with brands on their preferred channels, seamlessly transition between them, and get consistent service without having to repeat information. For agents, it boosts productivity by giving them the full context of customer interactions across all channels so they can resolve issues faster. And features like AI reply suggestions, conversation summaries, conversation notes, and CRM integration ensure reps can collaborate effectively and deliver more personalized responses.
