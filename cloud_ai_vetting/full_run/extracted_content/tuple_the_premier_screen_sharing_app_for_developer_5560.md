### Built for Pairing

## Engineers deserve sharp tools

Generic screen sharing tools just won't cut it. Tuple is purpose-built
    for an exceptional pairing experience.

### Ridiculously crisp audio and video

Tuple streams in super high resolution (up to 5K) so you can read your pair's tiny programming
      font.

### End-to-end encryption

All audio, video, and screen share data is end-to-end encrypted, and is never sent to Tuple's
      servers. We take your privacy extremely seriously.

### Seamless collaboration

Snappy remote control combined with clear audio and video make you feel like you're sitting
      right next to your pair.

### One click to swap who’s sharing

It just takes one click to switch who's sharing when you're on a call. Easily go back and
        forth with your pair on a tough problem.
