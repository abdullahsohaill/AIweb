## Discover Your Unique Eye Shape Among Various Types of Eyes

Understanding the different eye shapes is key to enhancing your natural beauty. Explore the diverse world of eye types and find your perfect match with our AI-powered eye shape analyzer.

### Almond Eyes

One of the most common eye shapes, almond eyes are characterized by a slight upward tilt and partially hidden irises. This versatile shape is adaptable for various makeup styles.

### Round Eyes

Round eyes are a distinctive type of eye shape featuring visible whites around the entire iris. This youthful shape appears larger and more open, ideal for dramatic eye makeup looks.

### Upturned Eyes

Also known as cat eyes, this eye shape has a natural lift at the outer corners. Upturned eyes create an exotic look and are perfect for winged eyeliner techniques.

### Downturned Eyes

This eye shape features a slight downward tilt at the outer corners, creating a gentle, approachable look. Specific makeup techniques can enhance and lift this eye shape beautifully.

### Wide-set Eyes

Wide-set eyes are a unique type of eye shape, spaced further apart than average. This eye shape creates an open, expansive look and offers ample lid space for creative makeup applications.

### Close-set Eyes

Close-set eyes are positioned nearer to each other, creating an intense gaze. This eye shape benefits from makeup techniques that create the illusion of more space between the eyes.
