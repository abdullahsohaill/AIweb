## Frequently Asked Questions

Dig deeper into the benefits, and characteristics of Braintrust AIR, the AI-powered recruitment app trusted by the world’s leading companies.
- Uncover hidden potential: Uses structured, competency-based interviews to go beyond resumes.
- Save time & effort: Automates scheduling, candidate interviewing, scoring, and initial filtering.
- Better candidate selection: Provides unbiased AI-generated scorecards and rank-ordering of top candidates.
- Scalability & access: Allows candidates to interview 24/7, supports global hiring, and broadens the talent pool.
- Significant reduction in recruiter time per hire
- Faster time-to-screening
- Higher completion rates and better candidate throughput
- Cost savings per hire
- Stronger candidate quality with less recruiter overhead
- Fairness by design: Scoring excludes personal identifiers and focuses on answers to job-relevant questions (no age, gender, name bias).
- Transparency & accountability: The scoring criteria are defined upfront, visible, and auditable by users.
- Inclusivity & accessibility: Supports multiple languages, accent comprehension, works on diverse devices, enables 24/7 access.
- Ethical oversight: Braintrust commits to ethical AI practices and follows a code of conduct for AI in hiring.
