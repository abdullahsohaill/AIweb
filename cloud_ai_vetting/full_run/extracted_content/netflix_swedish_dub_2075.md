## Watch Any Netflix Movie with Swedish Dubbing

Netflix Swedish Dub is a Chrome extension that dubs with AI in
              realtime any movie on netflix that has Swedish subtitles. For
              those who want to learn swedish but can't find anything
              interesting to watch.
