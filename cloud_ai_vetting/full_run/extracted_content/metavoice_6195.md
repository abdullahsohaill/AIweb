## We’re reimagining Voice AI

Today's Voice AI cannot handle real conversations. It requires walkie-talkie style turns, lacks empathy and has an inconsistent personality with little to no control. Developers can't build compelling experiences and users disengage. This limits Voice AI to simple receptionist tasks and basic customer support, blocking meaningful services (therapy, coaching, sales) where dialogue and emotional intelligence matter most. ‍ Scaling current tech does not work . We're re-building Voice AI to feel like you're talking to a friend – natural, familiar and emotionally aware. Our approach is a duplex speech-to-speech AI that learns conversation directly from data. That’s how we'll make voice the most natural way to interact with AI.
