### Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers

What is Foxy AI?
Foxy is an AI-powered platform that simplifies content creation. It helps you to generate ultra- realistic photos and videos of yourself in any setting to elevate your personal brand, grow as a creator, and monetize—without the need for photoshoots, expensive gear, or technical skills.
How does Foxy work?
Foxy uses advanced AI to make content creation effortless. Simply upload a few photos, and our AI builds a personalized model. You can then customize and generate images in any style or setting.
What makes Foxy different?
Foxy allows you to create high-quality, stunning photos in minutes. You can instantly generate content of yourself in any style or setting, giving you complete creative freedom while focusing on growing and monetizing your brand—without spending hours taking pictures.
Is Foxy only for content creators?
Not at all! While Foxy is great for content creators, it's also perfect for anyone looking to enhance their social media presence, create unique content, or explore new creative possibilities for themselves.
What type of content can I generate on Foxy?
Foxy lets you create all kinds of AI-generated content—just describe what you’re looking for or pick a preset to get started. Please note that adult content cannot be generated on Foxy. Our systems include strict safeguards to prevent this and ensure a safe, compliant experience. For more details, please refer to our Terms & Conditions.
Do I need technical skills to use Foxy?
Not at all! Foxy is designed to be simple and intuitive. Simply upload your picture, describe the content you want, and let the magic happen.
Is it safe to upload my photo to Foxy AI?
Yes, your privacy is our priority. The photos you upload are only used to create your personal AI and is securely processed and stored. The photos you create are only visible by you.
Can Foxy create realistic AI images of me if I have tattoos?
Yes! In most cases, our AI can generate highly realistic images of you with accurate tattoos. When creating your AI, please be sure to upload multiple photos that clearly showcase your tattoos from different angles and distances.
