## Seamless Handover from ChatBot to Human

Our new Live Chat / Human Handover feature gives your chatbot the ability to offer a seamless handoff to a human when a question falls outside of the knowledge you’ve provided.
If the chatbot can’t find a relevant answer, it politely asks the user if they’d like to speak with someone from your team. If they say yes, a notification is sent straight to your FastBots dashboard so a team member can take over the conversation in real time.
Whether it’s pre-sales questions, onboarding support, or customer service follow-ups, you’re now in full control of when and how real conversations happen—without losing the efficiency of AI.
