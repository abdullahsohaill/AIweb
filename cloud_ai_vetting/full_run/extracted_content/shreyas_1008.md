## About Me

I'm Shreyas. I like building things that feel simple, clean and smooth. I pay attention to tiny details, even something like a small hover animation or how a page moves. That is the part I enjoy the most. I started coding out of curiosity in my first year of college and it slowly became the thing I do every day.
I work on AI tools, SaaS products and quick MVPs for clients and creators. I enjoy taking an idea, shaping it, and turning it into something real that people can use. Right now I'm focused on TryScribe V2. It feels like the kind of tool I always wanted for myself.
