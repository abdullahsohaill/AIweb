Absolutely! Track follower growth, engagement, top posts, competitor insights, cross-channel reports, and more – all in one place.

Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, Threads, YouTube, X, Google Business Profile, Pinterest, and even more via mobile notifications.

### How can my clients access HeyOrca?

Set them up as external stakeholders so they can approve, reject, or give feedback – without logging in. Just send a secure shareable link, and you control what they see.

### What makes HeyOrca different from other social media management tools?

We make client collaboration easy, schedule content effortlessly, build engaged communities, and every plan comes with unlimited users – keeping your team on the same page.

### Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes! If you’re on a monthly subscription, any changes (like adding or removing a calendar) will be pro-rated and applied automatically. For biannual or annual subscriptions, changes will take effect at the start of your next billing period.
