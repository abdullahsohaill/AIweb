Making an interactive course with our AI Course Creator platform transforms a months-long process into a few simple steps. Define Your Topic (The Prompt) Input the Idea: Start with a simple instruction: a topic, a specific learning objective, or by uploading an existing document (like a PDF or training manual). Set the Scope: Specify your target audience and the core knowledge you want them to gain. 2. Generate the Course Outline Instant Structure: The {AI Course Creator analyzes your input and instantly generates a comprehensive, logical course outline (modules, lessons, and pages). Human Control: This outline is 100% editable. You are always the instructional designer; you can easily review, rearrange, and add your unique segments before proceeding. 3. AI-Powered eLearning Development Content Drafting: Based on the structure you confirm, the AI generates high-quality drafts for the lesson text and supportive material for each page. Enrich & Customize: Use the platform settings to guide the eLearning development. For instance, you can automatically add a specific learning objective to each page, generate relevant supporting images, or translate the content. 4. Get the Complete Course Built-in Elements : The AI automatically suggests and creates essential interactive elements, such as quiz questions and knowledge checks. Edit and Enrich: With the easiest elearning authoring platform and its drag & drop block structure, simply finalize the content as you wish. Enrich your courses with special blocks like “Image Hotspot”. Refine with Tools: For deeper engagement, leverage the AI Interaction Builder to transform plain text into dynamic elements like flashcards, tabs, and tables—all without manual formatting. 5. Review, Publish, and Deliver Final Review: Inject your unique voice, real-world examples, and final polish. The AI accelerates the drafting; your expertise completes the course. Publish Instantly: Once finalized, you can publish your interactive course instantly. We also provide a very flexible landing page builder that you can design instantly. You can also embed your courses or export to SCORM.
By following these steps, you eliminate the initial creative blocks and heavy manual lifting, ensuring your knowledge is turned into a beautiful, effective, and interactive course better and faster.

### What is a 'Mini-Course' and how does it relate to Micro-Learning?

From our launch, we’ve embraced the principle that micro-learning aligns perfectly with effective adult learning experience. This approach becomes especially valuable for customer training, partner training, and community training, where learners often have limited time and need digestible content. That’s why we designed our entire platform, both admin and learner interfaces, around creating courses that are naturally divided into bite-sized, focused segments. These ‘mini’ courses can allow learners to complete each piece without overwhelmed. However, ‘mini’ refers to our approach and structure, not limitations. Within our editor, there’s actually no restriction on the overall length of your course. If you prefer, you can create comprehensive training programs while maintaining that focused, digestible format that makes learning more effective.
