## Your data is not our business

Good Tape was built for confidentiality in collaboration with award-winning investigative journalists. All transcription files are stored securely in the EU, fully GDPR-compliant, and protected with AES-256 encryption. Unlike many other transcription tools, your recordings are never shared, sold, or used to train AI models. Good Tape is more than secure transcription software, it’s a commitment to privacy, transparency, and the highest ethical standards.

## We don’t promise 100% accuracy

Good Tape delivers some of the most accurate AI transcription available today. While no speech-to-text technology can guarantee 100% perfection, our system is trained to handle more than 100 languages, accents, fast speakers, and complex audio with a high degree of reliability. What makes Good Tape different is our focus on accuracy and transparency, we show you what AI can do, and tell you where the limits are. The result: transcripts that are as close to perfect as possible, so you spend less time editing and more time focusing on what matters the most.
