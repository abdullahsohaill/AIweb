Fast. Tactile. Dopamine-heavy. An operating system.

### MESSAGING

Signal/Noise split protects your attention. @mention a rfd, email or bug to share in channel. @assign tasks to people in messages. @link to
                            customer emails or code files.

### EMAIL

Superhuman shortcuts and speed x Cursor-like AI.
                            Mash e to triage emails, DMs, channels, and to-dos.
                            Conntect your existing Gmail or Google Workspace
                            account.

### DOCUMENTS

Notion-like docs at native-feeling speeds in the
                            browser: Rust CRDTs for collab x SolidJS frontend. @mention tasks, emails, and messages to
                            keep everything together.

### TASKS

Hit c t from anywhere to create a task @assign to your team and instantly @link to customer
                            emails, #bug-report messages, or code files.

### SPLITS

Macro has its own window manager. Just type cmd+\ to create a new split. A bigger screen can fit more
                            splits.

### SHORTCUTS

Everything has a shortcut. If you can't find it, you
                            can always hit cmd+~ to enter visor mode.

### THEMES

In-depth UI customization options like an IDE, with
                            a really cool color picker we built.
