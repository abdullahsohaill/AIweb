## From campaign to conversation to resolution , in one connected journey

### Step 1: Engage drives demand

Launch targeted WhatsApp and SMS campaigns promoting new credit cards to pre-qualified segments. Customers receive personalized offers with one-click responses.

### Step 2: AI agents handle volume

Replies route to AI Agents that authenticate the customer through Studio’s secure connections, explain terms, collect application data, and submit for approval without switching channels.

### Step 3: Live resolves complexity

For questions requiring human expertise, customers are seamlessly transferred to Live specialists. The specialist sees the full conversation history, campaign context, and customer data without asking the customer to repeat anything.

### Step 4: Studio orchestrates and governs

Studio connects to core systems for real-time account data, processes transactions compliantly, maintains audit trails, and ensures every action aligns with your institution’s policies.
