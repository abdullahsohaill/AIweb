## But what makes Nuelink different?

Automations is where Nuelink shines…

## Auto posts your products to social media

Automatically share products from various e-commerce stores to your social media in just minutes. Set it up once, and it runs forever, saving you hours weekly and maintains a consistent online presence on social media.
🔗 Add your store link. ✏️ Customize the caption using Nue AI and variables like {{link}}. 📲 Choose the social media platforms to post to. 🔁 Set a weekly schedule, like Mondays at 4:23 PM.
Nuelink will then import all your products and auto-post them on repeat following that schedule. And automatically create posts for any new products you add to your store in the future!

## Auto posts your blog posts and articles to social media

Automatically share your blog posts from WordPress, Medium and other blogging platforms to your social media accounts in a few steps.
🔗 Copy and add your blog or RSS link, ✏️ Customize the caption using Nue AI and variables like {{title}}. 📲 Select social media platforms to post to. 🔁 Set a weekly schedule, like Fridays at 2:30 PM.
Nuelink will then import all your articles and auto-post them on repeat following that schedule. It will also automatically import any new blog posts you add later and add them to the queue.

## Cross post your Instagram Reels to TikTok, YouTube and others automatically

Automatically share your Instagram reels to TikTok and others in one click:
🔗 Select the Instagram account to post from. 📲 Select social media platforms to post to, e.g. TikTok, YouTube, Pinterest etc.. ✅ Done! Nuelink will keep an eye on your Instagram and each time you post a Reel on it from the app, it will automatically cross post it to TikTok and others.

## Auto posts your podcast episodes to social media

Automatically share your podcast episodes from Spotify, Captivate and any RSS feed to your social media accounts in a few steps.
🔗 Copy and add your podcast or RSS link, ✏️ Customize the caption using Nue AI and variables like {{title}}. 📲 Select social media platforms to post to. 🔁 Set a weekly schedule, like Fridays at 2:30 PM.
Nuelink will then import all your podcasts and auto-post them on repeat following that schedule. It will also automatically import any new episodes you publish later and add them to the queue.
