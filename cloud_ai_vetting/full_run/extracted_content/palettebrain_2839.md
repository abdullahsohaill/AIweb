## W h a t ' s N e x t f o r P a l e t t e B r a i n ?

We're working hard around the clock to build the most useful macOS AI productivity supercharger. The following features are not available yet, but coming soon *

### Multiple cutting-edge AI models from in one macOS app

On PaletteBrain , you'll soon be able to use not only models by OpenAI, but also models from companies like Google, Meta, Mistral, Perplexity, Anthropic, xAI, and others.
Rather than using multiple apps, all your models will become available to you in one "super app" . These models will run through your Agent.so free or paid membership, without requiring separate API keys.

### Go beyond the basics with your own team of AI Agents

PaletteBrain will soon directly integrate with Agent.so to bring AI Agents straight to your macOS computer.
AI Agents can interact with third-party apps & services , help you with web research, help you scale your business or do work even faster.

### Chat with PDF & other types of files using our proprietary multi-modal RAG system

We're working on integrating the proprietary file analysis system powering platforms like Agent.so straight into PaletteBrain. Which will enable you to not only use your AI Agents, trained using links, sitemaps, or third-party integrations, but also chat with a variety of file formats by drag-and-dropping them into the chat:

### Image attachments via AI vision right inside of your macOS app

PaletteBrain will natively support image attachments, allowing you to drag and drop images from your computer, paste them via CMD + V, or simply select them as you run your command palette.
With AI Vision, you'll be able to easily take screenshots of things and paste them right into your chats and open up a whole new dimension of productivity. If you need AI vision, you can use Agent.so today.

### Import your conversations from ChatGPT & other apps

To make it even easier for you to keep all your AI conversations in one place, we are working on a system that allows you to easily import conversations from other apps, such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, Anthropic, Raycast, etc.
You will no longer have to keep switching between different apps to find older conversations. Keep all of them in one place.

### Folders, tagging, favorites, re-ordering and all the good stuff

PaletteBrain will add more ways to organize your conversations. Besides search, rename, and timestamp splits, which are live today, the new organization tools will allow you to keep chats organized and easy to find.
This will be an exciting foundation for the upcoming sharing features, which will allow you to share chats by link with your friends and co-workers.

### Sync your messages and conversations between devices

Not only will you be able to sync between your macOS computers privately via iCloud, but you will also be able to sync messages and conversations to your Agent.so account.
Meaning you will have the ability to take your conversations with you anywhere you have access to Agent.so, such as your phone, tablet, or web browser.
