Frequently Asked Questions
What tools are included in the JustDone platform?
JustDone is an all-in-one workspace designed for students and learners. It includes an AI Detector, AI Humanizer, Plagiarism Checker, Summarizer, Paraphraser, Grammar Checker, plus additional tools to support academic writing and research. Instead of switching between multiple apps, you can draft, rewrite, check originality, and refine your work in one workflow.
Who can benefit from using JustDone?
JustDone is built for learners who write and study regularly, especially students who need help with academic structure, clarity, citations, research-based writing, and originality checks. It’s useful for students writing essays, reports, and research papers, ESL learners, educators, tutors, and anyone who wants to study faster.
Can the output generated by JustDone be plagiarized?
JustDone generates original text, but plagiarism can still happen if you paste in copied material, forget to cite sources, or rely too heavily on one source when paraphrasing. That’s why we recommend using JustDone as part of an academic workflow, while still completing the final review, verification, and revision independently. This helps you submit work that’s not only original but also properly referenced.
Is JustDone capable of handling large-scale content generation projects?
Yes. JustDone can support everything from short homework tasks to long academic projects like research papers, literature reviews, and thesis chapters. No matter what type of assignment you are working with, the platform helps you generate drafts, refine sections, and keep your writing consistent at scale.
How accurate is the content generated by JustDone?
JustDone uses advanced AI models to support writing and study tasks, but AI should be treated like a draft assistant, not a final authority. For the best academic results, always verify facts and sources, adjust the text to match your course requirements, and add your own reasoning and examples.
Which models can JustDone AI check?
JustDone AI can analyze text that may have been generated or heavily assisted by the most widely used language models, including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Llama-based systems, as well as other AI writing tools.
How does JustDone ensure data privacy and protection?
We take privacy seriously because student writing often contains personal ideas, drafts, and academic work. JustDone uses security practices designed to protect your content and personal data. Your text is handled securely, and privacy is treated as a core part of the learning experience, so you can write and revise confidently.
Who owns the content produced using JustDone?
You keep full ownership of the work you create with JustDone. Think of JustDone as a writing and learning assistant: it helps you draft, revise, and improve your text, but the ideas and final submission belong to you.
Does JustDone store my text or use it to train models?
No. JustDone is designed with privacy in mind. So, your inputs are not used to train public models, and your content is not shared or added to public training datasets. Your text is processed to generate results (such as detection, rewriting, or summaries), but it remains private and is handled under strict data protection practices to support safe academic use.
Does JustDone provide customer support in case I need assistance?
Yes. Our support team can help you with technical questions, billing, and guidance on how to use JustDone effectively for studying and writing. If you’re unsure which tool to use, support can point you in the right direction.
