## What is the meaning of Data Services?

Core components of data service companies include:
- Data Collection – Gathering raw data from various sources
- Data Processing & Preparation – Cleaning and structuring data
- Data Annotation & Labeling – Adding tags, labels, or classifications to data
- Data Validation & Enrichment – Verifying accuracy, filling in missing data, or adding context
At clickworker, data services refer to providing custom, human-generated or human-validated data to support digital workflows, AI development, and business operations. These services are performed by clickworker’s global crowd via microtasks and can be tailored to virtually any scale or complexity.
