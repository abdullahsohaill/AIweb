## Commitment To Quality & Excellence

Fork Force is a proud and active member of the Australian Forklift and Industrial Trucks Association ( AFITA ).
The AFITA represents the interests of major suppliers of forklift trucks and associated equipment in Australia. The major focus of AFITA is to ensure the highest possible standards of performance, safety and client satisfaction.
The AFITA plays an active role in the development of Australian and International Standards for the forklift industry and in promoting the highest standards of safe use of equipment by producing guidance documents and through the conduct of annual National Forklift Safety Seminars.
The Hire and Rental Industry Association ( HIRA ) is the official industry association for hire and rental companies in Australia, providing benefits and services at both national and state levels.
HIRA holds Hire Industry Excellence Awards yearly recognising outstanding achievement and industry excellence. The Hire Industry Excellence Awards pay tribute to the exceptional standards of service and innovation displayed throughout the hire sector.
As part of our commitment to excellence, Fork Force was recognised by HIRA as the 2018 Rental Company of the Year .
