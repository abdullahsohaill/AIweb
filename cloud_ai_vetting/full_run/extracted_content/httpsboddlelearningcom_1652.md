### How Boddle works

Sign up for an account and easily add students with single sign-on or manually through your teacher/parent portal.
The game will teach them how to play. They will answer curriculum-aligned questions in order to collect pets, complete quests, and continue playing.
Either let the adaptive path progress your students or use the teacher/parent portal tools to assign specific content, view data, and manage gameplay.
