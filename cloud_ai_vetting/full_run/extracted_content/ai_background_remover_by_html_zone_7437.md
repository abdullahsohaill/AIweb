## FAQ

### What image formats are supported?

We support common image formats including JPG, PNG, WEBP, and AVIF.

### Why is the first processing slow?

The first processing requires downloading and initializing the AI model in your browser. Subsequent processing
      will be much faster.

### Is there an image size limit?

For optimal performance and browser stability, we recommend images under 10MB. Larger images may still work but
      could affect processing speed.
