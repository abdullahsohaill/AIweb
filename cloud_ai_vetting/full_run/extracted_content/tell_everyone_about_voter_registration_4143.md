## Am I Registered?

## “Am I Registered?” is the number one question of most voters.

U.S. Vote Foundation is committed to providing you the best voter services – or leading you to them if they are not available on our site.
Our experience has proven that the best source to consult to confirm your Voter Registration status is your state’s election website. No other service or third-party site has the latest, up-to-date real-time data.
To find out if your voter registration is active, we will direct you to the right place! All states, with just one exception*, have online look up tools available for voters to check the status of voter registration. Choose your state from the list to be taken directly to your state's voter registration confirmation  service.
*Voters in the state of Wyoming, you can contact your WY local election office to confirm your registration status.
