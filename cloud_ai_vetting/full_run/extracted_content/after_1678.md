**Page Title:** The Transformers: Comic Book Edition Limited Statues By Mbilia Studios - Transformers News - TFW2005
--------------------

Mbilia Studios website have been updated with the official release of their exclusive The Transformers: Comic Book Edition Limited statues.
We have 5 officially licensed statues: Optimus Prime, Bumblebee, Megatron, Starscream and Soundwave. Read on for details:
- Comic Book Edition Sculpture – 35 cm hand-crafted solid resin sculpture with a painted comic overlay, finished in automotive-grade paint and mounted on a brushed stainless-steel base. The base features the Autobot/Decepticon insignia and sculpture name engraved in Cybertronian, with each piece individually numbered.
- Limited Edition Comic Book Plate – Polished stainless-steel engraved comic plate, true comic size, with full-colour storyboard print on reverse. Supplied in a protective slab. Comic focus: Original Comic # (1987, US).
- Collectible Card – Limited-edition collectible card by Livio Ramondelli. Hand-signed for the first 100, unsigned for the rest. Each card arrives sealed in a protective slab.
- Character ID Card – A colorful, character ID card with limited-edition number.
- Presentation – Luxury black faux butterfly box with split Autobot/Decepticon metal logo, precision-cut cushioned interior fitted for sculpture, plate, and cards. Ships in protective outer packaging.
Each statue is limited to 500 units worldwide and priced $3800 USD each. See the promotional images attached to this news post after the break and then sound off on the 2005 Boards!
