### What is Final Round AI?

Final Round AI is an AI-powered platform that helps job seekers land their dream jobs faster. It offers three main tools: AI mock interviews for practice, AI Job Hunter that applies to jobs for you, and Interview Copilot that helps you during live interviews. The Interview Copilot gives you live transcription, smart answer suggestions, and structured responses without being detected, so you can interview with confidence.

### What is Final Round AI’s Interview Assistant?

Final Round AI’s Interview Assistant is a smart, real-time tool that supports you during live interviews. It provides instant answers, structured responses, and personalized guidance to help you stay confident, clear, and well-prepared throughout your interview.

### How to use Final Round AI?

It's super simple. You just sign up at finalroundai.com, upload your resume or choose the job role you're preparing for, and start using tools like the AI Resume Builder, Interview Copilot, or Auto Apply system. Everything is designed to be beginner-friendly, even if you're not tech-savvy.

### Is Final Round AI detectable during interviews?

No. With its new Stealth Mode feature, Final Round AI Interview Copilot is not detectable by the interviewer. It runs quietly in the background like a digital sticky note or teleprompter. It doesn't speak, type, or interfere with your screen share or camera. It's 100% safe and discreet for virtual interviews.
