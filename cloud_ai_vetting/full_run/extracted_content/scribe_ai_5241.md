## Data security

The security of your data is paramount. Scribe is committed to providing clear and straightforward information on AI and your data.
Scribe uses 3rd party AI providers, which are governed by a strict set of limitations on how your data can be used. Your data is never used to train their models.
Every 30 days, our AI service providers delete any Scribe user data they have processed for the purpose of providing services to you. Your data is not stored by them on a long-term basis.
Our service providers have contractually committed to meeting or exceeding Scribe's rigorous security controls. This includes data encryption, backups and retention measures.
