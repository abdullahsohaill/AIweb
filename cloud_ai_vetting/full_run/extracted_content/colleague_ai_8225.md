## Built on Research, With Schools and Teachers

Colleague AI isn’t a simple AI wrapper. It is a workflow-embedded tool for educators.
We are an AI platform for K–12 education developed and validated through federally funded research , with over $18M in competitive grants . Our work is conducted in partnership with leading researchers, teachers, schools, and districts to ensure our tools are pedagogically sound, safe, and effective at scale .
- Designed for scale across districts and schools

## AI That Supports Teaching—Without Replacing Professional Judgment

Colleague AI helps educators plan lessons, differentiate instruction, create assessments, grade, create assignments, provide feedback, reflect on student progress, fulfill individualized education plans (IEPs), and more—while keeping teachers fully in control.
Unlike point solutions, our tools are designed to align with curriculum, assessment, and data analytics across classrooms and grade levels to achieve continuous improvement and personalized learning.

## AI That Helps Students Learn—Not Just Finish Work

Our student-facing tools are built to reinforce understanding, guide problem-solving, and support independent learning—without turning AI into a shortcut.
Colleague AI emphasizes learning integrity, transparency, and age-appropriate use, aligned with school and district policies.
