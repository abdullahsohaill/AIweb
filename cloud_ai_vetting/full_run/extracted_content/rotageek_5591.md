## It's a rota...and a geek thing.

What is Rotageek? Put simply, we provide app-based workforce management and scheduling software solutions that help you plan labour to demand more efficiently. Whether you're scheduling staff on the shop floor or rostering doctors, we’ll help you create the perfect workforce schedule every time, broadcast it to your employees, fill and mitigate shift gaps, improve your labour to demand match and increase your sales or patient care.
Our workforce management solutions range from intuitive digital schedules to AI optimised rotas using forecasts from data points at your locations.
And with tools to book leave, track time and attendance, clock in and out for shifts, a self-serve employee app and integrations into HR & Payroll, we can provide an all-in-one workforce management solution.

## Solutions

Our Digital Scheduling solution includes the functionality you'd expect from an all-in-one workforce management solution. With digitised rota scheduling software at it's core, this workforce solution is ideal for businesses where labour scheduling is at the forefront of their operations.
Take your workforce management solution to the next level with Autoscheduling. One click, a million combinations – automatically create optimised schedules and rotas in seconds. Driven by AI, we'll create the best possible schedule, taking into account the needs of your business and the staff that are available.
Complete your workforce management solution with Forecasting. Using data points like footfall, historical transactions and key events and seasons, we'll forecast your customer demand and therefore the workforce labour needed to optimise labour costs and provide a great customer experience.

## FAQs

- What makes Rotageek's rota software different? At Rotageek, we're not just about schedules; we're about changing the scheduling process for the better. Our rota software is more than a tool, it's a solution designed to empower your team and revolutionise how you plan your workforce. By using our app-based platform, you're crafting a smarter, fairer, and more efficient way to match labour with demand. It's about ensuring that every shift is perfectly planned, communicated, and executed, keeping your team up to date and in sync.
- How does Rotageek combine employee availability, payroll systems, and managing leave? Our platform acts as a bridge, connecting the dots between your team's needs and your operational demands. With our intuitive integrations into HR & Payroll software, we ensure that every hour worked is an hour paid, and every leave requested is managed.
- How does Rotageek staff rota software use AI to schedule shifts? Powered by AI and driven by real-time data, our solution crafts schedules that are not just guesses but precise predictions of what your business needs. Whether it's understanding footfall or forecasting demand, our software ensures that every shift is not just filled, but optimised.
- How can Rotageek help manage leave and employee schedules? At Rotageek, we believe that managing your team's time should be straightforward, not a struggle. Our user-friendly interface makes managing leave and employee schedules as easy as a few clicks. With our self-serve employee app, your team can take control of their availability, request time off, and even swap shifts without stress.
