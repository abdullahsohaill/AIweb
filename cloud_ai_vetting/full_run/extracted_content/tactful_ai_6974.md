## Frequently asked questions

### What is a cloud contact centre solution?

A cloud contact centre solution is a customer service platform hosted in the cloud, allowing businesses to manage communications over channels like phone, email, chat, and social media without on-site hardware. It offers scalability, flexibility, and advanced features to enhance customer experience.

### What are the cloud contact centre providers?

Cloud contact centre providers offer platforms for managing customer interactions online that deliver scalable, omnichannel customer support solutions without the need for traditional on-premise infrastructure. Tactful AI is one of the leading providers that brings enterprise capabilities to SMEs. However, other providers are designed for large enterprises only like Amazon Connect, Genesys Cloud CX, and Cisco Webex Contact Centre.

### What is the difference between cloud contact centre and on-premise contact centre?

A cloud contact centre is hosted online, providing access from anywhere and easy scalability, while an on-premise contact centre requires physical hardware and maintenance at a specific location. Cloud solutions reduce upfront costs, offer automatic updates, and enhance flexibility compared to on-premise systems.

### How to improve customer experience using cloud contact centre technology?

Enhance customer experience by using cloud contact centre technology to provide seamless, omnichannel support. The cloud enables agents to access customer data in real-time, personalise interactions, and utilise AI-powered tools like chatbots, leading to faster resolutions and higher customer satisfaction.

### How to migrate to cloud contact centre?

To migrate to a cloud contact centre, evaluate your current systems, select a compatible cloud provider, and develop a detailed migration plan. Address data transfer, integrate existing tools, train staff, and test thoroughly to ensure a smooth transition with minimal service disruption.
