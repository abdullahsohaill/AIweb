## The Practical Training Story

### Philosophy

There is a difference between exercising and training. Exercising is great, but if you want to make progress and achieve your goals, you need to train.
But strength training effectively is challenging. It requires personalization, expertise, consistent feedback, and ongoing support to achieve optimal results.
We want to make strength training more accessible and simple with the help of AI, empowering individuals to reach their full potential.

### Solutions

- It's easy to exercise in the gym, but hard to start effective training. We help create effective and intelligent training programs in less than a minute.
- Training needs to be personalized with quick feedback and adjustments. We provide real-time adaptations based on your progress and feedback.
- Not everyone needs or wants a personal trainer. We offer an affordable alternative that provides personalized guidance without human intervention.
- Many fitness apps and solutions are complicated and cluttered. We provide a simple and clean training experience, focusing on what matters most.

### Roadmap

- Personalized intelligent training programs
