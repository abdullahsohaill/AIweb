## Features

Everything you need for secure, fast file sharing across your local network.

### Cross-platform

LocalSend is available for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS.

### Secure

End-to-end encryption ensures that only you and the recipient can access your files.

### No Internet Required

Works completely offline. Your data never leaves your local network.

### Blazingly Fast

Transfer files at the maximum speed of your WiFi network. No bandwidth limits.

## How It Works

Zero configuration. Just install and start sharing.

### Install & Open

Download LocalSend on all your devices. No account, no login, no servers.

### Select Files

Choose photos, videos, documents, or text. Any file type is supported.

### Tap to Send

Tap the nearby device. The transfer happens instantly over your local network.
