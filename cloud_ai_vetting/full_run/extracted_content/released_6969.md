## Designed for security and scale.

Sleep soundly with state-of-the-art user management and top-tier security, all seamlessly powered by the Atlassian platform. Say goodbye to the hassle of maintaining a separate platform, provisioning users, and syncing data.

### SOC 2 Type 2

Released is SOC 2 type 2 certified. We conduct regular third-party audits and penetration tests. https://trust.released.so

### SAML SSO

Automate user provisioning, and sync with Active Directory when using Atlassian Access .

### User management

No separate app to log into. No separate user-base to manage. Released seamlessly integrates with your Atlassian user management and security practices.

### Encryption

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
Your data is never stored or used for training the AI model.
