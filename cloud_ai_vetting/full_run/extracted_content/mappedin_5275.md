## Frequently asked questions

- What is indoor map software? Indoor map software lets teams turn building floor plans into interactive, searchable maps for wayfinding, operations, and analytics.

### What is indoor map software?

Indoor map software lets teams turn building floor plans into interactive, searchable maps for wayfinding, operations, and analytics.
- How do you make an indoor navigation map? To create an indoor navigation map, start by gathering accurate floor plans (preferably CAD files), identify key points of interest, and collect detailed location data. Use indoor mapping software like Mappedin to transform these into interactive digital maps, then geo-reference them for real-world accuracy and keep them updated as your venue changes.

- How can I configure wayfinding in indoor mapping? Wayfinding is configured by defining routes and points of interest within your digital map using mapping software or SDKs. You can customize navigation paths, enable turn-by-turn directions, and integrate indoor positioning for real-time guidance. Updates and branding can be managed through an easy-to-use map editor or APIs for seamless user experiences.
