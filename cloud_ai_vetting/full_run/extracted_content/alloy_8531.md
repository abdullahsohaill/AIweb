### Frequently asked questions

How do I capture my product?
Add the browser extension and open it on any page of your app. After you capture the page, you'll instantly see a preview and can start prototyping. Capturing more pages builds your library, generating even better results.
How is Alloy different from app builders like Lovable?
Prototypes in Alloy look exactly like your real product. Rather than pasting in screenshots and getting off-brand results, Alloy understands your product, component library, and design system. Everything you prototype looks like something your designer made, automatically.
Is there any setup required?
None! The magic of Alloy is that you can capture your product instantly, and start prototyping right away. No need to import a design system, wrangle dependencies, or use engineering tools.
Can I import from Figma?
Not right now, but we're working on it. For now you'll get the best results by capturing your actual web app, not Figma. You can export your Alloy prototype to Figma at any time by clicking the Figma button in the top right of your prototype.
Does Alloy generate code that I can use?
Yes, your Alloy prototype is a React app that can be exported. Please note that this feature requires our Pro plan.
