### How can I use a trip planner to organize my travels?

Add destinations, build your itinerary with activities, and upload bookings or tickets. Keep everything in one place, and collaborate with others to finalize plans. A trip planner makes it easy to stay organized and flexible with map-related tools to organize travel information and reservation details. This keeps you from digging up confirmation emails in your inbox or gmail, so you can have your best travel experience with minimal delays.

### Can I plan multi-stop trips with a free collaborative trip booking & planning app?

Yes! Tools like Pilot let you add multiple destinations, organize activities, and manage bookings for each location. Whether it’s a road trip or international tour, you can plan it all for free, organzing all transportation options.

### What is a trip booking & planning app, and how does it work?

A trip booking and planner app helps you book and organize trip details in one place. You can add destinations, plan daily activities, manage bookings, and store documents. Tools like Pilot also have important features like those that suggest activities, allow collaboration, and offer offline access for seamless trip management. They make itinerary organization easy, and let you checkout for items like hotels, so everything stays organized. Pilot also gets special rates on hotels that users take advantage of as members.

### What features should a good travel planner have?

Look for tools to build itineraries, manage bookings, and store documents. Features like collaboration, AI suggestions, offline access if you don't have internet, and route mapping make planning easier and more enjoyable.
