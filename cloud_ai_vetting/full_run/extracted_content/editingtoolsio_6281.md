### Streaming Platforms & Broadcasters

Our tools are primarily used by post-production companies and freelancers producing content for national an international audiences.

### Plugins

Listify is one of our world-class plugins for Adobe Premiere Pro that lets users manage and create all kind of lists directly inside Premiere Pro. This is a huge time saver for anyone who has to deal with rights management. Filtering clips and creating VFX sheets, ADR sheets, music cue sheets, or lists of stock footage from a sequence is a quick and easy process with Listify. It has saved countless hours of work on over 100 productions. We also offer a range of other plugins to optimize other workflows.

### Subtitles

Subtitles are not only important in international productions, but sometimes already in the editing process of a film project. We offer a powerful tool, developed in collaboration with industry professionals, to verify, modify and convert subtitles for every conceivable use case. For multi-language productions, we offer a subtitle translation tool with one of the best algorithms available.

### Markers

Transferring comments on a video version from one software to another should be a simple thing. With our Marker Converter it is easy to transfer the comments from the reviewing platform to any editing software. The Marker Converter is a powerful tool for exchanging comments between different platforms and formats.

### Music Cue Sheets

Cue sheets play an important role in the licensing of music within a project. Together with our production music partners, we offer a powerful tool to create cue sheets directly from an edit sequence, provide metadata for over 10 million tracks, and export complex Excel spreadsheets or documents in seconds. Our Cue Sheet Manager offers a wide range of custom export presets, including CISAC and APRA standards.

### Live Timecode Notes

Create comments and notes with real-time timecode for live events, studio recordings, concerts, live broadcasts, video streams, meetings or other events, which can then be exported as markers for an editing program or a format such as CSV for Excel or Google Sheets.

### ALE, EDL, XML & FCPXML

Our EDL tool can convert EDL, XML and FCPXML files to spreadsheets, CSV, or Excel files. It can also modify and repair files. Our ALE tools provide features that make it easy to work with metadata from the set to post-production. Need to merge information from ALE into an EDL? No problem, we have a tool for that too.

### Colorize Videos and Photos

We offer high-end GPU servers to colorize videos or photos from black and white to color. Reimagine the past and bring your ancestors and historic videos and photos back to life. The algorithms have been trained on millions of real photos to understand the world of color.

### Clean Audio Files

Recording on a noisy street, in a crowd, or in a windy forest can create a lot of distracting background noise if the situation or microphones are not the best. We offer an online tool to remove distracting background noise from voice recordings. For music, we also offer a tool to split a file into audio stems for instrumental or individual instruments such as piano, guitar, drums, strings, and more. Giving you more creative control over a mixed audio track.

### Loudness Normalizer

You can check a mix in your browser with our Loudness Analyzer. It can not only create a loudness report of your audio mix, but also normalize a track to different standards for R128 or web mixes and output a new normalized audio file.

### Native macOS apps

We support small developers with native post-production applications such as the free app Lutme, a tool for easily previewing LUT files without the need of an editing software. Apply a .lut to any image and use the slider to adjust the intensity of the look.
