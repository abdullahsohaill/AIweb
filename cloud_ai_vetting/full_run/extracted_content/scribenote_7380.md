## Effortless notes in 3 simple steps:

### Speak freely on any device

Use Scribenote to record conversations, appointments, or phone calls from your phone, tablet, or laptop.

### Get accurate notes instantly

Scribenote converts your speech into clear, structured notes in SOAP and other formats.

### Export to your clinic's records

Edit your notes as needed in just a few clicks, and then integrate them into your clinic's PIMS with PIMSPal Pro or Widget Mode Pro .
