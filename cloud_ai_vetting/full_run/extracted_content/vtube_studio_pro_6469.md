### VTube Studio

VTube Studio is an app for Virtual YouTubers that makes it easy and fun to bring your own Live2D models to life.

### Accurate

Thanks to webcam and iPhone face tracking, VTube Studio provides accurate control over your Live2D model, including eye-tracking and winking (might have to practice that one a bit though) VTube Studio now also supports hand tracking!

### Fun

VTube Studio can do everything you'll need and more! Hotkeys to control everything in your scene, microphone-lipsync, animated PNG props tracking your model, post-processing VFX and MUCH MORE!!

### Collab

VTube Studio now has online multiplayer! Play with your friends and have your model appear on their screen with super-low latency!!

### Integration

VTube Studio offers easy integration for Twitch. Let your viewers throw emotes at you or trigger hotkeys with redeems, subs, chat commands and more!!

### Community

Need help? Wanna share something you made? Request a feature? People in our Community Discord server are there to help you!

## Download VTube Studio

VTube Studio is available for iOS (iPhone/iPad), Android and Steam (PC/Mac). VTube Studio supports face tracking via webcam (using OpenSeeFace) or by using a connected iPhone/Android device as face tracker.
On iOS, you need a device with FaceID or the Apple A12 (or newer) chip.
On Android, you need a phone that supports Google ARCore.
