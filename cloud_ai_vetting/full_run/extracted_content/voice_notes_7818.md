## Use cases

### Personal life

Voicenotes, this year’s Bootstrapped winner, is one of those ‘how did I live without this?’ tools, turning your voice memos into searchable notes.
I LOVE the WhatsApp integration... I tried it immediately and it almost made me crying. I took an old vocal note on WhatsApp from my youngest daughter and shared it to the Voicenotes WhatsApp chat I have and... bang onto Voicenotes and forever with me :)

### Teams & Work

Brilliant assistant . Been using this to take my meeting minutes and it’s so good at capturing all of the points and making me much more efficient in my life!
Exactly what I needed to record and transcribe in-person meetings . This has met the need at a better price point (and with more effective single case use) than just about anything else.
