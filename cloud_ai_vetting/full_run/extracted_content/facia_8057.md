## Real Time Detection for Every Use Case

### Liveness Detection for Customer Onboarding

Detect spoofing, replay attempts, and AI-generated faces during digital onboarding. Facia strengthens existing KYC flows by adding a modern, deepfake-aware liveness layer that improves verification accuracy without adding friction.

### Deepfake Detection in Identity Verification

Detect deepfake images, manipulated videos, synthetic selfies, and face swaps throughout the KYC journey. Facia flags manipulated media in real time so verification teams can trust the authenticity of every face they review.

### Video Call Deepfake Detection

Detect synthetic faces and real-time deepfake overlays during live video, confidential meetings, interviews, or support calls. Facia surfaces deepfake signals instantly, helping teams evaluate the legitimacy of a live session with confidence.

### Step-Up Authentication for High-Risk Actions

Detect risk signals during critical account actions and trigger face-based step-up verification when needed. Facia provides real-time liveness detection that confirms the user's genuineness before sensitive operations continue.

### Biometric Enrollment & Re-Verification

Detect spoofing attempts, mismatches, and synthetic identities during biometric enrollment or recovery flows. Facia helps enterprises ensure that only genuine users are added to their biometric systems and re-verified later.

### Surveillance & Access Security Detection

Detect persons of interest across live video streams and identify spoofing attempts at secure entry points. Facia provides real-time detection signals that support faster decision-making for security and access control teams.
