## How It Works

### Resonance

Quantifies how well a piece of content aligns with your campaign’s intent.Qortex uses NLP and computer vision models trained to detect tone, themes, sentiment, pacing, and emotional context within each frame. These signals are combined into a single resonance score that identifies the moments most likely to drive impact.

### Premium Rating

Evaluates the quality and suitability of each video environment.Qortex applies IAB content taxonomy, brand suitability standards, and custom brand filters to measure factors such as production quality, topic alignment, and contextual risk — ensuring your ads show up only in environments that meet your standards.

### Inferred Audiences

Expands your reach by identifying patterns in intent-rich video consumption.Qortex analyzes aggregated video-level behavior, content themes, and overlapping topic clusters to surface audiences united by shared interests, enabling scalable audience extension without relying on user IDs or personal identifiers .
