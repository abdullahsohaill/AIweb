## Why IT Finance Still Lacks Control

Most enterprises still treat IT as a cost center, not a strategic lever. Data is scattered across tools, reporting cycles lag reality, and cost visibility depends on Excel. The result: unclear ownership, reactive decisions, and widening gaps between IT, Finance, and the business.
Without IT Financial Management Software, companies experience:
- Fragmented systems: cost, usage, and budget data never align.
- Low visibility: service costs can’t be traced or defended.
- Reactive budgeting: plans are static; forecasts unreliable.
- Manual billing: showback and chargeback drain time and trust.
- No shared language: IT value isn’t measured in business terms.
