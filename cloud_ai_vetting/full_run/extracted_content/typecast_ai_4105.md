## Typecast SSFM: Unparalleled speech synthesis technology

Our Speech Synthesis Foundation Model uses advanced AI modeling for unparalleled naturalness and control in AI voice generator performance.

### Voices that don’t just talk. They perform.

Our proprietary SSFM achieves lifelike speech quality and can reproduce both unique speaker characteristics and emotions accurately, maintaining naturalness across different sentences.

### Your voiceovers won’t all sound the same, and that’s a good thing.

Typecast’s foundational model adds natural variation to each line, changing rhythm and flow to mimic how people really speak. That means more expressive, less repetitive delivery, ideal for audiobooks, voice prompts, and anything else that needs vocal range.

Easily adjust tone, pace, pitch, emphasis, and pronunciation right in the editor. Whether you want to slow things down for impact or amp up the emotion, you get full creative control with zero friction.
