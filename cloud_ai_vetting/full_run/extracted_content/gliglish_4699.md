## 06 Research

Gliglish works. And we have the research to prove it.

### Students improved their English speaking skills by over 75%

Students improved their English speaking skills by over 75%
A study by Gualán & Ramírez (2024) measured pre- and post-test results for students using Gliglish, finding significant gains across all core speaking competencies.
Grammar: mean scores improved from 1.00 to 1.76, indicating enhanced precision and grammatical usage.
Pronunciation: the results increased from 0.73 to 1.50, showing more confident and articulate pronunciation by the students.
Fluency: scores increased from 0.89 to 1.70, demonstrating more fluid and reliable language use.
Comprehension: students' understanding and response to speaking practice improved, as evidenced by the increase from 1.23 to 1.70.
Vocabulary: increased lexical richness and diversity were reflected in scores, which elevated from 0.82 to 1.59.
In conclusion the app's effectiveness is demonstrated by the over 75% improvement in overall score from 4.69 to 8.24 .

### Fluency increased by +23% as students spoke at a better pace and hesitated less

Fluency increased by +23% as students spoke at a better pace and hesitated less
In an 8-week program, researchers Thao, Ly, Thu & Kien (2025) observed that students using Gliglish showed a steady climb in speaking scores.
The rise in fluency scores from week 1 to week 8 suggests that using Gliglish assists students in enhancing various aspects of their fluency, such as pace, pausing , and hesitation.
This study's conclusion recommends integrating this Al tool into the speaking practice to enhance students' speaking fluency.

### Confidence skyrockets. After just a few sessions, the number of confident students jumped from 6 to 21

Confidence skyrockets. After just a few sessions, the number of confident students jumped from 6 to 21
A classroom action research study by Radhiyya, Nasution & Ginting (2025) measured improvements in both proficiency and confidence, noting a dramatic reduction in speaking anxiety:
❌ Before: "I'm not confident at all. I feel scared of making mistakes and being laughed at by my friends." ✅ After: "I feel more confident now. Even if I make mistakes, I just keep speaking and learning from them."
Findings indicate a 19.25% improvement in speaking scores , increased student participation , and reduced speaking anxiety .

### Gliglish motivates students to practice more on their own time

Gliglish motivates students to practice more on their own time
The same study noted that, even with no mandatory homework, Gliglish inspired learners to continue practicing voluntarily, with after-school use jumping from 10 to 25 students.
❌ Before: "I rarely practice speaking outside the classroom, only when I have homework." ✅ After: "Now I use AI Gliglish almost every day. It's easy to access and helps me practice more."
Before the intervention, only 10 students practiced speaking English outside the classroom. After using AI Gliglish, 25 students developed a habit of practicing regularly.
