## Key Features of SuperChat

### End-to-End Encryption

All messages are encrypted using advanced cryptographic techniques, ensuring only the intended recipient can read them.

### Decentralized and Secure

Built on the Ethereum blockchain, SuperChat eliminates reliance on central servers, ensuring transparency and security.

### Get Paid for Receiving Messages

Set a fee for others to message you. Every time someone sends you a message, you earn the fee in ETH.

## How to Use SuperChat

### Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button to connect your Ethereum wallet (e.g., MetaMask).

### Set Fee for Messages

Set a fee (in ETH) for others to send you messages. Once set, click "Register."

### Send a Message

Enter the receiver's address, type your message, and click "Send Message." Ensure you pay the required fee.

### Check Your Inbox

Click "Load Messages" to view your received messages. Recent messages appear at the top.

### Disconnect Wallet

When done, click "Disconnect Account" to log out securely.
