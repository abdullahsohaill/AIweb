## Optimise¹ & Contextual¹

Jacquard’s decisioning engines—Contextual¹ and Optimise¹—ensure the right language content is used for performance, relevance, diversity and ongoing learning. Contextual¹ activates data for personalisation with or without merge-tags, and selects message variants based on real-time audience signals. Optimise¹dynamically and continually tracks message engagement  and automatically deploys the best variant. Together, they help teams scale 1:1 messaging and get more from every send, every campaign.
Key Features:
- Contextual¹ selects message variants based on user behaviour, time, device, and more
- Optimise¹ deploys top-performing messages automatically
- Works across Push, Email, In-App, SMS, Web and more
- Reduces manual testing and operational lift
- Maximises performance while preserving brand control
