## Organization meets Inspiration

### The Codex, a wiki that truly keeps track

Build up and refine all your characters, places, lore and more.
        The Codex automatically keeps track and links them for a true insight into your world.
        It's integral for brainstorming, writing and reviewing.

### See your work in a different light

It's easy to miss plot holes, inconsistencies with the world and crucial moments in your story.
        The different planning modes give you a unique ability to pinpoint issues early - and later on.

### Share across books

Your Codex can be shared across books in a series, so you don't have to copy-paste or enter the same information over and over again.

### Collaborate with others

If you're an author pair or just need to invite a friend,
        proofreader or editor to your story or series,
        Novelcrafter has many collaboration options - including teams for writing groups!
