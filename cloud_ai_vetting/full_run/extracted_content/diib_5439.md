### FAQs

### Is diib a SEO checker?

Yes, diib is a SEO checker, but diib also does much more than just scan your SEO. We look at and monitor the overall health of your website (speed, security, core vitals), we help you track competitors and benchmark against their traffic and keywords. Moving beyond SEO, diib looks at your social data from Facebook and Instagram, and even analyzes local map rankings! Last, we provide our users with actionable insights on how to grow their website traffic and improve conversions. Think of us as an SEO tool but also as an overall growth tool focused on growing your website traffic, improving your rankings, and monitoring your website.

### What is an SEO checker?

A SEO checker is a type of SEO tool that analyses your website’s traffic and keyword rankings as well as does a technical audit of your website’s overall structure. diib does all of this and more. diib’s proprietary technology goes deeper than just scanning your external website and giving you a quick SEO score. diib uses your real data and even your competitors’ keyword and backlink data to give you deep insights into how to quickly improve your SEO. We also automatically provide you with easy-to-understand suggestions on how to improve in each area.

### How often should you use a SEO checker?

A modern automated SEO checker like diib will automatically scan your website and data daily. Fluctuations in keyword ranking positions and competitors rankings can quickly impact your traffic. Additionally, diib will automatically look for keywords that you rank for that we feel you can already rank higher for and alert you when we find an opportunity.

### Do SEO checkers test for things like mobile speed and security?

Yes, keyword rankings are not just based on the quality of your content and the authority of your website. Google wants to help searchers find the best answer to their “search query”. Next, they want to make sure that when the searcher visits your website, they have a great and safe experience. This means that your website needs an active SSL and is secure, it needs to load fast, and have good core vitals. diib is an SEO tool that checks for all these issues and more on a regular basis. Fact, if your website isn’t fast and secure Google is not ever going to rank it very high so be sure to monitor it constantly with an SEO tool like diib.

At the end of the day the business owner using any SEO tools will still need to write high-quality content about their business, products, and solutions. People use Google to search for answers and in most cases those answers are going to come in the form of content on a website. Some people choose to hire an SEO company to do this for them as well as keyword research. With diib you can learn how to do SEO yourself and we’ll even help you find the right keywords to write about, but someone will still need to write the content. We have growth experts that can assist you with this as well. diib also is a global SEO company and if you are too busy to do our automated recommendations, we are happy to help. If you are already working with an SEO company, we’d suggest talking to them about the suggestions diib provides to you.

### What is a SEO company?

Seo companies help their clients rank higher in search engines like Google for keywords that are important to their business. SEO companies come in all forms and shapes. Some are very expensive and specialize in helping the world’s largest companies. There are also local SEO companies that tend to focus on helping local business rank in Google maps and for easier local terms. Most SEO companies will do periodic SEO audits and checks of their clients’ websites and ensure that everything is running smoothly, and all the important pages are optimized. Many will build or buy backlinks for their clients to increase their clients’ website authorities. One of the most important things an SEO company needs to do is add high-quality, optimized content to their clients websites.
