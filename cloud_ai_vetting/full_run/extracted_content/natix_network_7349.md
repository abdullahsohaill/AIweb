## The Mission Behind NATIX

Decentralized multi-camera data collection to support Physical AI, Autonomous Driving, and advanced maps

### Physical AI data Economy

NATIX incentives users to map their surroundings using their smartphone, or Tesla 360° cameras. This data powers Physical AI, Autonomous Driving, and advanced map making.

## How it works

The fastest, cheapest and most scalable way to crowdsource multi-camera data and create a digital twin of our world

### Data Collection

Our multi-camera network from Tesla vehicles and smartphones collects real-world visual and geospatial data. The data collected is free of any personally identifiable information.

### Data Validation

NATIX Network technology employs multiple data validation techniques to confirm the accuracy and authenticity of the data, ensuring its reliability and trustworthiness.

### Data Monetization

The data collected is monetized and used to power superior mapmaking and Physical AI products. Market demand determines the data worth, leading to network contributors being rewarded for their participation.
