## Transform your business with connected intelligent solutions at the Edge.

Add intelligence to existing legacy cameras and sensor installations with an Edge AI Box solution. Maximize investments and avoid costly upgrades and installation costs. Test and deploy new AI applications easier and enable faster deployments.
Easily add machine learning models that run power efficiently including object detection and recognition, people detection and counting and more, with the ability to run simultaneous AI models all across the same network.
Deliver real-time insights to enable on-demand decisions by processing data at the source. Reduce dependency on the cloud keeping data on-premise.
Build better privacy, security, and protection for your data by processing at the edge to eliminate unauthorized external access or hacking.

## Transforming business with on-prem AI

The Qualcomm Dragonwing™ AI On-Prem Appliance Solution enables businesses to run custom and off-the-shelf AI applications on their premises, including generative AI – allowing sensitive customer data, fine-tuned models and inference loads to remain on premises. Powered by the Qualcomm® Cloud AI family of accelerators and Qualcomm® AI Inference Suite for On-Prem , this scalable hardware solution is designed to deliver significant savings in operational costs and overall total cost of ownership (TCO), compared to the cost of renting third-party AI infrastructure.

Bringing Edge AI upgrades into the factory setting can increase operational efficiencies and capabilities. Increased visibility across assets and workflows, intelligent inventory control, security features and capabilities benefit overall warehouse operations. ## Featured Edge AI Boxes

## MegaEdge AIP-FR68 AI workstation

A powerhouse for on-prem computing, optimized for generative AI, natural language processing, and computer vision workloads. Supports up to two Qualcomm® Cloud AI 100 Ultra cards, providing significant AI inference with low-power consumption, and runs the Qualcomm AI Inference Suite for On-Prem. This combination of accessibility and performance makes it ideal for on-premises AI workloads in a compact design.

## Edge AI Box

Empower your cameras with Vision Edge AI and easily change AI inference models from the Cloud. Support multiple ONVIF cameras with different AI inferences on each input. Support for Microsoft and Amazon cloud with Edge AI capability. Made with the Qualcomm® QCS610.

## Ingrasys EA5165N (Gloria)

This high performance Edge AI box is a cutting-edge on-premise solution. Powered by the Qualcomm® QRB5165N and Qualcomm® Cloud AI 100, it provides incredible AI computing power at ultra low power consumption. Also supports superior video multiprocessing capability and optional 5G Connectivity for broad based deployment.

## TurboX EB2 Edge AI Box

EB2 Edge AI Box is a cost-effective edge computing device with 1.7 TOPS AI performance and 6 channels FHD video decoding. It can flexibly adapt to different network access requirements during deployment.

## TurboX EB5 Edge AI Box

EB5 Edge AI Box is a high-performance AI edge computing device with support for industrial-grade OSware, supports edge-cloud synergy, remote AI algorithm and application deployment, FOTA.

## TurboX EB6 Edge AI Box

EB6 Edge AI Box is a premium tier edge computing device with up to 15 TOPS of AI performance, extendable to 70 TOPS with an accelerator card. Support up to 24 channels of FHD video decoding. Ultra-high AI performance and embedded with OSware.
