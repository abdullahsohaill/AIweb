## Meeting Bot API

Record video conferences through a bot joining the call. Best when explicit recording consent is needed, or for building AI agents.
[LINK: Learn more](/product/meeting-bot-api)

## Desktop Recording SDK

Record video conferences and in-person meetings through a desktop app, without a bot in the call. Best for a stealthier recording experience.
