Likewise Learn uses deep learning to predict a tweet's engagement through its text content and the follower count of the user. No need for unnecessary inputs!
Not happy with your tweet? Our iterative post refiner systematically turns unpolished ideas into eye-catching content.
We believe that posts should focus on quality, not quantity. Instead of using unreliable and opaque values (likes and views), Likewise learn ranks and generates posts based on the engagement rate, a ratio of user interactions to impressions.
