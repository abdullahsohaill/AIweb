## How do I train my own AI singing voice?

Head over to the "My Voices" page and click "Create a Voice". You will be prompted with a form to upload audio files. You need a Plus or Creator Plan to create a voice.
1. For the highest quality, Gather 15-30 minutes of dry single layer vocals.
Works with:
Singing/Rapping/Speaking
iPhone memos (remove bg noise with tape.it )
EQ, Light Compression, Pitch Correction to taste
Studio or Bedroom Vocals
Does NOT work with:
Layered voices
Reverb and echoes
Modulation or distortion effects
Audio with headphone leak or instruments
2. After uploading, click "Create" and you'll get an email when your voice is ready (usually 15 min - 1 hour depending on length of your vocals)
3. After your voice is ready, you can create and release songs from our catalog on steaming platforms or convert any link to your voice for practice purposes on the Swap Voice page.
