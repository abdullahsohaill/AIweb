## Immersive Experiences on Any Device

## Why Immersive Experiences

There is a new way to experience content, and it is immersive. With Immersity, you can experience movies, images, social media as if you’re inside the scene—right from your phone, tablet, monitor, or laptop.
Immersity’s disruption is unlocking new experiences without new hardware. Immersity redefines how content is experienced — on the devices people already use every day.

## immersity Platform

The Immersity Platform includes Spatial AI Software and Switchable-Display Hardware; easy to integrate by OEMs into their devices. Immersity devices project true holographic depth, creating a lifelike immersive effect that makes you feel part of the scene—almost inside it.

## immersity Technology

Immersity is the most advanced technology that displays content or projects immersive experiences, at the push of a button. Immersive experiences enable Spatial Intelligence, the next generation of AI where digital content blends with the physical world.

### Spatial AI Software

Immersity uses Leia Inc.'s proprietary Spatial AI to continuously understand both the viewer’s environment and the depth within virtual content.

### Switchable-Display Hardware

Immersity provides a complete solution: designing, manufacturing, and validating Leia Inc.'s Switchable-Display Hardware for seamless integration in any device.

## immersity Value

The Immersity Platform provides value to all players in the ecosystems, from Consumers, to OEMs and Global Tech Platforms. Learn how you can benefit from immersive experiences.

### Consumers

Immersity transforms everyday digital moments, pulling consumers and professionals into the scene to experience immersive content like never before.

### OEMs

Immersity enables OEMs to enhance their devices with an added layer of depth, unlocking new consumer value and differentiating their products.

### Global Tech Platforms

Immersive stereo content by Immersity unlocks Spatial Intelligence — the foundation for AGI that can see, understand, and operate in the real world.
