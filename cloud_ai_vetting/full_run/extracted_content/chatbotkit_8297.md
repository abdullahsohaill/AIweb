### What is Conversational AI?

Conversational AI is a subfield of artificial intelligence that enables machines to interact with humans in a natural, conversation-like manner through text or voice. It's the technology behind chatbots, virtual assistants, agentic AI systems, and messaging apps, allowing them to understand, process, and respond to human language in a contextually relevant manner.
