## Who We Are

Tango Eye is a next gen Rev Op tool for offline retail sales
TangoEye was born out of the need to provide accurate and intelligent insights to
retailers. Even today, most stores rely on manual clickers and rudimentary forms of
counting footfall in their stores. Hardware based solutions, till date have not solved
for some of the pressing requirements of retailers.
At TangoEye, we’ve built pure plug & play solution that integrates into the existing
CCTV infrastructure of retailers to generate intelligent insights. With this, we’ve made it Affordable, Accurate and Actionable for retailers to track their shopping
patterns and benchmark conversion rates.
