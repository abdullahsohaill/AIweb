## Questions about Maki? We’ve got answers

What is Maki People?
Maki People is an AI hiring software that helps companies recruit faster, fairer, and more effectively. Our platform uses intelligent agents to automate candidate screening and assessment; reducing bias and saving recruiters hundreds of hours.
What are Maki Agents?
Maki Agents are AI-powered recruitment tools, designed to make the hiring process more efficient and accurate. For example, Mochi conducts conversational screenings, Shiro delivers short skills tests, and Ken provides in-depth assessments. Together, they create a structured and data-driven hiring process.
What is Mochi, the AI screening agent?
Mochi replaces the manual recruiter phone screen with automated conversations. Candidates complete a branded voice or web interview, where Mochi checks eligibility, motivation, and language skills. This saves recruiter time and ensures every candidate is treated consistently.
What is Shiro, the skills assessment agent?
Shiro delivers short, science-based skill tests early in the funnel. It quickly identifies qualified candidates with cognitive, behavioral, and technical assessments. Companies use Shiro to replace subjective CV screening and improve fairness in hiring.
What is the difference between Shiro and Ken?
Shiro is used early in the process for fast, high-volume skill screening. Ken is used later for deeper evaluations; like case studies, coding challenges, or leadership assessments. Ken is only available as an add-on to Shiro, ensuring structured, cost-effective recruitment.
Can I use Maki for interview scheduling?
Yes. Kumi, our scheduling agent, integrates with Google and Outlook calendars so candidates can self-book interviews. This reduces no-shows and saves time for both recruiters and hiring managers.
Does Maki also support talent management?
Yes. Riku, currently in early access, is designed for internal mobility and talent development. It helps companies map employee skills, suggest career paths, and support upskilling.
What impact does Maki have on recruitment results?
Maki has helped companies like H&M, Capgemini, and BNP Paribas reduce time-to-hire by up to 45%, cut turnover for top candidates by 30%, and save over 250,000 recruiter hours. Candidate satisfaction scores remain above 99% positive.
