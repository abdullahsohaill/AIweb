## Designed for Control and Creativity

### Full Control with Nodes

Build AI workflows visually by connecting nodes on a canvas.
Unlike traditional linear UIs, ComfyUI gives you full control — branch, remix, and adjust every part of your workflow at any time.

### Reusable Workflows

Save, share, and reuse entire workflows effortlessly.
Exported images, videos, and 3D files carry metadata, so anyone can drag and drop them into ComfyUI to instantly rebuild the full workflow.

### Live Preview

See your results in real time as you adjust your workflows. Make changes and iterate faster with instant visual feedback.

## Open Source and Yours to Shape

ComfyUI is 100% open source and always will be.
Build, create, and share without limits.
Extend ComfyUI by building your own nodes. Customize your workflows, add new functionalities, and shape the tool to your needs.
