## Y o u r Q u e s t i o n s , A n s w e r e d

Is there an AI that can analyze data?
An AI data analyst I refers to the use of AI to process, interpret and extract insights from data through natural language. In our case, we have a chat-based application where you can convert your messages into charts, insights, spreadsheets, data models and more.
What does Formula bot do?
Formula Bot started as a simple Excel formula generator, but has evolved into a multi-purpose data analytics chat interface where users can perform quantitative and qualitative analysis on data across multiple data sources - Excel, Google Sheets, Google Analytics & more.
Can I use it directly within Excel & Google Sheets?
Our free add-ons allow users to generate and explain formulas, as well as performing qualitative analysis directly in the cells of your spreadsheets.
How accurate is AI data analysis?
AI data analysis is highly accurate when working with clean, well-structured data, but results depend on data quality. It’s best used alongside human review for context and validation.
How fast can AI analyze data?
AI can analyze data almost instantly compared to traditional methods. Instead of hours or days of manual work, AI can scan millions of rows, detect patterns, and generate insights in seconds to minutes.
What data sources are supported for analysis?
You can analyze your data across the following: Excel, Google Sheets, Google Analytics, Google Trends, Google Search Console, MSSQL, MySQL and Postgres.
