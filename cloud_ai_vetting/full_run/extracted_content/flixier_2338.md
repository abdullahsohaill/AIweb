## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is Flixier a free online video editor?

When we built Flixier we wanted to make it accessible for everyone. We created powerful technologies that enable anyone to edit video smoothly even on lower end devices or on old computers. This is why we also took the decision to make a free online video editor enabling anyone to try it out super quick, not only you don’t need to pay but you don’t even need to create an account.

### Why edit videos using our video editor?

Flixier makes it easy for anyone to pick up video editing, no powerful computer needed, no software downloads or installs and the interface is easy to understand and use. We believe that our video editor has the right mix of features to enable you to create any kind of video, you can become a YouTube or TikTok creator, make social media ads or even make sales videos.

### Can I edit a video using my Chromebook?

Yes, our online video editor is perfect for Chromebooks, you can straight from your browser, and comes packed with powerful features that will put any other Chromebook video editor to shame.

### Is Flixier a good video editor for beginners?

We believe Flixier is the perfect video editor  for beginners, the powerful timeline lets you quickly arrange or stack videos in order and you can use our ready made transitions, motion titles and calls to action to add a professional look to your videos in no time. You can even add music or effects to your videos.

### How to edit videos using Flixier?

To edit videos using Flixier simply click the Get Started button above and you’ll get into the editor, no account needed.  Now add videos to your library from your computer or from other locations thanks to our direct integrations. With your files in the library you are ready to edit videos. Simply drag and drop clips on the timeline in the order you want them to appear. Here you can cut, trim or crop them and now is also the time to add text, subtitles, calls to action or shapes. When happy with the video simply click the Export button at the top right of the screen and your video will be processed by our powerful cloud servers and it will download automatically when ready.
