## Tome — AI Sales Tool

## What is Tome?

Tome is an AI-powered sales assistant that equips sales teams with buyer research and value articulation tools. It offers strategic account preparation, Salesforce MEDDIC automation, and sales framework features, helping account executives gather prospect insights and accelerate deals.

## What sets Tome apart?

Tome differentiates itself with its sales-specific AI model, which outperforms generic language models in business intelligence and buyer research. This specialized focus proves valuable for account executives seeking to accelerate deals and improve value articulation throughout the sales cycle. By automating processes like MEDDIC in Salesforce, Tome redefines how sales teams prepare for strategic accounts and close more deals.
