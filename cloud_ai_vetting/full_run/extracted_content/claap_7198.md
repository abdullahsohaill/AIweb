What are the key features of Claap's AI platform?
Claap offers a suite of AI assistants designed to level up your sales performance.
AI Agent : It automates administrative tasks, allowing sales representatives to focus on closing deals. It automatically records and transcribes meetings in 99 languages, generates personalized summaries using customizable templates, crafts follow-up emails that mirror your communication style, and keeps your CRM updated by auto-filling relevant information.
AI Analyst : It provides deep insights into your sales pipeline, helping you understand why deals are won or lost with deal automated qualification & reviews. It also delivers a suite of reports to monitor objections, competitors or feature requests on autopilot.
AI Coach : It accelerates onboarding and training, providing new account executives with the tools they need to succeed, from pitch trainings to live flash-cards and AI-assisted feedback. It also enables the creation of training materials from recorded clips or highlights of best calls in a secure and engaging video library.

What recording types do Claap support?
Claap supports audio & video uploads in a wide range of formats, from mov or mp4 to wav. The full list is available in our Help Center, but make sure to reach out to our team if you encounter any issues with your specific file format.
Besides, Claap allows you to easily record your own videos, whether by inviting a bot recorder to join your meetings or phone calls or by capturing screen recordings with our Claap Chrome extension and Desktop app.
What type of companies use Claap's AI platform?
Any B2B company with dynamic sales environments can benefit from Claap AI platform. We are used by a wide range of companies, from innovative startups to well-established industry leaders in industries like financial services, education or tech.
Claap is ideal for teams relying on conference and phone calls, as well as field sales teams needing a streamlined way to capture and share insights on the go.
What can Claap do for me if I'm not in a sales position?
Claap is incredibly valuable by enhancing communication & collaboration across teams, from founders to HR, product teams and consultants.
Claap allows everyone to capture, review, and share insights from meetings, ensuring everyone stays aligned without needing to attend every call.
Claap Chrome extension & Desktop app also let you record quick videos of your screen. It's ideal to share updates, capture feedback, and provide a record of discussions that matter.
