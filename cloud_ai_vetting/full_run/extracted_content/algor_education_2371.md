## Frequently Asked Questions

### How does Algor Education work?

Upload your study materials, including text, images, audio, or video, and let Algor’s AI help you learn more effectively. With a variety of templates, you can summarize information, solve exercises, organize ideas, and create personalized study and learning guides. Algor goes beyond generating tools. It helps you master subjects, improve retention, and reach your learning goals.

### Who is Algor Education for?

Algor is designed for students, teachers, and lifelong learners looking to enhance their learning process. Whether you're preparing for exams, organizing study materials, or creating teaching resources, Algor serves as an AI study buddy, making learning faster, smarter, and more interactive.

### How is Algor different from other AI tools?

Unlike other AI tools that simply generate static content, Algor acts as an AI study companion, structuring information into dynamic, interactive formats. Instead of just summarizing text into other text, Algor helps learners and educators organize ideas, visualize complex topics, solve problems, practise for exams, everything in the same platform.

### What types of input types are accepted on Algor?

Algor supports a variety of file types, including text documents, PDFs, images, audio recordings, Youtube videos. Our AI, tailored to students and teachers, processes this content instantly, transforming it into interactive learning tools like mind maps, summaries, tables, flashcards, and quizzes. This makes studying and learning more efficient, engaging, and personalized.

### What are Algor templates?

Algor templates are AI-powered study tools that structure learning materials for better understanding and retention. Explore the Algor templates page to see how they work.

### How much does Algor cost?

Algor is free to try, with premium features available. Check the pricing page for full details.
