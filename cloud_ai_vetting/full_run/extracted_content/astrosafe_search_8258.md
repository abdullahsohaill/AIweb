## What is AstroSafe?

AstroSafe is a plug-and-play safety infrastructure for kid-tech apps, games, and devices. It provides ready-made modules for parent portals, safe AI, media players, and compliance, so teams can launch faster without rebuilding safety from scratch.

## Who is AstroSafe for?

We servekid-tech and ed-tech companiesbuilding apps, games, streaming platforms, devices, and smart toys for children. If your product needs to be safe, compliant, and parent-trusted, AstroSafe is for you.

## What makes AstroSafe different from other parental control or AI tools?

Every module isbuilt for kids first— with guardrails, age-adaptive experiences, and audit-ready compliance. We combine both sides of the experience:safe AI for kids+parent portals for adults.

## Can I start with just one module?

Yes. You can start small with a single module (e.g., ParentHub™ or SafePlayer™) and expand as your needs grow. Pricing scales by module and usage.

## How do you handle compliance?

Our RuleBook™ engine automatically applies policies for COPPA, GDPR-K, and AADC. We also provide audit trails, consent logs, and configurable settings to meet region-specific requirements.

## How is pricing structured?

We combine amonthly base fee per module with usage-based pricingfor AI-heavy services like SafeGPT™, SafeImage™, and SafeVideo™. Plans start around $1,000/month and scale with your users.

## Can parents and schools manage multiple children?

Yes. ParentHub™ supports multi-account management— parents can manage siblings under one portal, and schools can manage classrooms with teacher dashboards.

## How do you ensure AI interactions are safe?

SafeGPT™, KidVoice™, and ClearSearch™ filter unsafe topics, redact personal information, and log all interactions. With ChatCheck™, parents get transcripts and digest reports of what their kids ask and how AI responds.
