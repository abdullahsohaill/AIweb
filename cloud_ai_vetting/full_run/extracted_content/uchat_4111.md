### What is UChat?

UChat is designed to serve all types of users. With our visual flow builder you can build any automation which requires no code knowledge at all which is perfect for beginners or none technical users, and to serve the more advanced users we also have nodes like Javascripts, external requests,ability to build mini apps and more

### Which channels does UChat support?

UChat supports over 15 messaging and social channels, including:Web Chat;Facebook Messenger; WhatsApp; Instagram; Telegram; Viber; SMS; Voice; WeChat; Line; Slack; VK; Intercom;Jivo Chat,etc

### Does UChat support AI-powered chatbots?

Yes. UChat offers full support for advanced AI-powered chatbots, with seamless integration of leading large language models (LLMs) including OpenAI, Claude, Google Gemini, xAI, DeepSeek, Coze, and more.  You can build intelligent, conversational AI agents using our visual drag-and-drop flow builder, without writing code. UChat also supports function calling (AI Functions), allowing AI to trigger real-time actions, fetch data, and complete tasks inside the conversation.  When needed, you can enable smart live chat transfer to escalate conversations to human agents — blending AI automation with human support.

### Do I need coding skills to use UChat?

No. UChat is designed for non-technical users. Its intuitive visual flow builder lets you create powerful automations and AI conversations without writing a single line of code.

### What kind of automation can I build?

With UChat, you can automate customer support, appointment bookings, sales funnels, lead qualification, reminders, order tracking, and much more — all customized for your business.

### Can I try UChat before subscribing?

Yes, UChat offers a free trial with access to all Pro features — no credit card required. You can build, test, and launch bots during the trial period without limitations.
