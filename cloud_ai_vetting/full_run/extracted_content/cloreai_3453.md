### FAQ

We support two currencies: Clore and BTC. The fees range from 1.8% to 2.5% for spot orders and from 5% to 10% for on-demand orders. On-Demand Rental: You can rent a server at a fixed price set by the host. The server is reserved for you. The server remains available to you until you cancel the contract or your balance is insufficient to continue the service. Spot Market: A quick transaction at auction rates. The minimum price is set by the host, usually lower than the on-demand rate. The server is not reserved, and higher bids can outbid your offer.
POH (Proof of Holding) is Clore.ai's unique system that rewards users for holding Clore tokens in their wallets. The more Clore you hold, the more benefits you receive: • Increased rewards: Earn additional bonuses in Clore when renting out your equipment. • Convenience: No need to transfer funds to our wallet—just sign a transaction from your desktop wallet. • No locking: Your funds remain available for withdrawal or sale at any time. • Service discounts: Clore holders get up to a 50% discount on our fees. • Voluntary participation: Using POH is completely optional, but it’s a great way to maximize the benefits of holding Clore tokens. • No penalties: You won’t face restrictions or penalties if you decide to withdraw funds from POH.
