## The Amira Reading Suite: One Coherent Learning Loop

Amira is the learning agent at the center of your literacy strategy—connecting assessment, instruction, and tutoring to deliver your district’s vision. Your reading program decisions just got simplified and elevated.

Amira listens to every student read aloud, analyzing each word, diagnosing foundational skills, and identifying reading difficulties in 20 minutes or less.

Amira is a personal tutor. Students practice reading while Amira coaches each student one-on-one, reinforcing instruction and responding to unique reading difficulties in real time.
