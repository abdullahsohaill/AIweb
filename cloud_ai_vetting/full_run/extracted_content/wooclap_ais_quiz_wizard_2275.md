## Create relevant and engaging questions, amazingly fast.

Avoid the hassle of coming up with the right questions to engage your audience. Save time and generate questions instantly from any source with Wooclap AI.

## Your Wooclap event, created in 3 simple steps

### 1. Upload content from any source

Choose from your preferred source: whether it’s a document, audio/video file, text, link to a website or even a Youtube video. Wooclap AI will scan it and will adapt the content it creates based on the information provided.

### 2. Choose the question types to generate

Instantly generate Multiple choices, Fill in the blanks, or Matching questions to test your audience knowledge, Open questions to engage in lively debates, or Brainstorming questions to collect ideas.

### 3. Moderate the questions created and add them to your event

You can always delete or edit the question titles and the answers suggested by Wooclap AI to make sure it matches your presentation and your audience, whether it’s students or professionals. Once you’re happy with all the questions, you can add them to your Wooclap event in just one click.
