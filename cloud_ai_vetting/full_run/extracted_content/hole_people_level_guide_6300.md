## Hole People FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Hole People

### How do I start playing Hole People?

After downloading and installing the game, you can start exploring different levels and fill characters of different colors into their corresponding holes.

### Is Hole People free?

The game is free to play but contains ads and in-app purchases.

### How do I complete levels in Hole People?

You need to fill all characters into the holes while making sure your slots don't get filled up. Different colored characters can only enter holes of the same color, and they will block each other if colors don't match.

### How do I handle bombs in the game?

When you encounter bombs in Hole People, you need to click on Hole People characters of the corresponding color to defuse them. Make sure to complete this action before the bomb explodes, or the game will end.

### What if the characters can't move?

Make sure there are no Hole People characters of other colors blocking the path. You need to clear the Hole People in front first to create a path for those behind.

### What if I encounter issues while playing?

You can contact the Hole People development team through the in-game support feature or by emailing hypeuplab@gmail.com.
