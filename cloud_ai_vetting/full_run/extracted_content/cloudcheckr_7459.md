## Frequently asked questions

### What is cloud cost optimization?

Cloud cost optimization is the practice of controlling and minimizing the financial impact of cloud usage across multiple providers. As organizations adopt services from AWS, Azure and Google Cloud, they benefit from agility and scalability, but also face challenges like fragmented visibility, complex pricing and unplanned expenses. Effective optimization means gaining unified insight into spend, aligning resources with business needs, and proactively managing costs to maximize value from the cloud.

### What are some common challenges associated with cloud cost optimization?

While cloud cost optimization offers benefits like reduced expenses, improved resource use and better budget control, organizations often face hurdles such as deciphering complex pricing, limited visibility into resource usage and balancing cost with performance. Managing dynamic workloads, ensuring consistent tagging and overcoming internal resistance can complicate efforts—especially for MSPs, who must address these challenges at scale across multiple client environments, each with unique requirements and compliance needs.

### How does Flexera One FinOps address common challenges in cloud cost optimization?

Flexera One FinOps meets cloud cost optimization challenges by providing full visibility into all cloud resources across hybrid and multi-cloud environments, enabling informed decision-making and cost control. It provides robust policy-based automation of recommendations and remediation actions, helping organizations identify and act on cost-saving opportunities. The platform also unifies cost optimization across FinOps Foundation expanded Scopes by supporting unified approaches to IT asset management and FinOps and SaaS and license optimization. This comprehensive approach empowers teams to optimize spend, reduce waste and align cloud investments with business goals.
