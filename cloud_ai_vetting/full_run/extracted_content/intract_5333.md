## Thank You for Being Part of Our Journey

After years of exploring Web3 together, Intract Quest is closing its doors. 
                Every quest completed, every reward earned, and every connection made has been 
                a part of something extraordinary.

## A Note from the Team

When we started Intract, we dreamed of making Web3 accessible and rewarding 
                        for everyone. Together, we built a community of curious explorers who weren't 
                        afraid to learn, experiment, and grow.
Your enthusiasm, feedback, and passion have meant everything to us. 
                        Though this chapter is ending, the skills you've gained and the connections 
                        you've made will continue to shape the future of Web3.
All your contributions, XP, and achievements have been saved. This isn't goodbye—it's a thank you for being the best community we could have asked for.
