### New ADA Title II Deadlines Are Driving Change

From April 2026, state and local governments in the U.S. must make all prerecorded video content accessible under WCAG 2.1 AA – including audio description. For many public entities, this will be the first time audio description is required at scale.
LEXI AD makes compliance achievable and affordable. Our automated, fully WCAG-compliant audio description delivers natural-sounding narration at up to 75 % faster turnaround and a fraction of traditional cost.
