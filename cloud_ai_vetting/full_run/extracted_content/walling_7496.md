## Your ideas. More engaging. More impressive.

## From idea to a complete project in seconds with AI

With Walling AI , just ask the app what you want to organize or manage, and watch it generate ideas, tasks, images, tables, and all the content you need in seconds.

## Make your work look 10x more impressive!

No more boring documents or restrictive slides. Say hello to the wall , the new visual medium that offers the flexibility to organize and display all the details of your ideas in an engaging and stunning way.

## Visual canvas for the nitty-gritty details of your ideas

### All the notes & context

What’s point of sharing ideas without context? Add all your notes and information to your wall in seconds.

### Images that inspire

Drag and drop images to your wall to create a beautiful, inspiring moodboard that brings your wall to life.

### Videos, files & links

Upload videos, PDFs and files. Embed links from other apps onto your wall. The wall is a visual canvas that brings all your project pieces together.

### Tables, calendars & more!

More than just a board of cards. Add tables, lists and calendars to your wall. Walling is a new medium that changes the way we share and ideas.

### All your ideas & project details on one wall

No jumping between pages or clicks over clicks to reveal your ideas. The wall is a visual, interactive page to organize, manage and present your work in one place.
