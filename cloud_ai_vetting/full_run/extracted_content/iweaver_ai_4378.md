## Frequently asked questions

### What is iWeaver.ai?

iWeaver is a personal knowledge management tool designed for professionals. It helps you systematically organize and structure the information you've gathered within your professional domain, allows you to utilize specialized AI agents for various tasks, and provides AI-powered question-answering for efficient knowledge retrieval. Think of it as your personalized AI assistant, boosting your productivity.

### What platforms does iWeaver support?

iWeaver offers versatile access across several platforms to suit various user preferences: Website: https://www.iweaver.ai/ Browser plugins: iWeaver AI Plugin Workspace: iWeaver AI Workspace

### What types of files can I ask questions about?

Iweaver AI supports a variety of file types including:Images: PNG, JPG, JPEG. Audio: MP3. Video: MP4. Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX, PPTX

### What specific tasks can iWeaver help me with?

1. Multi-format Content to Summary & Mind Map: Extracts key information and data from various content formats, and assesses the value of the information. 2. Multi-source, Cross-format Content Upload & Comprehensive Analysis: Supports uploading content from multiple sources in different formats, enabling comprehensive analysis and evaluation to aid decision-making. 3. Content Repurposing: Facilitates the creation of new content based on uploaded materials, such as rewriting industry reports, research papers, consulting proposals, and marketing copy. 4. AI Question and Answering for Key Data Extractions: iWeaver will allow you to ask questions of your uploaded data, and then it will use AI to extract the most relevant data for you.

### How long does it take to get a summary?

The response time for questions is typically 3-5 seconds, while document parsing takes about 10 seconds. Overall, you can expect the process to be completed within 7 seconds.

### Can I download the generated mind maps?

Yes, mind maps can be downloaded in Markdown, SVG, PNG, and XMind formats for editing or direct use.
