## Features

Generate drafts for any contract, from vendor contracts to SLAs and NDAs. Based on AI data from millions of contracts. Or import third-party drafts in a couple of clicks.
Evaluate contracts against preferred positions & terms. Flag deviations and get AI redline suggestions. Collaborate on documents in real time. Create multi-level approval workflows. Track contract status. Enable eSignature.
Extract 120+ dates and clauses across contracts. Expirations & renewals, jurisdiction, limitation of liability, payment terms, and much more. Track contract volume per day, task completion, and more. Identify bottlenecks. Monitor compliance and generate risk assessments.
Generate tasks from legal requests and assign them to team members in seconds. Easily add recurring subtasks and attachments. Track all team tasks in one place, monitoring and balancing workloads. No more spreadsheets.
Integrate with Google Drive, OneDrive, Salesforce, Microsoft Word, Dropbox, Slack, DocuSign, Adobe Sign, SharePoint, ShareFile, Amazon S3, box, and more. Plus access our open API or work with our Technical Services team to build custom integrations.
