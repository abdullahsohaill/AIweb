## Video Labeling

Annotate hours-long videos without cutting into images, with best object tracking models.
- Low-FPS, high-resolution and multi-view videos support
- Auto-tracking with no manual actions from labeler
- Off-screen detection and trajectories for tracking

## 3D Point Cloud Labeling

Comprehensive scenes from LiDAR or RADAR sensors with photo and video context.
- Object detection, tracking and segmentation in 3D
- AI models for automatic pre-labeling and cuboid tracking
- Sensor fusion with photo and video context and real-time synchronization between 2D and 3D

## Data curation and collaboration

Unify multiple teams and organize both local and cloud data for AI with advanced search and visualization.
Connect local or remote cloud storages like AWS, GCS and Azure, with no data duplication.
Use OpenID, LDAP and MFA together with built-in access roles, teams and monitoring.
Huge collection of popular import formats and migration tools — easy to add custom.

### Search and Visualize Billions of Labels

Use natural language and apply advanced filter, visualize and inspect data with tools such as embeddings plots.

### Quality Assurance and Statistics

Get deep insights with heatmaps, class co-occurrence and balances to identify issues with your datasets.

## Train , Evaluate and Deploy Models

Complete infrastructure to train state-of-the-art neural networks, evaluate model performance, find issues in training data and deploy production ready AI.
Train state of the art or custom models on your datasets in a few clicks.
Tools to experiment, manage and compare model versions with easy metrics.
Perform human-in-the-loop to improve models iteratively with out-of-the-box framework.

### Compare Performance of Models

### Deploy Models for Assisted Labeling
