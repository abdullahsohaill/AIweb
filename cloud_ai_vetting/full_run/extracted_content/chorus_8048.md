## Features

What makes Chorus the ultimate songwriting app for creative songwriters?

## Rhymes .

Words that were just meant to be
Forget endless lists of awkward words.
Chorus finds the most natural, singable rhymes for your style—right inside your lyrics.
No switching tabs. No losing your flow.

## Triggers .

Never run out of ideas
Triggers give you the perfect word to start your next line or verse, tailored to your genre.
Inspiration, exactly when you need it.

## Genius .

Writer’s block ends here
Stuck on a line? Give Genius a single lyric, word, or phrase, and it will keep your momentum going.
Fresh ideas, seamless flow, and no more blank-page panic.
