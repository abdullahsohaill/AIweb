FAQs about Seismic's learning & coaching capabilities
What are Seismic's learning & coaching capabilities?
Seismic is AI-powered sales training software that boosts seller performance with personalized, interactive learning—so teams are always ready to win every customer interaction.
How does Seismic support coaching of customer-facing teams?
Seismic supports sales coaching by providing personalized, data-driven insights and training resources tailored to individual needs. It fosters continuous improvement through microlearning, sales performance metrics, and targeted coaching playbooks, enhancing overall sales team performance.
Can Seismic be used for onboarding new sales reps?
Yes. Seismic can be used for onboarding new sellers. It provides interactive online sales training courses and AI-powered role-plays that ensure faster time to value and effective sales onboarding.
Is Seismic mobile friendly?
Yes. Seismic is mobile friendly. It offers a seamless experience across devices, allowing users to access training materials and resources anytime, anywhere.
