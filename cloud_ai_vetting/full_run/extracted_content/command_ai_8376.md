We think these are dangerous assumptions. Every interaction users have with a product shifts their perception of it. Towards either “frustrating, clumsy, not for me” or “sleek and functional”.
The effect of poorly done software that aims to help with “adoption” or “onboarding” is to push users down the wrong path. That’s why our first focus is the Hippocratic Oath of User Assistance: to avoid annoying users in the name of helping them.
