### How does AI detection work?

AI detectors work by looking for patterns in text that are more likely to have been written by a machine than a human. The most common factors they look for include: Perplexity: Is the writing obviously predictable? Burstiness: How much variation is there in the sentence length and style? Style: Is the tone and writing style overly generic or repetitive? GPTZero uses its own proprietary model that takes hundreds of factors into consideration and boasts the highest detection accuracy in the industry
