## AI Feedback Intelligence that transforms voice of customer into insights and action.

Zonka AI transforms high-volume, unstructured feedback from surveys, chats, tickets, reviews, and more into real-time, actionable intelligence. Fueled by Thematic Analysis, Impact & Sentiment Scoring, and Agentic AI, it instantly enlightens CX teams with what’s working, what’s broken, and where to focus—across themes, teams, and locations.
Break down silos and unlock unified customer insights from surveys, chats, tickets, reviews, social, and more.
Capture sentiment shifts, detect patterns, auto-tag feedback, and surface key drivers—no more guesswork.
Cut through the noise, focus on trends, and identify actions driving ROI, NPS, CSAT, and CES for strategic prioritization.
Deliver targeted insights to leadership, product, and frontline teams, ensuring alignment and ownership.
