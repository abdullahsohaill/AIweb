## Accessible by design

Adding chat to your website can make it more accessible, but this only works if you choose messaging tools that are created to include people with disabilities. Olark's platform is third-party tested and certified by a third-party to comply with WCAG 2.1 AA standards. This means that the chat widget on your website and the software your team uses to reply to incoming messages will work for disabled people, not against them.
