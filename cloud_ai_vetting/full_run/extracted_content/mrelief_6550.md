## Hi, we're mRelief —a nonprofit that helps you sign up for food stamps.

STEP 1

### Find out if you likely qualify for SNAP.

We designed our eligibility screener to make it easy to find out if you likely qualify for SNAP (food stamps). You can take our screener online or by text. Just text “Food” to 74544 to get started.*
