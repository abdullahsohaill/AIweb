## FAQs

### What is Zebracat?

Zebracat is an AI-powered platform that creates fully edited, high-performing videos automatically. It's designed for creators, marketers, and businesses who want to grow with video without editors or agencies.

### I’m Not a Video Editing Pro. Can I Still Use Zebracat?

Yes. It's built for non-editors. Just input your text or idea, and the AI handles the rest: script, visuals, editing, and more.

### What Kind of Content Can I Create With Zebracat?

Social media videos, ads, explainer videos, faceless content, AI avatar clips, podcast snippets, and more. All optimized for engagement.

### How Fast Can I Create a Video Using Your Tool?

Minutes. The average video is ready within 2–5 minutes, depending on complexity.

### Can I Add My Own Voice or Use AI Voiceovers?

You can do both. Upload your voice, clone your voice with AI  or pick from realistic AI voices in 70+ languages.

### Is Zebracat a Free AI Video Generator?

Yes. You can start for free. Premium features like advanced voices, avatars, and higher export quality require a paid plan.

### Can I Use the Videos for Commercial Purposes?

Yes. For paid plans, all exports can be used commercially, even in ads, campaigns, and websites.

### How Much Control Do I Have Over the AI-Generated Content?

You can tweak scripts, visuals, pacing, voices, music, and branding. AI handles the heavy lifting; you stay in control.

### Can I Convert Audio to Video With Zebracat?

Yes. Upload your voice, podcast, or music. Zebracat turns it into a video with visuals, captions, and effects.

### Are AI Avatars Customizable for Branding?

Yes. You can choose avatars, adjust tone, background, and add your logo and brand colors.

### Are Subtitles and Captions Automatically Generated?

Yes. Subtitles are auto-generated and editable. You can style them to match your brand.

### What Video Formats Do You Support for Download?

MP4 format. Optimized for platforms like Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn.

### Is My Data Safe With Zebracat?

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and never sell or misuse your data.

### Is AI Video Generation Secure and Ethical?

Yes. We follow strict ethical standards and are part of the Content Authenticity Initiative to ensure transparency.

### What Technical Requirements Do I Need to Use Zebracat?

A stable internet connection and a modern browser (Chrome works best). No special hardware needed.

### Can I Cancel My Subscription at Any Time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. You can do so by going to your account settings. And if you need help with anything, our (human) support team is here to assist you.

### What if I Need Help or Have Questions?

Use our in-app HelpCat chatbot or contact support via email (support@zebracat.ai) and Skool community. We usually reply within hours.
