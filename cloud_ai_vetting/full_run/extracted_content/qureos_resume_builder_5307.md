## Frequently asked questions

### What is a Resume Builder?

Resume builder is a tool that helps you create a powerful resume within minutes. It ensures that your resume highlights your strengths, skills, and experiences in the best possible way.

### How long should a resume be?

For entry-level candidates or those with under 5 years of experience, a resume should be succinct and ideally confined to a single page. Our resume builder is designed to deliver just that: a precise, one-page resume.

### Is this a free Resume Builder?

Yes! Our resume builder is completely free of cost.

### Can I make a two-page resume?

Yes, you can create a two-page resume, especially if you have extensive work experience or numerous relevant accomplishments. However, it's essential to ensure that all information is relevant and adds value. For entry-level candidates or those with less than 5 years of experience, a one-page resume is typically recommended. Always prioritize clarity and conciseness, regardless of length.
