## Frequently Asked Questions

## Do you offer freemium?

Yes. Everyone should have access to English speaking coach. Pronounce offers a free plan with limited access. Pronounce AI Free Plan is suitable for individuals who practice speaking English but can't afford Premium or a speech coach. It might be a good fit for you if you're an advanced speaker with almost perfect pronunciation and need conversational practice.

## How long does it take to speak confidently?

Depending on your goals and experience, it can take only 90 days of daily exercises to coordinate your muscles to speak clear English and adopt new vocabulary and sentence structure. In some cases, getting feedback is already a huge step forward. In addition, being aware of your improvement progress can boost your self-confidence and help you achieve the desired English speaking level.

## Is speaking an American accent important?

Executives and professionals from tech, finance, health, education, and legal industries are improving their American accents. American accent opens doors to a broader audience and can dramatically advance careers, professional opportunities, and personal lives.

## Should I use an English accent coach?

One out of five Pronounce users complements speech checking with Pronounce AI with one-on-one coaching sessions. Using Pronounce's reports on hours of your conversations in real life will make personal coaching more efficient.

## What is included in Pronounce AI Premium?

With Pronounce AI Premium, you get a personal English speech copilot that checks your speech grammar, pronunciation, and clarity. Premium users enjoy unlimited recording of conversations and unlimited speech recommendations with pronunciation practice.

## Do you offer a free trial for Premium Plan?

Yes, you can start Pronounce AI Premium subscription with a free trial and cancel anytime in your account on Plan & Billing page before the end of the trial period.
