## How Loopcv works in 3 simple steps

The process of finding a job is complicated and time-consuming. There are hundreds of tools and a lot of copy-pasting. We have designed a unique process allowing anyone to get a job faster with minimum effort using AI.

### Automatically apply to jobs

Loopcv collects new job postings every day, searching for jobs in your desired locations, and automatically applies on your behalf using AI. If you don't want to apply automatically, you can check all the matched jobs manually and apply with one click!

### Automatically email recruiters

We automatically find the email of the recruiter in a company sending a personalised email. You can select an email template from our predefined set or create your own from scratch. Loopcv will use this email to contact companies trying to hire people like you!

### Measure and improve

Loopcv collects statistics: how many companies opened your email, how many companies replied and which of your CVs got more attention. You can A/B test different CVs, try different keywords and use Loopcv as a tool to optimise your job search.
