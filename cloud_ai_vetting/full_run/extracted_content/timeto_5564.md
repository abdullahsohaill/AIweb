## All your calendars in one place

Consolidate calendars from Google, Outlook, Apple, Fastmail and more in one app. Morgen has the features you need, putting an end to jumping between calendars needed.

### Calendar Sets

View your calendars together, one at a time, or in subsets using customizable calendar shortcuts.

### Reminders

Set one or more reminders for events and scheduled tasks. Reminders can be configured for each calendar or event.

### Built-in scheduling links

Share personalized booking links to allow others to schedule time with you. Your availability is checked in real-time based on the calendars you choose.

### Team availability

View your colleagues' availability and get recommended meeting times that won’t disrupt their focus.

### Automate your calendar

Calendar sync . Sync events from one calendar to another to get an accurate view of your availability and avoid double bookings. Customize which events are copied and how they appear in the destination calendar. Travel time. Automatically block time for your commute to and from events, taking into account location, mode of travel, and time of day. Buffer time. Automatically schedule custom buffers around meetings for intentional breaks or in case they run over.
