## Enjoy having multiple cooks in the kitchen

Discuss certain emails, in those emails
Easily help one another with information and context, before the email is sent.
Focus with dedicated team spaces
Give each team a shared inbox, shared tasks, and a shared space to discuss relevant work.
See, read, but don’t touch
Internal statuses give visibility to those who need to be in the know, but not in the action.

## Always know who’s doing what

Turn any email into a task. See what people are working on today, tomorrow, and next week — without leaving your inbox.
See your team’s capacity
Who has a lot going on, who has a little, so you can move things around.
Keep tabs on specific emails
Some emails need specific attention from multiple people. Use tags to organize and keep top of mind.
