## Tips to Get the Best Out of FaceSymAI

Maximize the accuracy of your facial symmetry analysis with these simple steps.
Ensure your face is well-lit, with even lighting to avoid shadows that might distort your facial features. Natural daylight is preferable, facing towards the light source.
Take the photo with your face directly facing the camera, maintaining a neutral expression. This position helps the AI accurately assess your facial symmetry.
Use a simple, uncluttered background to ensure there's a clear contrast between your face and the surroundings. This helps the AI focus on analyzing your facial features without distractions.
Make sure your hair is pulled back and that glasses or other accessories are removed. Obstructions can interfere with the symmetry analysis, affecting the accuracy of the results.

## Understanding Symmetrical and Asymmetrical Faces

Learn how facial symmetry impacts appearance and how our AI technology detects it.

### Symmetrical Face

A symmetrical face has nearly identical left and right sides, with features that align perfectly when divided down the middle. Key characteristics include:
- Evenly positioned eyes of similar size and shape
- Balanced nostrils and nose alignment
- Symmetrical lips and mouth corners
- Equal cheekbone height and prominence
Symmetrical faces are often associated with attractiveness in scientific studies, as symmetry is believed to indicate good genetic health and developmental stability.

### Asymmetrical Face

An asymmetrical face shows noticeable differences between the left and right sides. Common asymmetrical features include:
- One eye slightly higher or differently shaped than the other
- Uneven smile or mouth that tilts to one side
- Nose that deviates from center of the face
- Different contours on either side of the jaw or face
It's important to note that perfectly symmetrical faces are extremely rare. Most people have some degree of facial asymmetry, which often contributes to uniqueness and character.

### How We Detect Facial Symmetry

FaceSymAI uses advanced technology to analyze and assess facial symmetry:
Our system identifies your facial features and creates a digital map of your face.
The technology compares both sides of your face to identify similarities and differences.
We examine key facial features such as eyes, nose, mouth, and overall face shape for symmetrical alignment.
Based on this analysis, we provide you with insights about your facial symmetry in an easy-to-understand format.
