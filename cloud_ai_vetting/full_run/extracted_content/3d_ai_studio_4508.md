## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does a generation take?

Generation times vary by model. Fast models like Prism Turbo, Swift, and Forge Sketch typically complete in 20-30 seconds. For highest quality generations using Prism 3 with detailed texture and geometry settings, expect 2-4 minutes.

### How can I generate a 3D model with AI?

3D AI Studio offers multiple generation methods: Text to 3D lets you describe your model with a text prompt, Image to 3D converts images into 3D models, or use our Image Studio to edit and refine your image before converting it to 3D. Simply choose your preferred input method and let our AI handle the rest.

### What image formats work for image-to-3D conversion?

Our AI supports all common image formats including JPG, PNG, and WebP. For best results, use high-resolution images with clear subject visibility. Single images work great, and our AI excels at converting photos and illustrations into detailed 3D models.

### What 3D file formats can I export?

Export your models in industry-standard formats: GLB (web/AR), FBX (game engines), OBJ (universal), USDZ (Apple AR), STL (3D printing), DAE (Collada), and BLEND (Blender native). All formats are optimized for their intended use case, whether that's Unity, Unreal Engine, Blender, web applications, or 3D printing.

### Can I use models for commercial projects?

Yes, all models you generate are yours to use commercially. Use them in games, apps, marketing materials, product visualizations, or any commercial project. You retain full rights to your generated 3D assets.

### Are models compatible with 3D printing?

Yes, our AI-generated models can be exported for 3D printing. We recommend using the highest quality settings and running the remesh tool to ensure proper geometry for physical printing. Export as OBJ or STL for best 3D printer compatibility.

### How many credits does each task cost?

3D generation costs between 25-35 credits depending on the model and settings. Image generation costs 2-4 credits. All tools display their exact credit cost before you generate, so you'll always know the cost upfront.

### Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we stand behind our service. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team at support@3daistudio.com and we'll handle your refund request quickly and professionally.
