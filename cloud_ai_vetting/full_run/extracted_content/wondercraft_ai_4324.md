## AI Video for real work

Wondercraft is an AI video studio built for real work. It turns AI-generated video, voice, images, and sound into business-ready videos through structured workflows, not loose prompts. Instead of generating isolated clips, Wondercraft helps teams reliably create videos they can actually ship.
Most AI video tools generate clips. Wondercraft generates outcomes. It combines best-in-class AI models with workflows that encode best practices, so teams can produce consistent, on-brand videos without becoming AI or video experts. The result is usable video, not just impressive output.
Wondercraft focuses on the types of videos teams actually need at work:
- Training and onboarding videos
- Internal communications and updates
- Product launches and announcements
- Explainers and educational content
- Social and promotional videos
- Polished edits and cutdowns from existing footage
