## How to Use Magi AI as an AI Video Generator & AI Video Extender

Start by registering for an account on the official Magi AI website： Sand.ai

### 2. Initiate a Project in Magi AI's Canvas

Once logged in, create a new project within Magi AI's canvas. It's worth noting that you can place multiple images in a project to generate multiple video clips.

### 3. Upload Your Image or a Video

Upload an image that you want to use as a reference for your video. If you want to use the AI video extenser feature, upload the video.

### 4. Enter Your Desired Prompt

Type in the prompt that describes what you want to generate.

### 5. Configure Generation Parameters

### 6. Download and Share Your Creation

Once the video is generated, download it and share your work with others.
