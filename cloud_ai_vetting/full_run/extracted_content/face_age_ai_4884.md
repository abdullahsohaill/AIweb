## How to Improve Your Face Age AI Results

Follow these easy steps to enhance the accuracy of your face age analysis.
Make sure your face is evenly lit to prevent shadows. Natural light works best, so try to face towards the source of light.
Position yourself so you’re looking straight at the camera with a relaxed face. This helps the AI accurately evaluate your features.
Choose a plain background to keep the focus on your face. This ensures your features stand out clearly for the AI.
Keep your hair away from your face and take off any glasses or hats. This prevents anything from skewing the analysis.
