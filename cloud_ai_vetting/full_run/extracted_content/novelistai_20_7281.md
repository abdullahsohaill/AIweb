Quick answers to help you get started with NovelistAI
NovelistAI is a cutting-edge platform that harnesses artificial intelligence to generate completely original novels, stories, and interactive books. Whether you're an aspiring writer seeking inspiration, a reader exploring AI-generated literature, or a creator looking to produce audiobooks and illustrated content, NovelistAI offers a revolutionary storytelling experience across multiple formats and languages.
Creating an account is simple and free. Click 'Sign Up' and provide your email address and password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account. Once verified, you can start exploring our library and try creating your first book with our free credits.
Navigate to the 'Write a Book' section and follow the guided steps. Choose your format (Novel, Tale, Non-Fiction, Gamebook, Poetry, or Script), select an AI model, language, and age rating. Define your story concept and customize additional settings as needed. The AI will then generate your book's title and introduction.
Click the 'Images' button to open the image generation panel. You can use auto-generated prompts or write your own, then select from AI models like SEEDREAM, IDEOGRAM, RECRAFT-3, or FLUX based on your artistic needs. For book covers, you can enable the 'Include Title' option. Generate multiple options and choose your favorite to apply to your book.
After generating a page, click the 'Generate Audio' button (headphones icon). Select a voice technology (OpenAI TTS, XTTS, or MINIMAX), then choose from available narrators or create your own by uploading a voice sample. Once selected, audio will be generated for your page. You can also export all narrated pages as a single audiobook file.
Our platform uses 'credits' as currency for content generation. Credit costs vary based on the AI models you select:
- Text Generation: 1-30 credits per page depending on model
- Image Creation: 1-10 credits per image depending on model
- Audio Narration: 1-5 credits per page depending on voice type
You can always see the exact credit cost before generating any content.
