### What is the difference between text-to-video and image-to-video with Seedance?

Text-to-video generates videos from detailed text descriptions, while image-to-video transforms static images into dynamic video sequences. Both methods use Seedance's advanced AI to create professional-quality content with cinematic camera movements and precise control.

### What video resolutions and durations are supported?

Seedance supports flexible video generation with 480p, 720p, and 1080p resolution options, multiple aspect ratios (1:1, 4:3, 16:9, 21:9), and duration settings of 5-10 seconds to match your content needs.

### How many credits does video generation cost?

Higher resolution and longer duration provide better quality but consume more credits.

### How does Seedance's multi-shot storytelling work?

Seedance excels in creating complex narrative sequences with consistent character representation and seamless transitions across multiple shots. This makes it perfect for cinematic storytelling and professional content creation.

### What camera movements and effects are available?

Seedance supports a wide range of professional camera techniques including pans, zooms, tracking shots, aerial views, handheld styles, and cinematic movements that add professional polish to your videos.

### Why might my video generation fail?

Video generation may fail if your prompt contains content that violates our content policy. Seedance has content moderation to prevent inappropriate content creation. Failed video generations don't consume any credits! Try adjusting your prompt or uploading different images and try again.

### How can I delete my generated videos?

You can easily delete your generated videos by visiting the My Creations page (https://seedanceai.net/dashboard/user/my-creations) in your dashboard. Simply click the delete button below any video you wish to remove. Please note that deleted videos cannot be recovered, so exercise caution when deleting content.

### Can I use generated videos for commercial purposes?

Yes, all videos generated through our Seedance platform can be used for commercial purposes. The professional-quality output is suitable for marketing, advertising, education, entertainment, and other business applications.
