## Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about SendX and our services

### Can I send unlimited emails?

Yes, absolutely. All our paid plans come with unlimited email sends. We base our pricing on the number of subscribers you have, not how many emails you send. This gives you the freedom to engage with your audience as often as you need to without worrying about hitting a limit or incurring extra charges.

### How does SendX pricing work?

Our pricing is simple and based on your number of subscribers. All our plans include access to every feature, unlimited email sends, and our 24/7 support. We believe in transparency, so there are no hidden fees or separate charges for core functionalities.

### Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we do! You can sign up for a 14-day free trial to experience SendX with full access to all our features. No credit card is required to get started, so you can explore everything we have to offer, risk-free.

### Can I import my existing email lists into SendX?

Yes, importing your contacts is easy. You can upload a CSV file with your subscriber lists, and our system will securely import them. We also help ensure a smooth transition by guiding you through the process of warming up your sending domain to maintain high deliverability.

### How easy is it to switch to SendX from another service?

Switching to SendX is a seamless process. You can easily import your existing contacts via CSV, and our dedicated 24/7 support team is always available to assist you with the migration. We provide personalized guidance on warming up your account to ensure you maintain high deliverability right from the start.

### What kind of customer support can I expect?

We pride ourselves on offering exceptional customer support. Every SendX user, regardless of their plan size, gets access to our 24/7 support team via email and live chat. Our mission is to help you succeed, and we're always here to answer your questions and provide assistance whenever you need it.

### What happens if I go over my subscriber limit?

Your service will continue without any interruption. If your subscriber count exceeds your current plan's limit, your account will be automatically and seamlessly upgraded to the next pricing tier. This ensures you can keep growing your list and sending campaigns without any downtime or manual intervention.
