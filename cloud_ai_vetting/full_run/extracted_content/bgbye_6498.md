## BGBYE

AI-powered background removal for images and videos

### Upload Files

Click to upload or drag & drop
Images: JPG, PNG, WebP | Videos: MP4, WebM, MOV
Multiple images or single video supported

### How to Use

### For Images

Upload multiple images to batch process with different AI models. Compare results and download individually or as a zip file.

### For Videos

Upload a single video to extract frames and process each frame. Preview results and export as image sequence.
