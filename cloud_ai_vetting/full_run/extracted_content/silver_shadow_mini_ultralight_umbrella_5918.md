6.8 oz - 193 g Trekking Sun Umbrella
Walking across deserts in the blazing sun or through summer rain showers shouldn't be a misery.
The Silver Shadow Series provides a large reflective surface on top to deflect sunlight and a black underside to absorb wandering rays, allowing you to walk cooler and better protected.  Its coating sheds water easily, eliminating the need to wear sweaty rain gear.
Overview
The Silver Shadow Mini is the collapsible, compact umbrella in our line. Packing to 40% the length of our other umbrellas, it is ideal for travel or when space is paramount. Wherever you travel, a Silver Shadow should be part of your kit! Mini Replacement Handle: Six Moon Designs now comes with the Mini Replacement Handle for free with your Umbrella.  The Replacement handle gives you a more comfortable EVA foam grip to hold onto for longer hikes.  As an added bonus the new handle makes this umbrella work much better with the Hands Free Kit .   If you do not want the added bulk of the EVA replacement handle you can keep on the smaller plastic handle.
