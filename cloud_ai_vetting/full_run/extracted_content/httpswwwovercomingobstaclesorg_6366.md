## Life Skills Lessons, Activities, and More for Free

Overcoming Obstacles provides educators around the world with the tools to teach their students how to communicate effectively, achieve meaningful goals, make good decisions, and more.  Download our entire K-12 Life Skills Curriculum for free in our online digital library—no registration required.

## What is Overcoming Obstacles?

Overcoming Obstacles is a free, award-winning K-12 life skills curriculum published by the nonprofit Community for Education Foundation. Since 1992, over 800,000 educators from around the world have taught the curriculum to more than 180 million students.
