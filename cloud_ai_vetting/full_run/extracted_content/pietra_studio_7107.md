## Frequently asked questions

### What is Pietra?

Pietra is an AI Commerce OS that helps small businesses and e-commerce brands automate product sourcing, supply chain operations, fulfillment, and marketing. It connects to your Shopify, Amazon, TikTok, Meta, and Google data so you can run your business faster and at lower cost.

### How does Pietra AI work?

Pietra uses multiple AI models, selecting the best one for each task—sourcing outreach, inventory planning, content creation, marketing strategy, fulfillment optimization, and more. Because Pietra connects to all your commerce data, the AI gets smarter the more you use it, offering personalized actions, not generic advice.

### How is Pietra different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?

Most AI tools only chat. Pietra takes action across your business, like: Contacts suppliers and manages sourcing; Optimizes supply chain and fulfillment workflows; Builds and deploys marketing campaigns; Forecasts inventory and sales; Automates repetitive tasks. Pietra blends chat, agents, software tools, and automations into one AI system built specifically for e-commerce operations.

### Can Pietra help me find manufacturers and source products?

Yes. Pietra gives you access to 50,000+ global factories and uses AI to counter-source products, request quotes, compare MOQs and pricing, and manage supplier communication. It’s the fastest way for small brands to find high-quality manufacturers and reduce sourcing costs.

### Does Pietra help with supply chain and fulfillment?

Absolutely. Pietra connects sourcing, POs, inventory, shipping, and fulfillment into one workflow. Our U.S. fulfillment network offers fast, affordable 3PL services with real-time tracking, optimized shipping rates, and automated inventory forecasting powered by AI.

### How does Pietra support marketing and content creation?

Pietra’s AI creates ads, social content, emails, SEO blog posts, and full marketing plans across Meta, TikTok, Google, YouTube, and Shopify. It can also source influencers, analyze performance, and run automations that generate content daily.

### Is Pietra good for both new and established businesses?

Yes. New founders get guided setup, sourcing help, and ready-to-use marketing tools. Growing brands get advanced automation, deeper integrations, and lower costs across sourcing, supply chain, and fulfillment. Agencies and larger teams can use Pietra to orchestrate multi-brand operations.

### What can I actually do inside Pietra?

You can: Chat with AI for ideas, strategy, and content; Deploy agents to contact suppliers or generate campaigns; Use software tools for sourcing, fulfillment, inventory, and marketing; Set automations that run daily, weekly, or on triggers; And much, much more! Everything works together as one AI-powered commerce system.
