## How our copilot helps you work smarter

Save time and boost ad performance with AI.
Copilot creates tailored job ad suggestions based on your job title, requirements, and your own past ads. It doesn’t just generate text—it learns from your data and combines it with proven best practices.
The result?
- Ads that inspire, stand out, and attract more qualified candidates - without you having to start from scratch.
- All suggestions are designed to save recruiters valuable time while improving both efficiency and hiring quality.
Prepare structured, fair interviews with ease.
Copilot generates competence-based interview questions directly linked to your job ad and requirements. This ensures that the questions you ask are always relevant and role-specific.
The result?
- Whether you’re interviewing outside of your expertise area or want to ensure fairness across the board, Copilot makes it easy to create consistent, structured interviews that improve hiring quality and reduce bias.
Extend your reach beyond job boards.
Copilot helps you design social media ads that speak to the right candidates. Suggestions are based on the role and job category, enriched with Talentech’s unique recruitment data.
The result?
- This means you get compelling, ready-to-use campaign content that attracts both active and passive candidates—helping you reach talent you might otherwise miss.
Let data, not guesswork, guide your recruitment.
With Copilot, every publishing decision is backed by insights from millions of applications processed through Talentech each year. You’ll know exactly which channels deliver results for roles like yours.
The result?
- Instead of wasting budget on trial and error, Copilot provides top suggestions backed by real recruitment data. That gives you confidence that your ads will reach the right candidates, at the right time, on the right platforms.
Free up HR time and reduce confusion.
Copilot Chat makes HR knowledge available to everyone in your organization. Employees and managers can ask about policies, leave, travel, or benefits - and instantly receive accurate answers sourced from company-approved documents.
The result?
- This reduces repetitive questions for HR, ensures consistent information across the business, and helps people make faster, better-informed decisions.
Focus on the best candidates faster.
Copilot automatically analyzes CVs and applications against job requirements, providing each candidate with a clear score and explanation of fit.
The result?
- This gives recruiters actionable insights on who to prioritize - saving hours of manual screening and helping ensure that no strong candidate is overlooked.
Rediscover great candidates for new roles.
Copilot analyzes your existing talent pool and identifies candidates who are a strong match for your new job ads. Past runner-ups or silver-medal candidates often turn out to be the perfect fit for a new role.
The result?
- By re-engaging talent you already know, you can shorten time-to-hire, reduce costs, and build stronger long-term relationships with candidates.
Ensure quality without the chase.
Copilot makes reference checking simple and efficient. Instead of chasing down busy referees, you can run a smooth, structured process that provides you with reliable insights faster.
The result?
- This not only saves time but also ensures a higher level of consistency and quality assurance across every hire.
