## Powerful Insights at a Glance

Our intuitive interface provides detailed analytics about student work patterns

### Comprehensive Writing Analytics

The summary bar provides at-a-glance metrics about the document's creation process, including:
- Total editing time spent on Doc/Slide
- Number of editing sessions over multiple days
- Word count and character statistics
- Percentage of content created through typing vs. pasting
These metrics help you quickly assess whether a student has put appropriate time and effort into their assignment.

### Detect Unusual Writing Patterns

Our intelligent analysis flags potential concerns in the writing process, helping you identify:
- Sudden large additions of text that may indicate AI use or plagiarism
- Inconsistent writing speeds across different sections
- Unusual editing patterns that don't match typical student behavior
- Sections with minimal revision that may have been generated externally
These insights help you have meaningful conversations with students about their writing process and academic integrity.

### Track Copy/Paste Sessions

See exactly when and how content was added to the document:
- Identify all copy/paste events with timestamps
- View the size and location of pasted content
- Distinguish between content pasted from external sources vs. within the document
- Analyze patterns of pasting behavior across the writing process
This detailed breakdown helps you understand how students are incorporating research and external content into their work.

## Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Revision History

### How does Revision History handle privacy and data security?

We take privacy seriously. Revision History only accesses document data when you explicitly use the extension. All data is processed securely, and we never store the content of your documents on our servers.

### Is Revision History an AI detector?

While we don't claim to be an AI detector, we do flag "unusual writing patterns" and provide a visual timeline of how the document was created. This gives teachers concrete information they can use to make their own informed assessments. Our tool should be used as a starting point for conversations with students, not as explicit evidence of any kind of behavior.

### Does Revision History integrate with Google Classroom?

Yes! Revision History works seamlessly with Google Classroom. You can use it with any document or presentation that students submit or that you create within the Google ecosystem.

### What's the difference between free and Plus subscriptions?

The free version allows analysis of a limited number of documents monthly, while Plus provides unlimited document analysis. Both versions process all document content locally - we never collect document content. For schools, we offer institutional licensing with volume discounts.

### Does Google Slides analysis count towards my free tier limit?

No! Google Slides analysis is completely free and does not count towards your free tier document analysis limit. You can analyze unlimited Google Slides presentations regardless of your subscription tier.
