Riff is for building tools for work – apps, automations and agents that increase your productivity, remove busywork, and drive business growth.

Examples include sales dashboards, finance calculators, content calendars, marketing video generators, and AI advisors.
Riff is for doers who want to solve a big problem or seize a big opportunity at work, without waiting for development resources.
Riff has native integrations to several systems, like HubSpot, Notion, Stripe, Gmail, Google Calendar, and more. You can connect your Riff app to those systems with one-click and sign in. This lets you build apps that leverage data from those systems and push data or make changes in those systems (e.g. pull deal data from HubSpot, send emails in Gmail, or update calendar invites in Google Calendar). Connecting using native integrations does not cost credits.
Riff can build integrations to any system by researching its APIs and requesting API keys from you. These integrations will be written in code and use credits.
Your app is deployed to the same infrastructure as we use for Riff.  We provide a 1-click deploy experience to [username].riff.works/[appname]. We also support deployment to custom domains.
