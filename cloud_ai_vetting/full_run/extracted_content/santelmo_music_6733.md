### What are “stems”?

Stems are individual tracks for each instrument and vocal part. For example, you would have separate tracks for the kick, snare, bass, piano, lead vocal, background vocal, etc.

### What is Music Editing and Manual Vocal Editing?

Music Editing involves replacing samples (kick, snare, bass, hats, etc.) to enhance the overall sound quality, and time-aligning instruments like guitars and drums. Manual Vocal Editing uses a combination of Melodyne and Autotune for precise pitch correction, resulting in perfect-sounding vocals. results of your work may vary on how good the samples are so be aware of that!
