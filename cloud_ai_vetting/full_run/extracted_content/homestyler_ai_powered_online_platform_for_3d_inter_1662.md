## How to Create Floor Plans in 3 Simple Steps

Start by uploading an image or a floor plan in DWG format, entering measurements manually, or using the Scan Room feature. With a single click, Homestyler converts it into a 3D floor plan, complete with room walls and windows.
Effortlessly add furniture through a drag-and-drop process to decorate your rooms in 3D using our catalog of over 1,000,000 models, including windows, kitchen islands, carpets, and more, or upload your own models.
