## CCAligned: A Massive Collection of Cross-lingual Web-Document Pairs

Domain \tab Source_URL \tab Source_Content \tab Target_URL \tab Target_Content
To obtain aligned documents from non-English to non-English languages, one
should simply join two English-aligned document pairs on the English
(source_url).
For example, if you have two URLs indicating a pair from English to Arabic
(english.test.com and arabic.test.com) as well as two URLs indicating a pair
from English to French (english.test.com and french.test.com), you can join on
the English URL to create (arabic.test.com and french.test.com).
