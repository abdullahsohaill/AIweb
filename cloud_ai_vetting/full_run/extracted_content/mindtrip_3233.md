## How it Works

### Start chatting with us.

Ask us for suggestions for any destination or ask us for an entire itinerary. Be as specific as you can about the types of experiences that you like or take our quiz to determine your travel style.

### Popular itineraries.

Visit our Inspiration page to get ideas and inspiration from other Mindtrippers. Add their suggestions to a new trip plan and customize it to make it your own.

### Get personalized recommendations.

We’ll provide personalized, actionable travel experiences based on your preferences. Check out photos, reviews, maps and more. Favorite the items you like and add them to your trip plan.

### Collab with friends and family.

Coordinating a trip with your crew has never been this fun and easy. Just invite them to check out your itinerary or dive right into the excitement of planning together.

### Upload and organize all your travel receipts.

Get started by uploading a receipt or confirmation to Mindtrip or forwarding it to receipts@mindtrip.ai . Add new ones as you book and access everything in one place while you travel.
