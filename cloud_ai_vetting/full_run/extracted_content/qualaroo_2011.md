## Advanced Targeting for Actionable User Insights

- Domains: Target surveys to specific domains.
- Subdomains: Target survey to subdomains or site sections.
- Regex matching: Utilize regex to survey on exact page matches.
- Identities: Survey specific users based on any crateria such as plan, history etc.
- All or a portion of audience: Target specific audience segments.
- Geography: Focus on users from a regions for location-specific insights.
- And More! Expand your targeting criteria for a broader feedback spectrum.
- Duration on page: Survey users based on the time they spend on a page for engagement insights.
- Exit intent: Capture feedback from exiting users to retain and understand their intent.
- Scroll location: Analyze and survey users based on their scroll depth to gauge content engagement.
- Display once per visitor: Survey users uniquely on their first visit for fresh perspectives.
- Until response is received: Persist with surveys until users provide their feedback.
- Recurring questions: Engage users with periodic surveys to track evolving opinions.
- Custom date range: Set a specific timeframe for your surveys, aligning with campaigns or events.
- Target number of responses: Aim for a set number of feedback entries to meet your research goals.
- Manual activation and deactivation: Toggle your surveys on or off, anytime, as needed.
