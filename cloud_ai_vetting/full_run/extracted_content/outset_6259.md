### A simple workflow, designed for enterprise researchers

Effortlessly set-up your research guide on Outset, either by bringing your own or with the help of our AI. Add qualitative and quantitative questions, set advanced probing guidelines, and set intelligent skipping logic.
Recruit hundreds of participants via Outset from any geography, without worry of timezone difficulty or language barrier. Have your own list? You can bring your own or send directly to a list directly from Outset.
Conduct hundreds of AI-moderated interviews at once, in any language, via video, voice, or text with modern multimodal research. Our AI interviewer engages participants in rich dialogue and dynamically probes deeper to get the "why" behind their answers.
Instantly generate summaries, themes, powerful quotes, and customizable highlight reels from every qualitative interview. No more manual transcript tagging or sifting through videos for powerful quotes, AI-powered interview synthesis handles it.
Easily turn your analysis into custom AI-generated reports, highlight reels, and PowerPoint decks that are easy to share out to stakeholders, enabling research to be a core part of the development lifecycle and driving force behind company strategy.
