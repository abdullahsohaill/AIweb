## How it works

## Add your data

Link a live Google Sheet , upload a CSV or Excel file, paste or type values, call our API, or ask AI to search for data.
No need to prepare or format, Graphy ingests it all.

## Let AI work its magic

Our data engine reads through your dataset and highlights the insights that matter most. You’ll always see how it got there, so you can trust every step.

## Chat with your data

Get the right chart, insight, or answer in seconds.

## Make your data shine

Instantly brand all your charts with your colors and fonts. Then, use annotations to call out the big wins.
