## AI-native Revenue Orchestration Platform

## Extract, structure, and move GTM data—automatically

Momentum is a structured data provider that tracks what's said in every customer interaction and turns it into structured, usable data. Your CRM fields are updated in real time—text, dropdowns, anything. That means cleaner pipeline, better reporting, and smarter AI agents that actually have context.
- No more blindspots. No more surprises.

## Right message to the right person at the right time

Momentum turns Slack (and soon Microsoft Teams!) into your command center. Deal rooms spin up automatically. Key moments, risks, and updates are routed to the right people—instantly. Sales. CS. Leadership. Everyone gets what they need without asking.
- No more tagging. No more chasing. No more gaps.

## Automate processes and move faster—with zero manual effort

Momentum runs your GTM workflows in the background. From nudging reps to follow up, to moving stages based on exit criteria—your process stays tight, consistent, and fast. You define the rules. Momentum makes sure they happen.
- No more guesswork. No more manual clean-up.
