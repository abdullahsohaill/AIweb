## Overview

Turn any page or flow from your product into a prototype you can edit with Reforge Build.
Reforge Build is AI prototyping for product teams.  Start from your product, not from scratch.  Prototype with AI that understands your customers, design, features, and strategy.

With the Reforge extension, you can:

1. Open any page of your product.
2. Click Capture Page with the Reforge extension.
3. Reforge Build automatically rebuilds it as a working prototype.

Reforge Build enables you to:

- Generate prototypes that start from your product
- Explore multiple variants and built-in collaboration
- Collaborate and share with your team.

The Reforge extension also brings expert-backed guidance into the tools you use every day — like Google Docs, Notion, and Jira — coaching you as you write and plan product documents without ever leaving your workflow.
