## Supernormal makes every meeting count

Supernormal streamlines your meeting notes, prep, and insights—turning meetings into moments of productivity and genuine connection.

## Focus on the discussion. Let Supernormal handle the rest.

Supernormal automatically transcribes and summarizes the meetings on your calendar, and identifies the next step tasks that come out of them.
The Norma bot joins your meeting to work as part of your team. Or download the Chrome extension for Google Meet meetings without Norma.

### Cross-platform

Supernormal works with Google Meet, Zoom, and Microsoft Teams.

### Scheduling

Rest easy knowing Supernormal appears only when you expect it.

### Custom name

Personalize the display name of the notetaker.

### Multi-language

Over 60 languages supported for all meeting platforms.

## Connect your tools and your team. Turn meetings into momentum.

Share your meeting notes and summaries, and even complete videos and transcripts with your colleagues or clients. Track identified tasks automatically with assignments and integrations with other common tools.
