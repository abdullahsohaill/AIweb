## Craft a personalized, mobile-first experience

### Engage and qualify

Create an engaging user experience that compels clicks and qualifies leads.
Set up conditions or advanced navigation rules to guide users based on their choices and data. From personalized funnels to interactive quizzes, you can deliver tailored experiences that keep visitors engaged and converting.
Use variables to capture inputs and reuse them throughout the funnel. Greet users by name, reference their choices, and tailor the journey in real time. Personalization keeps every interaction relevant and engaging.
