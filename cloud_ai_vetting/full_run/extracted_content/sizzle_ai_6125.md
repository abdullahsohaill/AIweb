# Sizzle AI
**URL:** https://web.szl.ai
**Page Title:** Sizzle - Learn anything
--------------------

Sizzle breaks any topic down into the key skills you need to understand it.
Meet Julia — and see how she uses Sizzle.
Julia's a sophomore in high school with a test on quadratic equations tomorrow.
She's confused. Time's running out. She thinks, "Why is this always so hard?"
Then she remembers Sizzle . She opens the app and uploads her study guide.
Getting started is simple
Upload a study guide, lecture slides, or just type a topic. Sizzle works with whatever you've got.
Sizzle breaks it down — identifying the core concepts and organizing them into the skills she needs to master.
Behind the scenes, Sizzle breaks it down
Powered by learning science, Sizzle analyzes your materials and breaks them into Knowledge Components (KCs)—the smallest units of understanding.
She starts practicing each skill. With every exercise, the fog begins to lift.
Learning gets smarter as you go
With every bite-sized exercise, Sizzle adapts to your learning—tracking what you've mastered, where you're stuck, and what to show next.
Finally, it clicks. She smiles to herself.
"Maybe I really can do this."
