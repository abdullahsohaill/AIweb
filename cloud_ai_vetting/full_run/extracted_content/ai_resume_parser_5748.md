## How Adaface is different

Traditional pre-employment tests focus on theoretical/ trick-based questions, which leads to inaccurate shortlisting.
At Adaface, our mission is to help companies find great candidates by assessing on-the-job skills required for a role.
We do this by creating high quality scenario based questions, which are designed to test the fundamental concepts needed to do the job well.
