### What is Shoutout.io?

Shoutout.io is the easiest way to collect, manage, and showcase testimonials. Whether you're a solopreneur, SaaS founder, agency, or coach, Shoutout helps you turn happy customers into your most powerful marketing asset, all in minutes, not weeks.

### Why do testimonials matter?

Testimonials build trust, reduce buying hesitation, and drive conversions. Social proof is one of the most powerful forces in marketing, and customers trust customers more than ads. With Shoutout, you can automatically gather testimonials and display them where they matter most: landing pages, sales pages, checkout flows, and more.

### Will Shoutout increase my sales or leads?

That's exactly what it's designed to do. Social proof is one of the most powerful conversion drivers, and testimonials help build trust with new visitors fast. By placing real customer praise on your landing pages, pricing pages, and checkouts, you reduce hesitation and boost credibility; leading to more signups, more purchases, and more leads. Users regularly tell us that adding Shoutout to their site led to immediate increases in conversion.

### How is Shoutout different from other testimonial tools?

We're not just a collection tool, we're an all-in-one testimonial engine. With Shoutout, you can collect video or text testimonials via a branded request form, import from other platforms and organize submissions, showcase testimonials with beautiful widgets on your site -- all for a one-time payment instead of a monthly subscription.

### Who is Shoutout for?

Shoutout is for anyone who sells online. That includes SaaS founders, course creators, coaches, freelancers, agencies, eCom brands, and more. If your customers love you, Shoutout helps you turn that love into revenue.

### How do I collect testimonials with Shoutout?

You can share your unique testimonial request link with customers or embed the form directly on your website. You can also use our one-click import tools to bring in praise from Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Product Hunt and tons of other platforms.

### Can I customize the design to match my brand?

Yes! You can fully customize your testimonial collection forms and display widgets — colors, fonts, layout, logos, buttons, and more — to make it feel completely on-brand.

Absolutely. You can remove all Shoutout branding with a one-time upgrade of $100. No monthly fee, no ongoing upsell — just a one-time charge to make it truly your own.

No limits. You can collect unlimited testimonials, create unlimited widgets, and use Shoutout on as many websites as you want (up to 3 with the standard license).

### Does it work with my website or tech stack?

Shoutout works with every website -- no matter what you use. Simply copy and paste one short embed snippet and you're done!

Yes, everything is hosted by us. No need to worry about servers, plugins, or security updates. We handle it all for you. Just collect and showcase praise and grow your sales and leads.
