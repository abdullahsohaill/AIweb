## Voice for All Project

At CoeFont, we are committed to promoting inclusivity and accessibility. Since 2021, our Voice for All project has provided free AI Voice services to individuals at risk of losing their voices.
In 2023, we expanded our coverage to include those with speech disabilities. Since then, over 500 people worldwide have signed up and joined us in making strides toward a more inclusive digital landscape.
