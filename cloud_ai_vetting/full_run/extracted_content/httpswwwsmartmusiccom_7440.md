## Sight Reading Studio

Tackle sight reading challenges with Sight Reading Studio.
- Unlimited, customizable exercises (adjust pitch, rhythm, key, and more)
- Immediate guidance and feedback for learners
- A deeper understanding of student progress
- An easier way to challenge every student appropriately

## Dynamic Accompaniments

Say goodbye to silent, uninspiring practice! MakeMusic Cloud offers professional-quality accompaniments for every piece in our library. Professional musicians guide practice, making it more engaging and effective. Students play along with full orchestral, band, or piano accompaniments, developing their sense of ensemble, rhythm, and intonation.

## Effortless Grading

We know your time is precious. Our intuitive grading tools provide instant insights into student progress and performance. Students can record their assignments directly in the platform, and MakeMusic Cloud automatically assesses pitch and rhythm. Grades can be automatically synced to Google Classroom gradebook as well.

## The Foundations Series by Brian Balmages

Establishing a solid foundation is crucial for every student. The Foundations Series offers pedagogically-sound technique and fundamental exercises designed to supplement any curriculum, build strong musical literacy from day one, and combine essential skills with engaging soundtrack accompaniments.
