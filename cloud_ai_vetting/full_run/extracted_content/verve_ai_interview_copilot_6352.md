## Got questions about Verve AI?

### What is Verve AI Interview Copilot?

Verve AI Interview Copilot gives you real-time support during live interviews—suggesting answers, structuring responses, and offering tips as you speak.

### Is Verve AI visible to the interviewer during screen sharing?

No, Verve AI is not visible to the interviewer during screen sharing. If you're sharing a browser tab, it stays fully discreet. For extra privacy, our desktop app includes Stealth Mode, which keeps Verve AI completely hidden and visible only to you—even during full-screen or app-based screen sharing.

### What types of interviews does Verve AI support?

Verve AI Interview Copilot supports all interviews, including behavioral, technical, live coding, case studies, and online assessments, with tailored guidance for every scenario.

### What types of meeting softwares does Verve AI support?

Verve AI seamlessly integrates with all meeting platforms,  including (but not limited to) Google Meet, Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Amazon Chime — on both browser and desktop app versions.

### Is Verve AI free to use?

We offer a free plan so you can experience the benefits before committing to a paid subscription.

### Does it work for coding or technical interviews?

Yes! With our plugin, Verve AI can instantly capture and solve coding questions directly from your screen—perfect for technical interviews and online assessments.

### Is Verve AI suitable for all industries?

Yes! Whether you’re interviewing for tech, finance, healthcare, marketing, or any other field, Verve AI trains specialized Copilots using expert materials tailored to each industry.
