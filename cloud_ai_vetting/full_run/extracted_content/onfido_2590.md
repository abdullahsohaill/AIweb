## IDV Products for Customers

### Workflow Studio

Orchestrate verification journeys that combine document and biometric verification, trusted data sources, and passive fraud detection signals to build the right level of trust in your customer at the right moment.

### Entrust IDV + Mastercard Identity

Deliver a seamless and secure onboarding experience. For more intelligent fraud defense, Entrust Workflow Studio leverages Mastercard Identity risk signals.

### Smart Capture

Delight users with a fast, efficient capture and upload experience. Our SDKs, powered by smart capture technology, are easy-to-integrate and make it simple to deliver exceptional identity verification.

### Data Verification

Navigate the complexities of global regulatory requirements by validating customer identities with trusted data sources. Meet KYC, AML, sanctions and PEPs screening requirements by comparing user data against global and local databases.

### Biometric Verification

Protect your business from stolen IDs and impersonation fraud with facial biometric verification that doesn’t compromise on experience. Our award-winning AI technology returns fair, fast and accurate results in seconds.

### IDV Compliance

Meet complex local regulatory needs and remotely onboard customers globally with an off-the-shelf compliance solution.

### Document Verification

Create confidence in a customer’s identity by verifying a photo ID. Powered by AI, our document verification delivers actionable results that are fair, fast, and accurate. We support over 2,500 document types in 195 countries.

### Fraud Detection

Fraud is a moving target. You need fraud prevention that adapts to your needs. Create a 360-degree view of customer identity with our fraud detection signals.

### AAMVA Check

By assessing trusted and reliable data from the American Association of Motor Vehicle Administrators (AAMVA), Onfido matches identity data against local Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) records to detect synthetic, duplicate, or false identities.
